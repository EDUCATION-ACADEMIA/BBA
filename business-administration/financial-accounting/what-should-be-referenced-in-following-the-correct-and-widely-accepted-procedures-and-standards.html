What should be referenced in following the correct and widely accepted procedures and standards?

(A) FHA

(B) FOIA

(C) ** GAAP

(D) IRS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Are all companies required to follow GAAP? Not all companies need to follow GAAP. Only regulated and publicly traded businesses must adhere to GAAP.

Concept note-2: -The correct option is B. Accrual basis of accounting is in accordance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP).

Concept note-3: -The specifications of GAAP, which is the standard adopted by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), include definitions of concepts and principles, as well as industry-specific rules. The purpose of GAAP is to ensure that financial reporting is transparent and consistent from one organization to another.

Concept note-4: -GAAP covers such topics as revenue recognition, balance sheet classification, and materiality.
