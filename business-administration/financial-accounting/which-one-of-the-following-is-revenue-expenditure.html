Which one of the following is revenue expenditure?

(A) Painting of a new building.

(B) Factory machine installation costs.

(C) ** Annual depreciation of factory machinery.

(D) Extension costs for office building.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Revenue expenditure are costs spent on fixed assets after they have been place in service. Depreciation charges, factory insurance premium, production royalty paid are all examples of revenue expenditure.

Concept note-2: -Since the asset is part of normal business operations, depreciation is considered as revenue expense.

Concept note-3: -Routine repairs of plant and machinery are regarded as revenue expenditure and they are charged to the Repairs and maintenance expense account.

Concept note-4: -Depreciation of fixed assets is considered as a revenue expenditure since the asset is used for the daily operations of the business.
