A transaction caused a $15, 000 decrease in both total assets and total liabilities. This transaction could have been ____

(A) The purchase of a truck for $15, 000 cash

(B) An asset with a cost of $15, 000 was destroyed by fire

(C) ** Repayment of a $15, 000 bank loan

(D) Collection of $15, 000 in accounts receivable

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(ii) Decrease in Liability, decrease in Asset: Transaction of payment to a creditor decreases liability (creditor) and also reduces asset (cash or bank).or Loan from bank repaid decreases the asset (Cash/Bank) and decrease the liability (Loan from Bank) simultaneously.

Concept note-2: -The payment of a liability decreases assets and liabilities. Explanation: The payment of a liability decreases assets and liabilities as the liability could be paid only through paying cash or cash equivalents hence it decreases the asset when liability is paid off then it is decreased.

Concept note-3: -3) Redemption of debenture-Debenture (liability) and cash(assets) decreases.

Concept note-4: -The payment for advertising expenses in cash increases the company’s expenses and decreases the cash account. Expense decreases equity. Thus, both assets and equity decrease in this transaction.
