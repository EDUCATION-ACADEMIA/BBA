A ____ journal is a special journal in which payments made in cash are posted.

(A) cash receipts

(B) purchase journal

(C) ** cash disbursements

(D) sales journal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A cash disbursement journal is a record of a companyâ€™s internal accounts that itemizes all financial expenditures made with cash or cash equivalents. A cash disbursement journal is done before payments are posted to the general ledger and is used in creating a general ledger.

Concept note-2: -Examples of special journals are the cash receipts journal, cash disbursements journal, payroll journal, purchases journal, and sales journal.

Concept note-3: -Cash Disbursement Journal is a special journal used to record all payments of cash, also called Cash Payment Journal.

Concept note-4: -4 Check Disbursements Journal 7 This special journal is used to record the disbursements through checks made by the Disbursing Officer in the Accounting Division/Unit. It shall be maintained by fund cluster.

Concept note-5: -Purchasing inventory or office supplies, paying out dividends, or making business loan payments with cash or cash equivalents are examples of disbursements.
