(A) Statement of Cash Flows

(B) Income Statement

(C) Balance Sheet

Concept note-1: -Budgeted financial statements contain the expected financial results, financial position, and cash flows of a business. These budgeted financials include an income statement, balance sheet, and statement of cash flows. These statements are compiled from the annual budgeting model of a business.
