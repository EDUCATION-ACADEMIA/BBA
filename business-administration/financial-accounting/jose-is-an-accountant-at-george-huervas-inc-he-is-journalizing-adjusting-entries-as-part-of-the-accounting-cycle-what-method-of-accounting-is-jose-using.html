Jose is an accountant at George Huervas Inc. He is journalizing adjusting entries as part of the accounting cycle. What method of accounting is Jose using?

(A) ** Accrual basis

(B) Cash basis

(C) Deferral basis

(D) Expense basis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Accrual accounting entries are journal entries that recognize revenues and expenses a company earned or incurred, respectively. Accruals are necessary adjustments that accountants make to their companyâ€™s financial statements before they issue them.

Concept note-2: -Typically, an accountant will record adjustments for accrued revenues through debit and credit journal entries in defined accounting periods. This helps account for accrued revenues accurately and so that the balance sheet remains in balance.

Concept note-3: -The key steps in the eight-step accounting cycle include recording journal entries, posting to the general ledger, calculating trial balances, making adjusting entries, and creating financial statements.

Concept note-4: -An accrual-type adjusting entry is a journal entry recorded at the end of a reporting period that alters the amount of revenues or expenses recorded in the income statement.
