At what level is the bank master data stored in SAP S/4 HANA? Please choose the correct answer

(A) At company code level

(B) At controlling area level

(C) At company level

(D) ** At client level

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the SAP system, bank master data is stored centrally in the bank directory.

Concept note-2: -Bank master data has three distinct characteristics which are: A bank’s country and a unique code called bank key. These two fields help to identify any bank in the SAP system. Address data and control data.

Concept note-3: -The transaction code for creating a Bank Master in SAP is ‘FI01’(individually) and ‘BAUP’(mass).
