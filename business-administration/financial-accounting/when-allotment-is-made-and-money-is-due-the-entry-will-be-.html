When Allotment is made and money is due, the entry will be ____

(A) Debit:Share Capital Credit:Share Allotment Account

(B) ** Debit:Share Allotment Account Credit:Share Capital

(C) Debit:Cash Account Credit:Share Allotment Account

(D) Debit:Share Allotment Account Credit:Cash Account

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In allotment of shares, A/c is debited and A/c is credited, for receipt of allotment amount.

Concept note-2: -Share Application and Allotment A/c is debited while Share Capital A/c is credited for transfer of application money and allotment amount due.

Concept note-3: -Securities premium a/c Is credited with premium amount and Share capital a/c is also credited with the amount due on allotment less premium amount.

Concept note-4: -When allotment money is received, the following entry is passed: Bank a/c Dr. 4.
