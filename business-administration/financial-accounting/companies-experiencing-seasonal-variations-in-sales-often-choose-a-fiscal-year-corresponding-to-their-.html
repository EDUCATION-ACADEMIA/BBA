Companies experiencing seasonal variations in sales often choose a fiscal year corresponding to their ____

(A) ** Natural business year

(B) Interim year end

(C) Quarterly year end

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Seasonal businesses must select fiscal years that correspond to their natural business year because seasonal businesses operate on some particular season or a special occasion. Hence, they need to select a fiscal year that corresponds to its natural business year.

Concept note-2: -Companies with little seasonal variation in sales often choose the calendar year as their fiscal year. Facebook, Inc., uses calendar-year reporting. Companies that have seasonal variations in sales often choose a natural business year end, which is when sales activities are at their lowest level for the year.

Concept note-3: -Why do companies base their fiscal year on a natural business cycle? It’s at the low point in the operating cycle and provides time to analyze operations and prepare financial statements.

Concept note-4: -Not necessarily. A fiscal year spans twelve months and corresponds with a company’s financial reporting periods. Sometimes, a fiscal year may differ from a calendar year.
