A computer used to collect, store, and report all the information of a sales transaction.

(A) ** point-of-sale terminal

(B) sales journal

(C) batching out

(D) batch report

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -terminal summary. The report that summarizes the cash and credit card sales of a point-of-sale terminal. A terminal summary is also known as a Z tape.

Concept note-2: -A terminal summary is a report that details the information related to the cash and credit card sales which are maintained by the point of sale terminal. A point of sale terminal is a computer that collects, stores, and reports all the information related to the sales transactions.

Concept note-3: -A terminal summary is also known as a Z tape. True. Separate Transactions are recorded for cash credit card and debit card totals listed on a terminal summary.

Concept note-4: -How does a POS terminal determine the price of an item? number and total of sales by credit card type.
