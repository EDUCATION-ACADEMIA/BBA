WHICH ASSET GENERALLY IS REQUIRED TO BE TESTED AT LEAST ANNUALLY FOR IMPAIRMENT?

(A) MACHINERY

(B) PATENT

(C) ** RENEWABLE BROADCAST LICENSE

(D) COPYRIGHT

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -goodwill, indefinite life intangibles and intangible assets not yet available for use are tested for impairment at least annually, in addition to when there is any indication of impairment, and. all other assets are tested for impairment when there is any indication that the asset may be impaired.

Concept note-2: -The recoverable amount of the following assets in the scope of IAS 36 must be assessed each year: intangible assets with indefinite useful lives; intangible assets not yet available for use; and goodwill acquired in a business combination.
