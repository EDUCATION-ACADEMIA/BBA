Based on the realization principle, if a company sells goods on credit in March and receives payment in May, this would be shown as sales in what month?

(A) ** The month the goods were sold.

(B) The month the goods were produced.

(C) The month the cash is received from the customer.

(D) None of the above.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The realization principle is the concept that revenue can only be recognized once the underlying goods or services associated with the revenue have been delivered or rendered, respectively. Thus, revenue can only be recognized after it has been earned.

Concept note-2: -Essentially, the revenue recognition principle means that companiesâ€™ revenues are recognized when the service or product is considered delivered to the customer-not when the cash is received.

Concept note-3: -When goods are sold on credit, debtors which is an asset account is debited as money is receivable from the customers and sales which is a revenue account is credited.

Concept note-4: -When goods are sold on credit, accounts receivables account is debited and sales account is credited with an equal amount in order to record this transaction.
