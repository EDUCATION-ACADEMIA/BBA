Which is the first step of accounting process?

(A) Classifying

(B) analyzing and interpretation

(C) ** recording

(D) financial statements.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first four steps in the accounting cycle are (1) identify and analyze transactions, (2) record transactions to a journal, (3) post journal information to a ledger, and (4) prepare an unadjusted trial balance.

Concept note-2: -Therefore the first step in the accounting process is recording.

Concept note-3: -Defining the accounting cycle with steps: (1) Financial transactions, (2)Journal entries, (3) Posting to the Ledger, (4) Trial Balance Period, and (5) Reporting Period with Financial Reporting and Auditing.
