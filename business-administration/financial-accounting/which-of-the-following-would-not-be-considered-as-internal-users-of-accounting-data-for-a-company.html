Which of the following would NOT be considered as internal users of accounting data for a company?

(A) ** External auditor

(B) CEO of the company

(C) President of the company

(D) Salesman of the company

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Creditors are the outsiders that lend money to the company. Therefore, creditors would not be considered internal users of accounting data for a company.

Concept note-2: -Examples of internal users are owners, managers, and employees. External users are people outside the business entity (organization) who use accounting information. Examples of external users are suppliers, banks, customers, investors, potential investors, and tax authorities.

Concept note-3: -Creditors are not the internal users.
