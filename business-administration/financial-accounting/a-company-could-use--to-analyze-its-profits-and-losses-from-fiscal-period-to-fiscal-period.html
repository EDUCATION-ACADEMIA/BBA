FINANCIAL ACCOUNTING

[SOURCES]
A company could use ____ to analyze its profits and losses from fiscal period to fiscal period

(A) Bank reconciliations

(B) ** A comparative income statement

(C) A checkbook register

(D) Post-closing trial-balance records

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -However, a Comparative Income Statement or Comparative Statement of Profit & Loss is a horizontal analysis of the Income Statement showing operating results for more than one accounting year. In simple terms, it shows the absolute change and percentage change in the figures from one period to another.

Concept note-2: -An income statement shows a company’s revenues, expenses and profitability over a period of time. It is also sometimes called a profit-and-loss (P&L) statement or an earnings statement.

Concept note-3: -A comparative statement is a document that compares a particular financial statement with prior period statements. Previous financials are presented alongside the latest figures in side-by-side columns, enabling investors to easily track a company’s progress and compare it with peers.
