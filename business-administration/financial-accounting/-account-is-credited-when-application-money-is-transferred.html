____ account is credited when application money is transferred

(A) ** Share Capital

(B) Share Application Account

(C) None of the Above

(D) Any of the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -These two stages of share capital are closely inter-related. Share Application and Allotment A/c is debited while Share Capital A/c is credited for transfer of application money and allotment amount due.

Concept note-2: -On receipt of application money, share application account is credited.

Concept note-3: -This ruling lays down an important principle that share application money constitutes a capital asset under section 2(14) of the ITA.

Concept note-4: -Definition of capital accounts A debit to a capital account means the business doesn’t owe so much to its owners (i.e. reduces the business’s capital), and a credit to a capital account means the business owes more to its owners (i.e. increases the business’s capital).

Concept note-5: -Share Application or share allotment or Share capital A/c all are personal accounts as they represent money from the shareholders and when money is due, these are to be debited because of the rule “Debit the receiver".
