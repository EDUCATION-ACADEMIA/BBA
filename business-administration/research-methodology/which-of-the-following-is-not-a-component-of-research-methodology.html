Which of the following is NOT a component of research methodology?

(A) ** Research proposal

(B) Research Design

(C) Research Instruments

(D) Research Locale

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is option Setting up the institute scientific committee.

Concept note-2: -The research methodology comprises basic components including the design, sampling, tools, collection procedures, analysis, and ethical considerations.

Concept note-3: -A research report consists of a Title page, Abstract, Introduction, Methodology, Results, Discussions, and References. Footnote is an extra piece of information provided at the bottom of the page. Footnote is not that important in the research report.

Concept note-4: -research components, introduction, literature review, method, results, discussion, conclusion.
