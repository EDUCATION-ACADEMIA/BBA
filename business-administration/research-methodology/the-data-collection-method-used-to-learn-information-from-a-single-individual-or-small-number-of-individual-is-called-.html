The data collection method used to learn information from a single individual or small number of individual is called ____

(A) observation

(B) survey

(C) ** interview

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is one of the most trusted, widely used, and familiar qualitative data collection methods primarily because of its approach. An individual or face-to-face interview is a direct conversation between two people with a specific structure and purpose.

Concept note-2: -An interview is a qualitative research method that relies on asking questions in order to collect data. Interviews involve two or more people, one of whom is the interviewer asking the questions. There are several types of interviews, often differentiated by their level of structure.

Concept note-3: -Questionnaires are a simple, straightforward data collection method. Respondents get a series of questions, either open or close-ended, related to the matter at hand.

Concept note-4: -Data may be grouped into four main types based on methods for collection: observational, experimental, simulation, and derived.

Concept note-5: -An interview is generally a qualitative research technique that involves asking open-ended questions to converse with respondents and collect elicit data about a subject.
