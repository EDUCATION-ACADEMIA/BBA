BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

[SOURCES]
It is the process of finding solutions to a problem after a thorough study and analysis of the situational factors

(A) ** Research

(B) Thesis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Research is simply the process of finding solutions to a problem after a thorough study and analysis of the situational factors. Managers in organizations constantly engage themselves in studying and analyzing issues and hence are involved in some form of research activity as they make decisions at the workplace.

Concept note-2: -Broadly defined, problem solving is the process of finding solutions to difficult or complex issues.

Concept note-3: -Finding a solution to a problem involves constructing a course of action that will transform your current situation into one where your objective has been achieved. Some problems require no further analysis once they have been defined effectively.

Concept note-4: -Modern solution methodology offers a set of macro-and micro-practices that help a practitioner systematically maximize the odds of a successful analytics project outcome. Methodology is all about approach. Every discipline, whether it be applied or theoretical in nature, has methodologies.

Concept note-5: -Applied research is a type of research design that seeks to solve a specific problem or provide innovative solutions to issues affecting an individual, group or society.
