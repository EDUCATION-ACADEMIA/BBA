RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

[SOURCES]
Research is ____

(A) An observation to an object which is based on questions

(B) ** An organized and systematic way of finding answers to questions

(C) An implementation of a strategy to see whether it works

(D) A comparison between two events to see the differences between them

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Generally, research is the organised and systematic method of finding answers to questions. It is systematic because it is a process broken up into clear steps that lead to conclusions. Research is organised because there is a planned structure or method used to reach the conclusion.

Concept note-2: -Answer: “Research is an organized and systematic enquiry” Defined by Research methodology. Marshall.

Concept note-3: -a. Research is the systematic and objective analysis and recording of controlled observations that may lead to the development of generalizations, principles, or theories, resulting in prediction and possibly ultimate control of events.

Concept note-4: -Research is a systematic and organized effort to investigate a specific problem that needs a solution.
