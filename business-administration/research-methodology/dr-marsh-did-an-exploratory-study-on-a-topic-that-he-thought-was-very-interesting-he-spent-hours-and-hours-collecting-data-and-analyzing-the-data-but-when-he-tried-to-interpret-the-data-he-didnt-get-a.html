BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

[SOURCES]
Dr. Marsh did an exploratory study on a topic that he thought was very interesting. He spent hours and hours collecting data and analyzing the data, but when he tried to interpret the data he didn’t get anywhere-nothing made sense. Which of the following steps in the research process do you think that Dr. Marsh skipped?

(A) informing others

(B) ** designing the study

(C) background research

(D) selecting a topic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Exploratory studies use consumer and expert interviews, which are quite popular as a primary data collection method. Additionally, focus groups, observation methods, and surveys are used to collect primary data for these studies.

Concept note-2: -There are four main types of Quantitative research: Descriptive, Correlational, Causal-Comparative/Quasi-Experimental, and Experimental Research. attempts to establish cause-effect relationships among the variables. These types of design are very similar to true experiments, but with some key differences.

Concept note-3: -Qualitative research is a process of naturalistic inquiry that seeks an in-depth understanding of social phenomena within their natural setting. It focuses on the “why” rather than the “what” of social phenomena and relies on the direct experiences of human beings as meaning-making agents in their every day lives.

Concept note-4: -Exploratory research is often used when the issue you’re studying is new or when the data collection process is challenging for some reason. You can use exploratory research if you have a general idea or a specific question that you want to study but there is no preexisting knowledge or paradigm with which to study it.
