[SOURCES]
This refers to a particular procedure or set of procedures applied in conducting a research or study.

(B) Review of Related Literature

(C) ** Methodology

(D) Findings, Results and Recommendations

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A scientific procedure means a procedure through which a given task related to the research and reaching the research aim is successively implemented. A scientific procedure is based on certain methodology.

Concept note-2: -Questionnaires are usually paper-and-pencil instruments that the respondent completes. Interviews are completed by the interviewer based on the respondent says.

Concept note-3: -The first step in the research process is to develop a research question. This can be a problem that needs to be solved, or some piece of information that is missing about a particular topic. Answering this question will be the focus of the research study.
