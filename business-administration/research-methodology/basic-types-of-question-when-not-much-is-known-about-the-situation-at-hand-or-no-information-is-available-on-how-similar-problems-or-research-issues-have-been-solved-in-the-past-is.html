Basic types of question when not much is known about the situation at hand, or no information is available on how similar problems or research issues have been solved in the past is?

(A) Descriptive

(B) ** Exploratory

(C) Causal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Exploratory Study Is Undertaken When Not Much Is Known About The Situation At Hand, Or No Information Is Available On How Similar Problems Or Research Issues Have Been Solved In The Past.

Concept note-2: -Exploratory research is a methodology approach that investigates research questions that have not previously been studied in depth. Exploratory research is often qualitative and primary in nature.

Concept note-3: -In analytical research, on the other hand, the researcher has to use facts or information already available, and analyze these to make a critical evaluation of the material.

Concept note-4: -Basic research focuses on the advancement of knowledge, rather than solving a problem. However, applied research directs its efforts toward finding a solution to a specific problem.
