(A) Bias

(B) Integrity

(C) ** Reliability

(D) Validity

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reliability refers to the consistency of a measure.

Concept note-2: -Reliability refers to the consistency of a measure (whether the results can be reproduced under the same conditions). Validity refers to the accuracy of a measure (whether the results really do represent what they are supposed to measure).

Concept note-3: -Reliability is the degree to which an assessment tool produces stable and consistent results.
