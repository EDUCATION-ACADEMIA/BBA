Which of the following research leads to development of new theories or concepts?

(A) Applied research

(B) ** Fundamental research

(C) Action research

(D) Experimental research

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Fundamental research: It is scientific research aims to build or improve the theory.

Concept note-2: -Inductive research, also known as theory-building research, collects data that may help develop a new theory about a process or phenomenon. It examines observations and patterns and offers several hypotheses to explain these patterns.

Concept note-3: -In simple words, basic research (also known as pure research or fundamental research) is conducted with the sole objective of contributing to existing knowledge by gathering information.

Concept note-4: -Basic or pure research is done for the development of theories or principles. It is conducted for the intellectual pleasure of learning. Much of this kind of research has been done in psychology and sociology.

Concept note-5: -It applies the theory or model developed through fundamental research to the actual solution to the problems. Applied research focuses on analyzing and solving social problems and tackles practical problems.
