Which statement reflects the data in the graph?

(A) 70% of males in the 80-84 age group have a disability.

(B) ** The percentage of people with disabilities is greater in higher age groups.

(C) The percentage of females with a disability is higher than that of males in the 65-69 age group.

(D) The percentage of males and females with a disability in the 15-24 age group is greater than 20%.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Up to 1 in 4 (26 percent) adults in the United States have some type of disability.

Concept note-2: -In 2021, across both England and Wales, the proportion of disabled people was 17.8% (10.4 million). The proportion of people that are disabled has decreased 1.7 percentage points from 2011, when it was 19.5% (10.0 million).

Concept note-3: -Among students receiving special education services, the most common category of disability was specific learning disabilities (33 percent).

Concept note-4: -An estimated 1.3 billion people – or 16% of the global population – experience a significant disability today.
