A ‘consent’ form is ____

(A) a special concert ticket.

(B) ** an official permission.

(C) researcher’s guidelines.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A document with important information about a medical procedure or treatment, a clinical trial, or genetic testing. It also includes information on possible risks and benefits. If a person chooses to take part in the treatment, procedure, trial, or testing, he or she signs the form to give official consent.

Concept note-2: -A consent letter is a formal letter written by one person or party to seek permission from another person or party. Writing a consent letter is important in order to take permission to initiate an action.

Concept note-3: -Consent is an agreement between participants to engage in sexual activity. Consent should be clearly and freely communicated. A verbal and affirmative expression of consent can help both you and your partner to understand and respect each other’s boundaries.

Concept note-4: -The consent letter must contain original signature(s), and cannot contain any restrictions, conditions, or stipulations. Any restrictions or conditions must be kept separately between the parties involved. The consent letter must simply state that consent is given to a person to use the similar name.
