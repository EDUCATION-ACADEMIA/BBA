Which is NOT the characteristic of Ethnographic Research?

(B) carried out in the context in which the subjects normally live and work

(D) ** the researcher does not try to control or handle the phenomenon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We identified six trademark features to be considered when embracing an ethnographic approach: naturalism; context; multiple data sources; small case numbers; ‘emic’ and ‘etic’ perspectives, and ethical considerations.

Concept note-2: -Ethnography can be briefly defined as the systematic study of people and cultures. It is designed to explore cultural phenomena where the researcher observes society from the point of view of the subject of the study. It is a means to represent graphically and in writing the culture of a group.
