If a research involves careful judgement, it is ____

(A) Empirical

(B) Analytical

(C) ** Critical

(D) Logical

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -RESEARCH is “a careful or critical inquiry or examination in. seeking facts or principles; a diligent investigation to ascertain something, ” according to Webster’s New International Dictionary.

Concept note-2: -Good research is replicable, reproducible, and transparent. Replicability, reproducibility, and transparency are some of the most important characteristics of research. The replicability of a research study is important because this allows other researchers to test the study’s findings.

Concept note-3: -A characteristic of research that exhibits, careful and precise judgment is critical research, hence of all the options. Option c is the right answer.

Concept note-4: -Critical research is a loosely defined genre of social inquiry whose central theme involves the problematization of knowledge.
