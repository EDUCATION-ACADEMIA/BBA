RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

[SOURCES]
The research method chapter in the mini-research proposal should consists of ____

(A) data collection method, intrumentation, population, sample and sampling method, research question, data analysis

(B) data collection method, research objective, population, sample and sampling method, research question, data analysis

(C) ** data collection method, intrumentation, population, sample and sampling method, data analysis

(D) data collection method, theories, intrumentation, population, sample and sampling method, data analysis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A key part of your thesis, dissertation, or research paper, the methodology chapter explains what you did and how you did it, allowing readers to evaluate the reliability and validity of your research and your dissertation topic.

Concept note-2: -The methods section of a research proposal contains details about how you will conduct your research. It includes your study design-the methodology and methods that you plan to use-as well as your work plan-the activities that you plan to undertake to complete your project.

Concept note-3: -CHAPTER 3-RESEARCH METHODOLOGY: Data collection method and Research tools.

Concept note-4: -Survey research a research method involving the use of standardized questionnaires or interviews to collect data about people and their preferences, thoughts, and behaviors in a systematic manner.
