Reporting focus group data does not result in graphs or charts; rather, the focus is on ____ and is thus a ____ methodology.

(A) ** language ____ qualitative

(B) language ____ quantitative

(C) numbers ____ qualitative

(D) themes ____ quantitative

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A focus group is a group interview of approximately six to twelve people who share similar characteristics or common interests. A facilitator guides the group based on a predetermined set of topics. The facilitator creates an environment that encourages participants to share their perceptions and points of view.

Concept note-2: -Include a cover page with the name of the report, date, location, company name and topic discussed. Start the report with an executive summary that outlines when the focus group was held, where, who participated, the purpose and any big-picture results obtained.

Concept note-3: -Thematic analysis is a qualitative data analysis method that involves reading through a data set (such as transcripts from in depth interviews or focus groups), and identifying patterns in meaning across the data to derive themes.
