It is a collection of observations from which conclusions can be drawn. Itt can be words, numbers or images.

(A) hypothesis

(B) systematic

(C) research

(D) ** data

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Data is a collection of facts, such as numbers, words, measurements, observations or even just descriptions of things.

Concept note-2: -Information: A collection of facts from which conclusions may be drawn.

Concept note-3: -The correct option is A Data. Data are a collection of facts and figures which include numbers, words, measurements and observations for a definite purpose.

Concept note-4: -These facts or figures, which are numerical or otherwise, collected with a definite purpose are called data. Data is the plural form of the Latin word datum.
