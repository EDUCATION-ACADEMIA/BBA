CUSTOMER RELATION MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
Which question below represents a CRM analyzing technology question?

(A) ** Why did sales not meet forecasts?

(B) What customers are at risk of leaving?

(C) What is the total revenue by customer?

(D) All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A collective value proposition refers to a business or marketing statement that summarizes why a consumer should buy a product or use a service. Thus it is is not an element of Customer Relationship Management.

Concept note-2: -Analytical CRM systems provide business intelligence by analyzing customer behavior and perceptions. Customer-facing applications and customer-touching applications provide data to be used in analytical CRM systems.

Concept note-3: -CRM predicting technologies help organizations identify their customers across other applications.

Concept note-4: -CRM predicting technologies – Help organizations make predictions regarding customer behavior such as which customers are at risk of leaving.
