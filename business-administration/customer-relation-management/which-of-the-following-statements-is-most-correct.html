EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Acceptance testing ensures that the end-user (customers) can achieve the goals set in the business requirements, which determines whether the software is acceptable for delivery or not. It is also known as user acceptance testing (UAT).

Concept note-2: -This cycle for quality assurance consists of four steps: Plan, Do, Check, and Act. because it analyzes existing conditions and methods used to provide the product or service customers.

Concept note-3: -User or customer testing is a stage in the testing process in which users or customers provide input and advice on system testing.

Concept note-4: -System testing: In this method, your software is compiled as a whole and then tested as a whole.
