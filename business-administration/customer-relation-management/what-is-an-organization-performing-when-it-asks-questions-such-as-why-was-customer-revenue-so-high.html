What is an organization performing when it asks questions such as “why was customer revenue so high”?

(A) CRM reporting technologies

(B) ** CRM analyzing technologies

(C) CRM processing technologies

(D) CRM predicting technologies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is an organization performing when it asks questions such as “why was customer revenue so high"? The management of information flows between and among activities in a supply chain to maximize total supply chain effectiveness and corporate profitability.

Concept note-2: -CRM Predicting technologies are the ones that help an organization to predict the behavior of the customers like which customers are unsatisfied and are on a verge of leaving. Hence, when a organization asks questions like ‘which customers are at risk of leaving, it is involved in CRM predicting technologies.

Concept note-3: -An operational CRM is a system incorporating the sales, marketing, and service automation tools that store and process information about your company’s every single interaction with the customer.
