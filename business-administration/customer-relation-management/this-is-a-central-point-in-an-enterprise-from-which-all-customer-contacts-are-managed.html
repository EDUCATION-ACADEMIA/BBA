CUSTOMER RELATION MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
This is a central point in an enterprise from which all customer contacts are managed.

(A) ** Contact center

(B) Help system

(C) Multichannel marketing

(D) Help desk

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A Contact Center is a central point in an enterprise from which all customer contacts are managed.

Concept note-2: -Contact center is a central point in an enterprise from which all customer contacts are managed.

Concept note-3: -Customer relationship management (CRM) is a technology for managing all your company’s relationships and interactions with customers and potential customers. The goal is simple: Improve business relationships.

Concept note-4: -Customer relationship management (CRM) is a process in which a business or other organization administers its interactions with customers, typically using data analysis to study large amounts of information.

Concept note-5: -Database marketing is a systematic approach to the gathering, consolidation and processing of consumer data. Database marketing is also a form of direct marketing and may be called customer relationship management. Data of both customers and potential customers are collected and are maintained in a company’s database.
