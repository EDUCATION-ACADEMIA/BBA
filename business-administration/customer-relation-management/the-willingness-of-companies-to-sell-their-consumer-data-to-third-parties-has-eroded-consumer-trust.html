CUSTOMER RELATION MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
The willingness of companies to sell their consumer data to third parties has eroded consumer trust.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -70% of consumers are willing to share their data to receive special discounts and offers. 71% of consumers will shop more often with brands or retailers that personalize their communications.

Concept note-2: -User data can legally be sold as long as legal conditions for its collection and sale have been met and there isn’t any regulation against it. In some instances, for example, data has to be de-identified first.

Concept note-3: -Recommend the business to others (73%) Defend the company to others (74%) Think highly or have a positive perception about the organization (73%) Share information about the business on social media (64%)

Concept note-4: -Privacy policies establish the relationship between a company and a customer or client concerning the methods by which the company is able to use the individual’s PII. Increasingly an industry standard, privacy policies inform customers what PII is collected, how it is used, and with whom it may be shared.
