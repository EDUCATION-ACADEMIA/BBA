You don’t have to Connect your ManyChat account to your FB page to use it.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Connecting a Facebook Page You will see the “Sign Up With Facebook” button. Click on it and confirm that you grant necessary permissions to ManyChat which you can look up at https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=business tools by clicking on the ManyChat app.

Concept note-2: -Go to Settings-> Team, find your name in the table with users that have access to the account. If you used your own credentials to log in to ManyChat you should see “It’s me” text right next to your name. This is your status on this ManyChat account. As you can see, on this account I am the owner.

Concept note-3: -Click on “My Profile” at the bottom of the left sidebar: The “Accounts” section contains the following information about each page: Name.
