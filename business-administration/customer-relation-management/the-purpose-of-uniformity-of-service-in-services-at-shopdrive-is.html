The purpose of uniformity of service in services at Shop&Drive is

(A) Quick response and focus on solutions for customers

(B) ** Uniformity of service, product and warranty

(C) Qualified product knowledge and skills

(D) Attitude and accurate customer database

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A service is a means of delivering value to customers by facilitating outcomes that customers want to achieve without owning the costs and risks.

Concept note-2: -Service quality is an input variable for customer satisfaction and leads to customer satisfaction. This relationship could be clarified in a way that quality of products and services is lead to customer satisfaction and customer satisfaction also lead to profitability.

Concept note-3: -Products are broadly classified into two categories – consumer products and industrial products.

Concept note-4: -You can set positive expectations. If you consistently go above and beyond for your customers, then this will also be noticed. You can build a reputation for being fair, reliable, and dependable. Customers will come to you because they know they can count on receiving an excellent experience.
