Concept note-1: -flicked. past participle. flicked. DEFINITIONS3. transitive to make something move quickly and suddenly, especially with a quick movement of the hand.

Concept note-2: -/flɪk/ to move or hit something with a short sudden movement: He carefully flicked the loose hairs from the shoulders of his jacket. She quickly flicked the crumbs off the table. Horses flick their tails to make flies go away.

Concept note-3: -verb. flicked; flicking; flicks. transitive verb. : to move or propel with a light quick movement.

Concept note-4: -It is a tool used to circulate information about the product or service. A brochure is like a magazine but with pictures of the product or the service which the brand is promoting.
