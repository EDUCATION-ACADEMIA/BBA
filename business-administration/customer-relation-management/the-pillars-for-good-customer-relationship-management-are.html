The pillars for good customer relationship management are

(A) ** Customer database and technology

(B) Technology and knowledge

(C) Excellent service dan database customer

(D) Knowledge dan excellent service

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -customer-focused employees; customer-focused processes; the right CRM system; having access to the right, current customer data.

Concept note-2: -Marketing, Sales and Customer Support/Service are the three pillars of customer relationship.

Concept note-3: -Why is Customer Service So Important Today? Pillar 1: Persistence. Pillar 2: Personalization. Pillar 3: Intelligence.

Concept note-4: -With relationship marketing, all of the above is transformed and brought to life through three key pillars: marketing, service quality and personalized customer service.
