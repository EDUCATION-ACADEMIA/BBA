What is the second step in communication planning?

(A) Determine the best channel of communication

(B) Determine the purpose of the message

(C) ** Identify the audience

(D) Clarify objectives

(E) Outline the communication for delivery

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2. Identify the target audience. After indicating your objectives, the second step is to identify the group of people that can receive your message.

Concept note-2: -The five steps involved in creating a communications plan are: (1) establishing goals; (2) defining key audiences; (3) identifying key messages; (4) creating a tactical outreach plan; and (5) specifying a timeline for moving forward.

Concept note-3: -The communication process is made up of four key components. Those components include encoding, medium of transmission, decoding, and feedback.

Concept note-4: -The primary target audience is the people or group of people for whom you want to change the way they think, act or behave in relationship to your CSR initiatives. These may be your employees, members of the community, consumers of your product or services, or government officials.
