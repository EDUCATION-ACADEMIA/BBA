Can a letter be a method of communication?

(A) No

(B) ** Yes

(C) Sometimes

(D) I don’t know

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Even today a letter is an important means of communication in both the workspace as well as our personal lives.

Concept note-2: -While e-mail and text messages may be used more frequently today, the effective business letter remains a common form of written communication. It can serve to introduce you to a potential employer, announce a product or service, or even serve to communicate feelings and emotions.

Concept note-3: -A letter is a form of non-fictional written, typed or printed communication. These are usually written to facilitate communication between two individuals and are usually sent to the recipient via mail or post in an envelope.

Concept note-4: -Thus, the letter is an example of verbal communication.

Concept note-5: -Email is typically a form of one-way communication-meaning it does not allow for an immediate exchange of ideas like calls, in-person, or online virtual meetings do.
