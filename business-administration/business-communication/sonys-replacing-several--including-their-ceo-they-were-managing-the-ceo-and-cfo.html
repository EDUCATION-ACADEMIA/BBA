Sony’s replacing several ____, including their CEO. They were managing the CEO and CFO.

(A) ** executive officers

(B) HR personnel

(C) marketing shareholders

(D) business companions

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The CEO assumes the main role of overseeing the operations of the entire company, from sales to administration. He holds the highest rank in the company and only reports to the board of directors. On the other hand, the CFO assumes the highest-ranked financial position in the company.

Concept note-2: -Hiroki Totoki, Sony’s chief financial officer, will become its president and COO, Sony Corp. said in a statement. Kenichiro Yoshida remains chairman and chief executive, signaling continuity at the Tokyo-based maker of PlayStation game machines, Bravia TVs and Spider-Man movies.

Concept note-3: -Hirai became chairman of Sony Computer Entertainment on September 1, 2011, and was replaced by Andrew House as president and group CEO. He retired as chairman of SCEI on June 25, 2012, but remains on the board of directors.
