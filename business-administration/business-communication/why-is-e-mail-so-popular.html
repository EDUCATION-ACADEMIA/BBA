Why is e-mail so popular?

(A) It is usually informal

(B) It saves time and provides a record

(C) It takes little training to learn

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Widespread usage: Email is important because many people may use it daily to communicate with others and learn more about businesses. For example, many people check their email on their computers and carry their phones, which allows them to access their email inboxes frequently.

Concept note-2: -It is faster and more secure than conventional mail. It requires less physical effort to edit and send a letter of communication. Once the hardware, software, and Internet connection are in place, email on the Internet is free, even if messages is to be sent to the other side of the world.

Concept note-3: -Advantages1. E-mail is extremely cost effective. 2. E-mail takes lesser time for transmitting data than conventional mail.

Concept note-4: -The main advantage we could think of Email is that it is paperless. The documents of instructions and status updates can be sent through the mail rather than typed on paper and distributing the same. This makes the work easy with less clutter and is environment-friendly.
