This type of communication style works best in a group setting and feeds off other people’s ideas

(A) Socializer

(B) ** Collaborator

(C) Analyzer

(D) Controller

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A Collaborative Communication Style is focused on including other people instead of just working with other people. It is an environment where everyone’s voice is heard and valued. People with a Collaborative Communication Style want to make sure they get feedback/opinions from as many people as possible.

Concept note-2: -There are four main styles of communication: passive, aggressive, passive-aggressive, and assertive.

Concept note-3: -There are a few different frameworks for understanding communication styles. Do a quick Google search and you’ll find the classic four: assertive, aggressive, passive-aggressive, and passive.

Concept note-4: -Assertive. Thought to be the most effective form of communication, the assertive communication style features an open communication link while not being overbearing. Assertive communicators can express their own needs, desires, ideas and feelings, while also considering the needs of others.
