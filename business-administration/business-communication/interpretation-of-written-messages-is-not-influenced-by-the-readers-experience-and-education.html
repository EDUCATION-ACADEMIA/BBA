Interpretation of written messages is not influenced by the readers’ experience and education

(A) TRUE

(B) ** FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Language comprehension is the ability to understand the different elements of spoken or written language, like the meaning of words and how words are put together to form sentences. Language comprehension is one of the building blocks of reading comprehension.

Concept note-2: -There is no right or wrong answer to a reading response. Nonetheless, it is important that you demonstrate an understanding of the reading and clearly explain and support your reactions.

Concept note-3: -According to the original theory, an individual’s reading comprehension is the product of her decoding skill and language comprehension (Gough & Tunmer, 1986). The key idea is that both the ability to decode and language comprehension are necessary for reading comprehension.

Concept note-4: -The difference in Language: is the most obvious barrier to communication as two people speaking two different languages cannot communicate with each other. For example, an American goes to Egypt. He does not understand Arabic, and most people in Egypt do not understand English.
