Sender has idea is the ____ steps in the communication process.

(A) ** First

(B) Second

(C) Fifth

(D) Eight

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The sender starts the steps of communication by creating a message they intend to send to an individual or group. They take time to craft the information they want to transmit.

Concept note-2: -Sender: This is the person that is delivering a message to a recipient. Message: This refers to the information that the sender is relaying to the receiver. Channel of communication: This is the transmission or method of delivering the message. Decoding: This is the interpretation of the message.

Concept note-3: -The communication process has five steps: idea formation, encoding, channel selection, decoding and feedback. Anything that interferes with clear communication is called noise. Noise can interfere with each step of the communication process.

Concept note-4: -The sender will initiate the communication process by developing an idea into a message, also known as encoding. The sender will then transmit the message through a channel, or a method of delivery.

Concept note-5: -Before the message can be sent to the receiver, the sender needs to encode the message.
