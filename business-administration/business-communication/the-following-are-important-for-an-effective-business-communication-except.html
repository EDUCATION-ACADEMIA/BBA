BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

BUSINESS COMMUNICATION

[SOURCES]
The following are important for an effective business communication EXCEPT;

(A) specific message and content

(B) ** random audience that is targeted for the content of the message

(C) email is used as most of the receiver have access to an email and internet connection.

(D) Mr Brown who is made in-charged of sharing the specific message knows everything about the message.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Business communication contributes to the development of an ability to influence others, bringing about changes in the attitudes and views of people, driving motivation, as well as creating and maintaining professional relationships.

Concept note-2: -Effective communication is essential for managing relationships with your staff, customers and stakeholders. Poor communication can ruin relationships, and potentially result in lost sales and a damaged reputation. Read how to improve your business communication with staff, suppliers and customers.

Concept note-3: -For communication to be effective, it must be clear, correct, complete, concise, and compassionate. We consider these to be the 5 C’s of communication, though they may vary depending on who you’re asking.

Concept note-4: -The seven C’s of communication is a list of principles for written and spoken communications to ensure that they are effective. The seven C’s are: clear, correct, complete, concrete, concise, considered and courteous.
