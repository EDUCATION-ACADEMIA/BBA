What is enterprise instant messaging (EIM)?

(A) ** IM systems designed for large-scale corporate use,

(B) It is another name used to refer to short messaging service (SMS)

(C) It is a form of phone-based text messaging

(D) It is a system of e-mail messaging

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Enterprise instant messaging apps can be used for file sharing, company updates, and even employee recognition. These are the top benefits of using a real-time enterprise messaging solution for your internal communication.

Concept note-2: -there are two kinds of instant messaging software – application based and Web based. Application based instant messaging software is downloaded and installed on user’s computer. Some of the popular instant messaging software are: Google Talk • Yahoo! Messenger • Skype • Windows Live Messenger • Rediff Bol, etc.

Concept note-3: -Instant messaging (IM) technology is a type of online chat that offers real-time exchange of text messages between two or more people logged into a particular instant messaging (IM) service. In order to set up an instant message, you have to add the usernames of the people you want to message to your contact list.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Instant messaging (IM) technology is a type of online chat that offers real-time text transmission over the Internet. A LAN messenger operates in a similar way over a local area network. Short messages are typically transmitted between two parties, when each user chooses to complete a thought and select “send".
