How does intercultural communication NOT affect you personally?

(A) How you treat people from different cultures

(B) ** How you interact with your family members

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By way of summarizing, the role of Intercultural Communication in work life is in helping people understand how culture shapes the different ways we communicate, collaborate and coordinate. We can use this understanding to help us recognize what is being communicated to us and how we communicate with others.

Concept note-2: -Your family culture influences things like your moral compass, beliefs, values, and traditions. You might choose a career based on your family culture by picking something you know your family values.

Concept note-3: -Cultural values can influence communication orientation, or the degree of interaction between family members, as well as conformity orientation, or the degree of conformity within a family. Both of these define the limitations of communication within the family.

Concept note-4: -The cultural beliefs of individual family members and the entire family inform decisions made about the child and the family. Cultures shape our views on key issues such as family roles and goals, caregiving practices, learning, education, school readiness, child behaviors, and the nature of childhood itself.
