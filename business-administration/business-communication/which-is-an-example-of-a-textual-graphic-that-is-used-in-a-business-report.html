Concept note-3: -In a full block business letter, every component of the letter (heading, address, salutation, body, salutation, signature, identification, enclosures) is aligned to the left. Also, first sentences of paragraphs are not indented.

Concept note-4: -Communication is an essential part of every business, and it is one of the most crucial human relations skills for professionals. Businesses often find more success when their employees can politely and effectively communicate their opinions, needs and concerns.

Concept note-5: -The way data is stored plays a major role in how easy it is to control access, use and protect.
