There have been a high number of resignations in your organisation in the last 6 months. Your manager has asked you to investigate the issue and prepare a report for the management. To ensure your report is credible, you need to ____

(A) ** present both sides of the issue

(B) use labels and slanted expressions to appear convincing

(C) ** tell your readers where the information came from

(D) highlight your opinion towards the matter

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Acknowledge the resignation – The reporting boss and the HR manager coordinate to decide if they want to attempt to retain the employee or not. – Arrange for an informal meeting where you can discuss with the employee if there is any way you can retain him.

Concept note-2: -Answers (1) You can quit the job without your employer’s consent, but you need to send him an official email to tell him you will leave the job, even if he refuses before 6 months are complete. You must review your contract whether it has penal conditions or not.
