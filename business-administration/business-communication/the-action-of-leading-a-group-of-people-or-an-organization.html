The action of leading a group of people or an organization.

(A) ** Leadership

(B) Initiative

(C) Responsibility

(D) Effective

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“The action of leading a group of people or an organisation.” That’s how the Oxford Dictionary defines leadership. In simple words, leadership is about taking risks and challenging the status quo. Leaders motivate others to achieve something new and better.

Concept note-2: -leaders. A leader is the one in the charge, the person who convinces other people to follow. A great leader inspires confidence in other people and moves them to action.

Concept note-3: -Leadership is not about a position or a title. Leadership is an action. We make leadership decisions every minute of every day, both at work and in our personal lives. When we speak up for what is right, either at work or in our home lives, we are demonstrating leadership.

Concept note-4: -Shared leadership is a leadership style that broadly distributes leadership responsibility, such that people within a team and organization lead each other.

Concept note-5: -What is Leading as a Manager? Leading involves influencing others through direction, inspiration, and motivation toward the attainment of organizational objectives. Leading is distinct from the organizing function of management, which generally includes arranging or assembling resources.
