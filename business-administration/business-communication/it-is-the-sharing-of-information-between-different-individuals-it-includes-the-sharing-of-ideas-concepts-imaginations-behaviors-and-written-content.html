BUSINESS COMMUNICATION

[SOURCES]
It is the sharing of information between different individuals. It includes the sharing of ideas, concepts, imaginations, behaviors and written content.

(A) Language

(B) ** Communication

(C) Language Learning

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Communication is a Latin word which means ‘to share’. It is the sharing of information between different individuals. It includes the sharing of ideas, concepts, imaginations, behaviours and written content. Communication is simply defined as the transfer of information from one place to another.

Concept note-2: -communication, n. The imparting or exchanging of information by speaking, writing, or using some other medium. The successful conveying or sharing of ideas and feelings.

Concept note-3: -Verbal communication refers to the use of speech to send a message.

Concept note-4: -Communication is defined as the process of understanding and sharing meaning (Pearson & Nelson, 2000).
