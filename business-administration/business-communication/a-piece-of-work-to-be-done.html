A piece of work to be done.

(A) ** Task

(B) Goal

(C) Schedule

(D) Value

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution A man can do a piece of work in 5 days. ∴ Time taken by son to finish work = 15/2 = 7.5 days.

Concept note-2: -A can do a piece of work in 20 days and B can do the same piece of work in 30 days. Find that in how many days both can do the work? So, the work will be completed in 12 days if they work together. Hence option [B] is correct answer.

Concept note-3: -“How would you respond to a request for doing a task you’ve never done before?” Correct answer: “I’m a very quick learner and like a challenge, so I would accept the task. If it’s something that I feel uncertain about, I would ask for guidance.

Concept note-4: -GET HELP – Just don’t sit with the task in hand and waste time, get out of your desk and ask your colleague, your friends, your mentor any one . Call someone you know, ask your manager, ask any one who you think can help.
