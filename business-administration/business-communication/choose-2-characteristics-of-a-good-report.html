(A) ** Accurate-Information is factually correct and error-free.

(B) ** Complete-Include all information for readers to understand the situation, problems, or proposal.

(C) Provide practical information-instruction, explanation, and solution

(D) State precise responsibilities

Concept note-2: -The facts presented in a report should be accurate and relevant.
