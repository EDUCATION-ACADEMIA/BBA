[SOURCES]
Telephones are used a lot in many businesses on a day to day basis

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Time Spent Using Smartphones Annually And 1 in 5 smartphone users spends upwards of 4.5 hours on average on their phones every day. Perhaps surprisingly, weekdays average more smartphone use than weekends. On average, people check their phones 58 times per day.

Concept note-2: -Smartphones both have advantages and disadvantages to our society including: Time Saving: The ability to do so many different things on one device (from checking your email, texting friends, checking the weather and news, making appointments, make bank transcations, etc.

Concept note-3: -An efficient business telephone system streamlines good communication between organisation and customers. The telephone offers a faster interaction than email, is more personal, and easy and quick to use.

Concept note-4: -Your business likely uses landline, mobile or digital phones to share information throughout departments, handle inquiries from customers and clients, book appointments for essential services and hold conference calls.
