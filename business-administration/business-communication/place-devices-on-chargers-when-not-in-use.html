Place devices on chargers when not in use.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is perfectly safe to leave your phone charger cord and power adapters plugged in when you are not charging your phone. What is this? The main reason for this is that there will be little to no electricity running through the power cords.

Concept note-2: -Cell Phones use approximately 2 to 6 watts when charging, while a charger left plugged in without a phone will consume 0.1 to 0.5 of a watt. Charging an iphone or android phone under normal use conditions will typically cost under a dollar for a full year.

Concept note-3: -The truth is that the consumption is negligible. If you are one of those people who go around the house unplugging every charger that comes your way, you should know that your efforts are worth practically nothing.

Concept note-4: -Unlike your cell phone battery, LaMay says keeping your phone charger plugged all the time probably won’t have any effect on it’s lifespan. “Chances are that you will change phones (and chargers) long before your current charger stops working, ” he says.
