When a person is trying to get his or her message across to another person, it means he or she is trying to ____ the message

(A) decode

(B) encode

(C) compile

(D) ** transmit

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Communication refers to the process by which the information is transmitted and understood between two or more people. Transmitting the sender’s intended meaning is the essence of effective communication. Communication involves two people – a sender and a receiver.

Concept note-2: -In other words, the sender sends a message to the receiver, who then sends a message back to the sender. The messages that are sent back from the receiver to the sender are called feedback.

Concept note-3: -Also called the channel, the medium is the means by which a message is transmitted.
