As per Newman and Summer Communication is the Exchange of

(A) Facts

(B) Opinion

(C) Emotions

(D) ** All the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As per Newman and Summer Communication is the Exchange of Facts, Opinion and Emotions.

Concept note-2: -William Newman and Charles Summer: Communication is an exchange of ideas, facts, opinions or emotions of two or more persons.

Concept note-3: -William Newman and Charles Summer define communication as “an exchange of ideas, facts, opinions or emotions of two or more persons”.

Concept note-4: -Summer Newman is a fictional character from The Young and the Restless, an American soap opera on the CBS network. Created by head writers Lynn Marie Latham and Kay Alden, the role is currently portrayed by Allison Lanier.

Concept note-5: -Horizontal communication, sometimes referred to as lateral communication, encompasses workplace communications among people, departments or teams at the same level in an organization.
