Which is horizontal communication?

(A) ** Communication between employees with similar jobs

(B) Communication between manager and employee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The horizontal communication definition refers to the interactions that occur between employees that work at the same hierarchical level. Also known as lateral communication, this type of communication includes employees who work together in the same area, as well as employees who work in different departments.

Concept note-2: -Think about brainstorming sessions, department-wide meetings and group discussions with your coworkers. These are examples of horizontal communication.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Lateral communication.

Concept note-4: -Horizontal Communication When communication takes place between people at the same level of the organization, like between two departments or between two peers, it’s called horizontal (or lateral) communication.

Concept note-5: -Horizontal Communication – This type of communication is also known as “lateral” communication. It may be defined as communication that takes place between co-workers in the same department, or in different departments, with different areas of responsibility.
