Which of the following describes an Open communication style?

(A) Very deliberate; provide very accurate, carefully thought-out results

(B) ** Readily expresses emotions, thoughts, and feelings without holding back in any way

(C) Tends to hold back on giving away too much information that may reveal their inner nature

(D) Fast-paced and assertive; can be argumentative

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Assertive. Thought to be the most effective form of communication, the assertive communication style features an open communication link while not being overbearing. Assertive communicators can express their own needs, desires, ideas and feelings, while also considering the needs of others.

Concept note-2: -Open communication is when people can openly express their thoughts and ideas to one another. In a business setting, an organization can implement open communication by encouraging all employees to express their feedback and thoughts.

Concept note-3: -There are four main styles of communication: passive, aggressive, passive-aggressive, and assertive.
