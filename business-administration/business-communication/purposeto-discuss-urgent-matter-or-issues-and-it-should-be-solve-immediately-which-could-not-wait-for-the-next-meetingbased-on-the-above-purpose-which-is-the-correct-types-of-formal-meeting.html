BUSINESS COMMUNICATION

[SOURCES]
“Purpose:To discuss urgent matter or issues and it should be solve immediately which could not wait for the next meeting"Based on the above purpose, which is the correct types of formal meeting?

(A) Inaugural general meeting

(B) Annual general meeting

(C) ** Extraordinary general meeting

(D) Committee meeting

(E) Subcommittee Meeting

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An Extraordinary General Meeting (EGM) is a meeting held by a company to deliberate upon matters that require the urgent attention of senior executives, the board of directors, and all shareholders and cannot be deferred till the next scheduled annual general meeting.

Concept note-2: -An extraordinary general meeting (EGM) refers to any shareholder meeting called by a company other than its scheduled annual meeting. The extraordinary general meeting is utilized to deal with urgent matters that come up between annual shareholders’ meetings.

Concept note-3: -If the business to be transacted is not special and not urgent. then such business can be discussed in the next Annual General Meeting. Thus, only under special circumstances Extraordinary General Meeting is held.

Concept note-4: -Three forms of resolutions are available: ordinary resolution, special resolution and unanimous resolution. There is no concept of special resolution in board meetings and very few unanimous resolutions are also required.
