[SOURCES]
____ is a written form of communication b/w different departments in the same organization.

(A) ** Memoramdum

(B) Artical

(C) Report

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In simple words, a memorandum is a written message or information from one person or department to another in the same business. It is less formal than a letter. A memorandum is often abbreviated as a memo. Effective memos clearly state the objective in the first sentence.

Concept note-2: -Interdepartmental communication is largely a formal affair between different departments of an organization. Interdepartmental communication is effective when it is supported by good infrastructural facilities.

Concept note-3: -A memo, short for memorandum, is a brief internal communication that informs a group about a specific problem, solution, or event. Memos may be sent as a paper letter or fax or attached to an email as a PDF.

Concept note-4: -There are two types of internal communication: intradepartmental (within the same department) and interdepartmental (between two different departments). The benefits of cultivating strong interdepartmental communication are obvious.

Concept note-5: -Interdepartmental memos were a method of communication between departments at the British Ministry of Magic.
