Which of the following summarizes the main points of a report?

(A) table of contents

(B) ** executive summary

(C) conclusion

(D) citation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An executive summary should summarize the key points of the report. It should restate the purpose of the report, highlight the major points of the report, and describe any results, conclusions, or recommendations from the report.

Concept note-2: -An executive summary is a thorough overview of a research report or other type of document that synthesizes key points for its readers, saving them time and preparing them to understand the study’s overall content.

Concept note-3: -Which of the following is true of the executive summary of a report? It includes all important findings, conclusions, and recommendations.

Concept note-4: -Whether you’ve put together a business plan or an investment proposal, you’re going to need an executive summary to preface your report. The summary should include the major details of your report, but it’s important not to bore the reader with minutiae.
