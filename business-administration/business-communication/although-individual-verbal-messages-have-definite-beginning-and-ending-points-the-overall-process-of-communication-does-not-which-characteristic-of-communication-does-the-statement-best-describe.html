Although individual verbal messages have definite beginning and ending points, the overall process of communication does not. Which characteristic of communication does the statement best describe?

(A) Communication is intentional

(B) Communication is interactive

(C) ** Communication is dynamic

(D) Communication is symbolic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2. Although individual verbal messages have definite beginning and ending points, the overall process of communication does not. Which characteristic of communication does this statement best describe? Communication is symbolic.

The individual begins to appreciate important cultural differences in behaviors and eventually in values. ▫An individual in the acceptance stage might be heard saying: “These people and I have different values and experiences, and I think we can learn from one.

Concept note-3: -Intercultural communication refers to the effects on communication behavior, when different cultures interact together.

Concept note-4: -Inter-cultural communication principles guide the process of exchanging meaningful and unambiguous information across cultural boundaries, that preserves mutual respect and minimises antagonism. Intercultural communication can be defined simply by the communication between people from two different cultures.
