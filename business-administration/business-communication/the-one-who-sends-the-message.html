The one who sends the message.

(A) ** Speaker

(B) Receiver

(C) Message

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A sender is someone who encodes and sends a message to a receiver through a particular channel. The sender is the initiator of communication.

Concept note-2: -The sender is the person sending the message. The receiver is the person who receives the message.

Concept note-3: -In the communication process, the sender is the individual who initiates a message and is also called the communicator or source of communication. The sender might be a speaker, a writer, or someone who merely gestures.

Concept note-4: -Sender: It is also called the source. Sender or source, of a communication is the person or organization that has the information to share with another person or group of persons.

Concept note-5: -The sender is the originator of the idea or message that is to be conveyed. The sender must choose the best way to convert the idea(s) or message(s) into words, diagrams, graphs, reports, and so on. This conversion process is known as encoding the message.
