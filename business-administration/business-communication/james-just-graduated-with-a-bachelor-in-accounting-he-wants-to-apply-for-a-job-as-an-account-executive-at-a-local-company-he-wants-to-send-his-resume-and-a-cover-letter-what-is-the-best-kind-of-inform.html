(A) The information provided in both documents should be as early as his secondary school days.

(B) ** The information in both documents should only be about the latest information from his last few semesters of his study.

(C) The resume should include information about his secondary school days but the cover letter should include only his qualification related to being an accountant

(D) The cover letter should include information about his secondary school days but the resume should include only the qualification needed for an Account Executive
