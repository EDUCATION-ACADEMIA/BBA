When two or more people come together to communicate, each person brings their own ____

(A) ** Perspective

(B) Conversation

(C) Ideas

(D) Tools

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Your tone should support the message and indicate your feelings or emotion behind the message. Differing Perspectives: When two or more people come together to communicate, each person brings their own perspective, and they send and receive information from that perspective.

Concept note-2: -Interpersonal communication occurs when two people form a dyad, also known as two people communicating with one another.

Concept note-3: -When communication occurs, it typically happens in one of three ways: verbal, nonverbal and visual.

Concept note-4: -Nonverbal Nonverbal communication is the use of body language, gestures and facial expressions to convey information to others.
