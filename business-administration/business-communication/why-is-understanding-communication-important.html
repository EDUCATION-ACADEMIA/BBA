Why is understanding communication important?

(A) It will help you be more effective in communicating with others

(B) It will help to reduce misunderstanding between people

(C) It helps to avoid friction between people

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Good communication skills are essential to allow others and yourself to understand information more accurately and quickly. In contrast, poor communication skills lead to frequent misunderstandings and frustration.

Concept note-2: -Good communication is critical in ensuring coordination and control of individuals, groups and departments. Good communication ensures individuals know what is expected of them, that the appropriate person receives the correct information and that there is coordination within the organisation.

Concept note-3: -Importance of communication in management are: (1) basis of co-ordination (2) smooth working of an enterprise (3) basis of decision-making (4) enhances managerial efficiency (5) promotion of co-operation and industrial peace (6) establishment of effective leadership and (7) morale-building and motivation.

Concept note-4: -Communication plays a vital part in building up a strong relationship across the world, either in organisational structure or outside of it. It is an essential pillar for people in sharing the ideas, delegating responsibilities, management of a team, building up a healthy relationship, etc.
