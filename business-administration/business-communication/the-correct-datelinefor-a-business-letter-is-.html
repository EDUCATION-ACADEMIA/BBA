The correct datelinefor a business letter is ____

(A) 6/2/02

(B) June 2, 2002

(C) 2 June 2002

(D) ** Both b and c

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Question 1-The answer of this question is option ‘d’ that is both b &c A date in business letter can be written both month, date, and year or date, month and year Question 2-The answer of this question is option ‘c’ …

Concept note-2: -Write out the month, day and year two inches from the top of the page. Depending which format you are using for your letter, either left justify the date or tab to the center point and type the date. In the latter case, include the sender’s address in letterhead, rather than left-justified.

Concept note-3: -Expert-Verified Answer The date can be written on the right side of the page, a few lines below your address, or on the left side, a few lines below the other person’s address.

Concept note-4: -The seven parts of a business letter are: sender’s address, date, recipient address, salutation, body, closing salutation and signature. If you have documents attached with the letter, include a list of enclosures after the signature.
