What is nonverbal communication?

(A) Unofficial way of sharing information in an organization

(B) method in which team members write down and evaluate ideas to be shared with the team

(C) ** Delivering messages by means other than speaking or writing skills

(D) The means by which a message is conveyed

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nonverbal communication (NVC) is the transmission of messages or signals through a nonverbal platform such as eye contact, facial expressions, gestures, posture, use of objects and body language.

Concept note-2: -Your nonverbal communication cues-the way you listen, look, move, and react-tell the person you’re communicating with whether or not you care, if you’re being truthful, and how well you’re listening. When your nonverbal signals match up with the words you’re saying, they increase trust, clarity, and rapport.
