____ is also called the Gossip chain.

(A) ** Star chain

(B) single chain

(C) cluster chain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gossip Chain: In the gossip chain, one person seeks and tells the information to everyone. This chain is just like the wheel where one person stays at the center and the information passes along the spokes of the wheel to others stationed on the rim.

Concept note-2: -For example, two employees of the organisation are going in for a love marriage and some particular person has got this information, he passes on this information to a large number of people. Gossip chain has been shown in diagram. In this diagram, Mr ‘A’ is passing on his information to B, C, D, E, F, G, H, I, etc.

Concept note-3: -3) Single Strand chain: This method involves the transfer of information through a single line of persons to the final recipient. For example, person A will tell person B, person B will tell person C, and so on until the message reaches the final recipient.

Concept note-4: -Gossip chain In gossip chain grapevine communication, one professional shares a piece of information with multiple colleagues at one time. This professional also typically seeks the information with the purpose of sharing it.
