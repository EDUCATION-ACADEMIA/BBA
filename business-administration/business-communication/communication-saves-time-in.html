Communication saves time in

(A) Internal communication..

(B) interview

(C) schedule

(D) ** oral communication

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Oral communication is time-saving as far as daily interactions are concerned, but in case of meetings, long speeches consume lot of time and are unproductive at times.

Concept note-2: -Oral communication skills are fundamental to the development of literacy and essential for thinking and learning. It is the glue that puts all the components of a language together.

Concept note-3: -Informal communication is considered effective as employees can discuss work-related issues which saves the organization time and money.

Concept note-4: -Oral communication is the ability to transmit ideas from your brain to either one person or a group of people. Good use of verbal skills means presenting an idea clearly while each thought is articulated in a cohesive manner. It has everything to do with the language that we choose to use.

Concept note-5: -Immediate Feedback. The biggest advantage of oral communication is that it provides immediate feedback to both the sender and the receiver. Each can therefore ask for clarification and elaboration on the spot.
