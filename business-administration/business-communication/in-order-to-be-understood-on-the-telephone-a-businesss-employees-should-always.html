In order to be understood on the telephone, a business’s employees should always

(A) ** enunciate clearly

(B) speak from notes

(C) use technical terminology

(D) speak really fast

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In order to be understood on the telephone, a business’s employees should ALWAYS: Enunciate clearly. When organizing information to present in a business report, it is effective to: Developed an outline.

Concept note-2: -Formal: When conducting evaluation or counseling interviews with employees, it is usually appropriate to use a formal communication style.

Concept note-3: -Listen respectfully. The best way for businesses to communicate with customers is to listen respectfully. Customers have a lot of useful information to give businesses if they will listen.

Concept note-4: -You can increase your productivity. By keeping organized, you will save time looking for things and will have more time to work on important tasks. As organization can improve the flow of communication between you and your team, you can also make your team more productive.

Concept note-5: -In a full block business letter, every component of the letter (heading, address, salutation, body, salutation, signature, identification, enclosures) is aligned to the left. Also, first sentences of paragraphs are not indented.
