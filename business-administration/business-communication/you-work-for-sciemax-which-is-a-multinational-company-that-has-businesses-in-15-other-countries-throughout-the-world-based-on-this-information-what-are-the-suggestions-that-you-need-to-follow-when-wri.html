You work for Sciemax which is a multinational company that has businesses in 15 other countries throughout the world. Based on this information, what are the suggestions that you need to follow when writing to your colleagues?

(A) Use idioms

(B) Use slang words

(C) ** Avoid metaphors

(D) ** Use action-specific verbs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Run, walk, jump, talk, sing, speak, eat, drink, cry, skip, pull, push, fetch, give, make, bake, try, bring, teach, study, etc. are some examples of action verbs.

Concept note-2: -A verb is a word, or group of words, which indicates an action or a state of being. Every sentence must have a verb.

Concept note-3: -It is important that you spend some time to read the question carefully before answering your assessment as your answer may completely change if you misunderstand the action verb used within the question.

Concept note-4: -To determine if a word is an action verb, look at the sentence and ask yourself if the word shows something someone can do or something someone can be or feel. If it is something they can do, then it is an action verb (if it is something they can be or feel, it is a non-action, or stative, verb).
