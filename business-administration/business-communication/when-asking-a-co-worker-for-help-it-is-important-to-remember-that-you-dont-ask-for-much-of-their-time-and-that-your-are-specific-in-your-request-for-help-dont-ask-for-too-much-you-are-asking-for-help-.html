BUSINESS COMMUNICATION

[SOURCES]
When asking a co-worker for help it is important to remember that you don’t ask for much of their time and that your are specific in your request for help; don’t ask for too much. You are asking for help not for them to do your job.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -– It helps us be more vulnerable and authentic. When we ask for help, we put ourselves out there. Being vulnerable allows us to connect with others, and it is important for us to embrace it. But many of us are afraid to do this because vulnerability carries a sense of risk and uncertainty.

Concept note-2: -Do not approach management right away. Instead, follow business etiquette rules and have an honest conversation with your offending colleague. Let them know that their work habits are affecting you and you’re not able to do your job properly. Also, do not approach them when you’re angry.
