EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is the quickest way to get feedback immediately.

Concept note-2: -Interpersonal communication has the facility of getting quick and significant feedback. The face-to-face nature of this communication makes it dynamic in nature, e and instant feedback from the receiver also happens. The notification for the MH SET (Maharashtra State Eligibility Test) released on 10th November 2022.

Concept note-3: -Instant feedback is when information is provided contextually and “on-demand”, in immediate response to a learners’ action and in the flow of learning. It helps a learner deepen their understanding.

Concept note-4: -A disadvantage of written communication is lack of feedback. Oral communication allows a receiver to respond instantly to the sender with feedback. Written communication doesn’t have a built-in feedback mechanism, and because of that feedback can arrive too late for appropriate action.
