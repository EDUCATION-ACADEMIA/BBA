Which part of a Social Studies report will list all of the sources?

(A) Table of contents

(B) Title page

(C) ** Work Cited

(D) Report body

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In MLA style, the list of Works Cited (also known as a reference list or bibliography) appears at the end of your paper. It gives full details of every source that you cited in an MLA in-text citation. Like the rest of an MLA format paper, the Works Cited should be left-aligned and double-spaced with 1-inch margins.

Concept note-2: -In the body of a paper, the in-text citation acknowledges the source of information used. At the end of a paper, the citations are compiled on a References or Works Cited list. A basic citation includes the author, title, and publication information of the source.
