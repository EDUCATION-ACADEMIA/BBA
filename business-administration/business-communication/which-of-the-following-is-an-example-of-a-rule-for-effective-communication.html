Which of the following is an example of a rule for effective communication:

(A) The message must be clear

(B) Sender must deliver the message clearly and concisely

(C) Receiver must be able to hear and receive the message

(D) ** all the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The characteristics of effective communication are as follows: Clarity of Purpose: The message to be delivered must be clear in the mind of sender. The person to whom it is targeted and the aim of the message should be clear in the mind of the sender. Completeness: The message delivered should not be incomplete.

Concept note-2: -Effective communication examples can be stated as active listening, giving and taking feedback, empathy, and respectfulness, responding to messages, having volume and clarity in messages, understanding non-verbal data, building friendliness and confidence, adapting your communication style to the audience, and so on.
