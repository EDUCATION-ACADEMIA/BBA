Benefits of using visual communication

(A) ** To improve communication effectiveness

(B) To monitor and control information

(C) ** To improve audience perceptions of the presenter

(D) To document work performed for a client

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Advantages of Visual Communication Visual aids can deliver information more directly. Visual communication is more flexible than verbal communication. Visual aids are more attention-grabbing and engaging. Research has been able to establish that visual communication makes an impact on the audience.

Concept note-2: -Visual communication is a way to communicate ideas graphically in ways that are efficient and help to convey more meaning. It’s a critical element of any content marketing strategy. This is because visuals can help to evoke emotions in your audience, provide stronger examples for your message and so much more.

Concept note-3: -Visuals not only help your audience retain information-they ensure they’re more likely to recall your brand. This is vital when it comes to ‘warming up’ an audience, an essential part of the conversion process as people are very unlikely to buy from a brand they’ve never heard of before.

Concept note-4: -Your visuals play a role in your communication and should help to move your audience closer to your message or call to action by creating excitement, highlight a message, overcome language barriers, or clarify complex information.
