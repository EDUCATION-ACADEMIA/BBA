what does non verbal language include

(A) talking to people

(B) shouting at people

(C) ** using gestures and body language

(D) singing with the children

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All of your nonverbal behaviors-the gestures you make, your posture, your tone of voice, how much eye contact you make-send strong messages. They can put people at ease, build trust, and draw others towards you, or they can offend, confuse, and undermine what youâ€™re trying to convey.

Concept note-2: -Nonverbal communication is the transfer of information through body language, facial expressions, gestures, created space and more. For example, smiling when you meet someone conveys friendliness, acceptance and openness.

Concept note-3: -Waving to a friend, pointing to a restaurant menu item, and indicating how many apples you want by holding up three fingers are all examples of gestural non-verbal communication.

Concept note-4: -A gesture is a form of non-verbal communication in which visible bodily actions communicate particular messages, either in place of speech or together and in parallel with spoken words. Gestures include movement of the hands, face, or other parts of the body.

Concept note-5: -Gestures are overt and observable movements or actions using various parts of the body. They may involve the use of a single body part like the wagging of a finger or a nod, or multiple body parts at the same time, like gesticulating with both hands or using the hand and the head together.
