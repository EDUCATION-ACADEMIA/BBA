In a block style letter ____

(A) ** all lines start at left margin

(B) date and closing lines start at horizontal center

(C) paragraphs must be indented

(D) the complimentary closing is omitted

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Full block letter format is a type of business letter format where all lines begin on the left margin. This means that no components of the letter appear in the centre of a line, are indented, or start from the right margin.

Concept note-2: -Proper margins for a business letter in block format are typically two inches at the top and bottom and one inch on the left and right sides.

Concept note-3: -In a block style letter, all lines begin at the left margin. An enclosure notation is keyed, in all capital letters, a double space below the date line of a business a letter. A mailing notation is keyed, in all capital letters, a double space below the date line of a business a letter.

Concept note-4: -(i) Block Format: In block format, all the letter parts or elements such as date, address, salutation, paragraphs and close, all start from the left hand margin.

Concept note-5: -Answer. Block format is typically used for business letters. In block format, the entire text is left aligned and single spaced. The exception to the single spacing is a double space between paragraphs (instead of indents for paragraphs).
