On an annual basis, the human resources department of your company revises and sends out a list of ethical guidelines that its employees are expected to follow. What is the name of this document?

(A) ethical audit

(B) ethical dilemma list

(C) code of ethical dilemmas

(D) ** code of ethics

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which of the following are effective guidelines for achieving a natural writing style that is easy to read? Use short, simple words.

Concept note-2: -What is the key to ensuring that a message will include the right amount of information? Keep asking yourself what information will be necessary to achieve the purpose of the message.

Concept note-3: -Business communication is the process of sharing information between people within the workplace and outside a company. Effective business communication is how employees and management interact to reach organizational goals. Its purpose is to improve organizational practices and reduce errors.

Concept note-4: -Clarity of thought, conciseness, correct grammar and sentence structure, and simple language characterize effective business writing.
