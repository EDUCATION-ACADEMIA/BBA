BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

BUSINESS COMMUNICATION

[SOURCES]
To be able to use effective communication, we must learn..

(A) Video, Audio and volume

(B) ** Pronunciation, Voice Quality and Pitch

(C) Voice Quality, Pitch and Audio

(D) Audio, Rate and Pitch

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Your pitch is a powerful part of tone of voice in communication because it helps you cut through noise. When you’re needing to make a professional impression or drive in a point, a low pitch can make you seem more authoritative and serious, even if what you’re actually saying isn’t profound.

Concept note-2: -Vocal qualities include: tone, word emphasis, speed and volume. You’ve heard people use tone to help make their point.

Concept note-3: -pitch, in speech, the relative highness or lowness of a tone as perceived by the ear, which depends on the number of vibrations per second produced by the vocal cords. Pitch is the main acoustic correlate of tone and intonation (qq. v.).

Concept note-4: -Vary your vocal tone Speaking in a monotone voice is a surefire way to bore your audience and show you’re disengaged. Instead, use voice inflection to add emphasis to important points, and vary the pitch of your voice to express emotion. This will help keep your audience engaged in your message.
