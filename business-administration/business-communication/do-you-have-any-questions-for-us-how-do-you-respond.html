BUSINESS COMMUNICATION

[SOURCES]
“Do you have any questions for us?” How do you respond?

(A) No, thank you!

(B) Yes, who else are you interviewing?

(C) ** Yes, can you talk about the culture of the company?

(D) Yes, would I have to deal with the public?

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Interviewers often save this query for the close of the interview. You’ll want to ask questions that haven’t been answered during the interview already. Stay away from “yes” or “no” questions, as well as queries that you could answer with a quick online search. Instead, try asking questions about the role.

Concept note-2: -The easiest way to know about culture fit is to ask directly. While the supporting questions will tell you how the company is living its values and how a particular manager fits in the culture, the best way to know about what the office is really going to feel like is to be straightforward.
