The purposes of business communication are ____

(A) observe

(B) ** control

(C) ** balancing needs and goals

(D) ** motivation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Its main purpose is to improve organizational practices, eliminate silos, keep employees informed and reduce errors. Effective business communication is essential for the success and growth of every organization. Unlike everyday communications, business communication is always goal-oriented.

Concept note-2: -Business communication is exchanging information between employees and those outside the organization. Employees and management interact with one another through effective communication to accomplish organizational goals. It aims to reduce errors and enhance organizational procedures.

Concept note-3: -After all, motivation boosts productivity, workplace morale, and reduces turnover. Thatâ€™s a win-win-win if you ask. One of the most overlooked ways to motivate your team is through communication. Through effective communication your employees feel more empowered.

Concept note-4: -Effective communication between employees at all levels strengthens relationships and ensures everyone is on the same page. This creates a positive working environment, and improves both employee motivation and productivity, too.

Concept note-5: -Communication serves five major purposes: to inform, to express feelings, to imagine, to influence, and to meet social expectations. Each of these purposes is reflected in a form of communication.
