In a recent workshop conducted by Sarah, many of the audience left the venue feeling confused and frustrated. They complained to her boss how they wasted two hours being unproductive. What advice would you give Sarah to improve her presentations especially her visuals?

(A) Limit 20 lines per visual

(B) Have two ideas per visual

(C) ** Use numbers or bullet points

(D) Read and explain to the audience

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What are the 4 communication styles in business? The four communication archetypes are: Analytical, Intuitive, Functional, and Personal. Understanding the subtle differences between these various communication styles will help you to adjust and align communications at your company.

Concept note-2: -When communication occurs, it typically happens in one of three ways: verbal, nonverbal and visual.

Concept note-3: -There are five major types of communication styles: assertive, aggressive, passive, passive-aggressive, and manipulative. The assertive style is considered the most effective and healthy way to express yourself in a professional setting, no matter the circumstance.
