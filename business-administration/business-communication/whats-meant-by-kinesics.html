What’s meant by kinesics?

(A) ** the study of body language, which is not universal, but, instead, learned from one’s culture

(B) the study of cultural space requirements

(C) the study of how a culture perceives time and its use

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kinesics is the interpretation of body motion communication such as facial expressions and gestures, nonverbal behavior related to movement of any part of the body or the body as a whole.

Concept note-2: -Body language is universal because it conveys messages about our thoughts, emotions, and experiences everywhere in the world. However, most body language, specifically gestures, are culture-specific. For example, the American peace sign is similar to flipping someone off in England.

Concept note-3: -Kinesics: It is the use of gestures, postures and body language.

Concept note-4: -Although body language isn’t universal, the emotions behind it may be.
