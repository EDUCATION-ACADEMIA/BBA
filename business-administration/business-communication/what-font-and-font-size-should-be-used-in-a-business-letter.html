What font and font size should be used in a business letter?

(A) Arial, 14 point

(B) Courier New, 10 point

(C) Century Gothic, 16 point

(D) ** Times New Roman, 12 point

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The generally accepted font is Times New Roman, size 12, although other fonts such as Arial may be used.

Concept note-2: -APA recommends using the same font throughout your paper. Taft College library staff recommend using 12-point Times New Roman font. If not using Times New Roman, then another serif or sans serif typeface should be used for its readability.

Concept note-3: -At minimum, your legal documents should use font size 12. However, increasing the font size to 14 will make a huge difference in readability, especially when reading on a screen.
