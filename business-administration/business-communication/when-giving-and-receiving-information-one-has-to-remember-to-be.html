When giving and receiving information one has to remember to be?

(A) Spontaneous

(B) Special

(C) ** Specific

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If you are not careful, facts will be forgotten or distorted. This is because both the individual sending and the person receiving the information may unintentionally obscure the message.

Concept note-2: -Communication refers to the process by which the information is transmitted and understood between two or more people. Transmitting the sender’s intended meaning is the essence of effective communication. Communication involves two people – a sender and a receiver.
