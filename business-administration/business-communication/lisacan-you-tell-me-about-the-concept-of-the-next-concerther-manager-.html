Lisa:Can you tell me about the concept of the next concert?Her manager: ____

(A) That sounds interesting.

(B) Exactly.

(C) ** Yes, of course.

(D) Well, I’ll think about it.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indirect: She said (that) she was happy.

Concept note-2: -Reported speech or indirect speech is the form of speech used to convey what was said by someone at some point of time.

Concept note-3: -Direct: He said to me, “I will go there tomorrow.” Indirect: He told me that he would go there the next day.

Concept note-4: -When we want to report what someone said without speech marks and without necessarily using exactly the same words, we can use indirect speech (also called reported speech). For example: Direct speech: “We’re quite cold in here.” Indirect speech: They say (that) they’re cold.
