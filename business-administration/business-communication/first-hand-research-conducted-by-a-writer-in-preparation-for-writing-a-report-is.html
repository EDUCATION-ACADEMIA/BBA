First-hand research conducted by a writer in preparation for writing a report is

(A) data

(B) ** primary research

(C) secondary research

(D) representative sampling

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Primary research definition Primary research is data which is obtained first-hand. This means that the researcher conducts the research themselves or commissions the data to be collected on their behalf. Primary research means going directly to the source, rather than relying on pre-existing data samples.

Concept note-2: -Primary Research: This is research that is done by the author of the source you are using where that author conducted some method of research to gather new data that s/he then reports, analyzes and interprets in that source.

Concept note-3: -Primary research or a primary study refers to a research article that is an authorâ€™s original research that is almost always published in a peer-reviewed journal. A primary study reports on the details, methods and results of a research study.

Concept note-4: -A primary source is a first-hand or contemporary account of an event or topic. They are the most direct evidence of a time or event because they were created by people or things that were there at the time or event. These sources have not been modified by interpretation and offer original thought or new information.
