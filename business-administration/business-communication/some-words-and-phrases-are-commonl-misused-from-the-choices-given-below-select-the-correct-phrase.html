Some words and phrases are commonl misused. From the choices given below, select the correct phrase.

(A) had less errors

(B) ** had fewer errors

(C) had lesser errors

(D) none of these choices

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It’s incorrect for one to say, “I always sleep late”, when they actually mean to say “It’s always late, when I go to sleep” or “I always go to bed late”. “I always sleep late” in fact means “I always sleep for a long time” (meaning you don’t get up until the late morning and early afternoon).

Concept note-2: -A phrase is a group of words that works together in a sentence but does not contain a subject or a verb. Often phrases are used for descriptions of people, things, or events. Examples: Filled with joy, the girl jumped up and down.
