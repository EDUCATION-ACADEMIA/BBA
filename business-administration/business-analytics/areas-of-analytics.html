Concept note-1: -There are four types of analytics, Descriptive, Diagnostic, Predictive, and Prescriptive.

Concept note-2: -Supply chain predictive analytics use historical data and statistical models to forecast future supply chain performance, demand, and potential disruptions. This helps businesses proactively identify and address risks, optimize resources and processes, and improve decision-making.

Concept note-3: -There are three types of analytics that businesses use to drive their decision making; descriptive analytics, which tell us what has already happened; predictive analytics, which show us what could happen, and finally, prescriptive analytics, which inform us what should happen in the future.
