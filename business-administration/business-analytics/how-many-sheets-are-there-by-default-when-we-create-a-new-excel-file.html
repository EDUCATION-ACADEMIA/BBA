How many sheets are there, by default, when we create a new Excel file?

(A) 1

(B) ** 3

(C) 5

(D) 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By default, a new workbook contains three worksheets, but you can change the number of worksheets that you want a new workbook to contain.

Concept note-2: -When you open an Excel workbook, there are three sheets by default, and the default name on the tabs are Sheet1, Sheet2, and Sheet3.
