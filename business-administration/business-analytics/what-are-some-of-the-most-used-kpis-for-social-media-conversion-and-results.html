What are some of the most used KPIs for social media conversion and results?

(A) ** CPL, ROI, CPA

(B) CPC, EPC, CJM

(C) CPM, TO, VPP

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Focus area #1: engagement. Engagement is, hands down, the #1 area that you should be concerned with on social media. It is the catalyst for improvement in all of the other social media KPIs weâ€™ll discuss. Simply put, engagement measures the amount of likes, shares, and comments that your social updates receive.

Concept note-2: -Community Growth KPIs New Subscribers: Number of new followers on your social media page within a period. Other similar KPIs include Fan Adds and New Followers. Total Subscribers: Total number of subscribers you attract within a period. Other similar KPIs include Page Likes and Total Followers.

Concept note-3: -KPIs tell you what happens after each chapter, whereas ROI tells you what happened after the conclusion of the entire story. KPIs are a forward-looking predictor of end performance, whereas ROI is used as a backward-looking informer of future budget allocation decisions.
