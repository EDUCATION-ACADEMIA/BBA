If the forecasted value of the time series variable for period 2 is 22.5 and the actual value observed for period 2 is 25, what is the forecast error in period 2?

(A) 3

(B) 2

(C) ** 2.5

(D) -2.5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Forecast error is the amount by which the predicted value differs from the observed value of the time series variable.

Concept note-2: -Explanation: In statistics, a forecast error is the difference between the actual or real and the predicted or forecast value of a time series or any other phenomenon of interest.

Concept note-3: -variance is NOT a time series component, it refers to the spread of a data set.
