EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Based on the data in the table, the relative frequency for Devon Pride is 0.12.

Concept note-2: -Relative frequency or experimental probability is calculated from the number of times an event happens, divided by the total number of trials in an actual experiment.

Concept note-3: -Example 1: A die is tossed 40 times and lands 6 times on the number 4. What is the relative frequency of observing the die land on the number 4? Example 2: A coin is tossed 20 times and lands 15 time on heads. What is the relative frequency of observing the coin land on heads?

Concept note-4: -The relative frequency of an event is defined as the number of times that the event occurs during experimental trials, divided by the total number of trials conducted. The relative frequency is not a theoretical quantity, but an experimental one.
