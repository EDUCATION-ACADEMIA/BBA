The 4 V’s of Big Data are:

(A) Volume, Velocity, Varsity, Veracity

(B) Volume, Veracity, Visionary, Variety

(C) Variety, Velocity, Vision, Volume

(D) ** Variety, Velocity, Volume, Veracity

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 5 V’s of big data (velocity, volume, value, variety and veracity) are the five main and innate characteristics of big data. Knowing the 5 V’s allows data scientists to derive more value from their data while also allowing the scientists’ organization to become more customer-centric.
