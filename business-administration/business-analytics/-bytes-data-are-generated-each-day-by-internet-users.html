____ bytes data are generated each day by internet users.

(A) 3.5 quadrillion

(B) ** 2.5 quintillion

(C) 1.5 million

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The world generates 2.5 quintillion bytes per day. That’s 1, 000 petabytes!

Concept note-2: -2.5 quintillion bytes of data generated everyday.

Concept note-3: -Do you know that 2.5 quintillion bytes of data in Gb are 2500000000 gigabytes? This data is unfathomably large. Every minute, People send about 42 million WhatsApp messages.

Concept note-4: -An exabyte is the equivalent of one quintillion bytes, one billion gigabytes or one million terabytes (TB). In context with other units of digital data and storage: 8 bits equals one byte. 1, 024 bytes equal one kilobyte (KB)

Concept note-5: -So 2.5 quintillion would equal Bill Gates’s projected fortune times 2.5 million, assuming he lives to see 2042. Or, it’s 250 million human brains (counted in neurons).
