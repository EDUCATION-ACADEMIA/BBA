The moving averages method refers to a forecasting method that ____

(A) is used when considerable trend, cyclical, or seasonal effects are present

(B) uses regression relationship based on past time series values to predict the future time series values

(C) relates a time series to other variables that are believed to explain or cause its behavior

(D) ** uses the average of the most recent data values in the time series as the forecast for the next period

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The moving average method uses the average of the most recent n data values in the time series as the forecast for the next period. Note that the n past observations are equally weighted. Issues with moving average forecast: All n past observations treated equally.

Concept note-2: -A forecasting technique referred to as moving averages uses the average or mean of the most recent n periods to forecast the next value for time series data. With a three-period moving average, the most recent three periods of data are used in the forecast computation.

Concept note-3: -A moving average is a technique that calculates the overall trend in a data set. In operations management, the data set is sales volume from historical data of the company. This technique is very useful for forecasting short-term trends. It is simply the average of a select set of time periods.

Concept note-4: -The method that uses the average of the most recent k data values in the time series as the forecast for the next period is called: weighted moving averages.
