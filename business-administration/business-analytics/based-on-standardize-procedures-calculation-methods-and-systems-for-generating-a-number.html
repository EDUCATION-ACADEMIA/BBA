EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Standard Metrics are Metrics that only store and display 1 Metric Value for each Date Range. Examples of Standard Metrics are “Sessions, ” “Clicks, ” and “Likes."

Concept note-2: -What is process standardization? Process standardization refers to the organization of different tasks, approaches, and administrative steps in a business, as well as their formalization and documentation. A process consists of input, transformation steps, and output (delivery).

Concept note-3: -In statistics, standardization is the process of putting different variables on the same scale. This process allows you to compare scores between different types of variables. Typically, to standardize variables, you calculate the mean and standard deviation for a variable.

Concept note-4: -Standardization can be applied to any process or procedure or task that is important for the business. Payroll processing, invoice processing, answering phone calls, gathering client information, etc. are some standardization examples. Each operation comes with its own approved standards.
