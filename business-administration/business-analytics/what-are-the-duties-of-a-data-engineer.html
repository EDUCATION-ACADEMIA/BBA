What are the duties of a data engineer?

(A) ** Data Collection & Storage

(B) Data Exploration & Visualization

(C) Data Preparation

(D) Data Experimentation & Prediction

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Data engineers are responsible for designing, maintaining, and optimizing data infrastructure for data collection, management, transformation, and access. They are in charge of creating pipelines that convert raw data into usable formats for data scientists and other data consumers to utilize.

Concept note-2: -A data engineer is responsible for collecting, managing, and converting raw data into information that can be interpreted by data scientists and business analysts. Data accessibility is their ultimate goal, which is to enable organizations to utilize data for performance evaluation and optimization.

Concept note-3: -Data engineering uses tools like SQL and Python to make data ready for data scientists. Data engineering works with data scientists to understand their specific needs for a job. They build data pipelines that source and transform the data into the structures needed for analysis.

Concept note-4: -To be successful in data engineering requires solid programming skills, statistics knowledge, analytical skills, and an understanding of big data technologies.
