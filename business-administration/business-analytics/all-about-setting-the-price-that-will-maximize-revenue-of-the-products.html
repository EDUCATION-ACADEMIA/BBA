(A) Price Elasticity

(C) Modern Logistic Service

(D) ** Price Level Optimization

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Penetration pricing. When you use a penetration pricing strategy, you initially charge low prices-usually lower than your competitors-then make gradual price increases as your market share grows. This helps you launch with a high volume of sales right away.

Concept note-2: -Price optimization is the practice of analyzing customer and market data to find the most optimal price point for a product or service. The goal of price optimization is to determine the best price that will help attract customers, maximize sales, and increase profits.

Concept note-3: -1. Price skimming. Skimming involves setting high prices when a product is introduced and then gradually lowering the price as more competitors enter the market.
