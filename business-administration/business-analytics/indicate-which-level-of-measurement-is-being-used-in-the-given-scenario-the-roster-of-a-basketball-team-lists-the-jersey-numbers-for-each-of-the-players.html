Indicate which level of measurement is being used in the given scenario. The roster of a basketball team lists the jersey numbers for each of the players.

(A) Nominal

(B) Ordinal

(C) ** Interval

(D) Ratio

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For example, jersey numbers in basketball are measures at the nominal level.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: The number on an athlete’s jersey is a nominal scale of measurement. So, the correct answer is a. nominal.

Concept note-3: -The roster of a basketball team lists the jersey numbers for each of the players. Even though there are numbers associated with this data set, the numbers serve as alternate forms of names for the players and the data is at the nominal level of measurement.

Concept note-4: -A. Cars described as compact, midsize, and full-size-it is an ordinal measurement, since the order of the category matters.

Concept note-5: -Ordinal Scale is defined as a variable measurement scale used to simply depict the order of variables and not the difference between each of the variables.
