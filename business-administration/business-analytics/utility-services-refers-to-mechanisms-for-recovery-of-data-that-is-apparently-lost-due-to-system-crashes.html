Utility Services refers to mechanisms for recovery of data that is apparently lost due to system crashes

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Utility Services refers to mechanisms for recovery of data that is apparently lost due to system crashes.

Concept note-2: -Data Independence – it maintains the segregation between the program and the data.

Concept note-3: -Atomicity is a property that ensures that a database follows the all or nothing rule. In other words, the database considers all transaction operations as one whole unit or atom. Thus, when a database processes a transaction, it is either fully completed or not executed at all.

Concept note-4: -Databases typically have one of two basic forms: single-file or flat file database. multi-file relational or structured database.
