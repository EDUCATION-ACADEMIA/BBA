Compute the mode for the following data. 12, 16, 19, 10, 12, 11, 21, 12, 21, 10

(A) 21

(B) 11

(C) ** 12

(D) 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As, mode is the most frequently occurring value. Therefore, mode is 10.

Concept note-2: -From given data we see that 14 occurs maximum numbers of times so 14 is the mode of the given data. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-3: -Solution. The highest frequency is 10 which corresponds to the value 15. Hence mode of this data is 15.

Concept note-4: -Summary: Both mode and median of the data: 13, 16, 12, 14, 19, 12, 14, 13, 14 is equal to 14.
