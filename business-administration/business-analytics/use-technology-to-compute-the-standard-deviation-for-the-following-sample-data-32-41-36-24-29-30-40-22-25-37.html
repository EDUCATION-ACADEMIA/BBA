Use technology to compute the standard deviation for the following sample data. 32, 41, 36, 24, 29, 30, 40, 22, 25, 37

(A) 5.96

(B) 6.41

(C) 5.42

(D) ** 6.75

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Given data: 10, 28, 13, 18, 29, 30, 22, 23, 25, 32. Hence, ∑xi = 10 + 28 + 13 + 18 + 29 + 30 + 22 + 23 + 25 + 32 = 230. Hence, Mean, = 230/10 = 23. Hence, the standard deviation is 7.

Concept note-2: -The standard deviation formula is, = √ ∑i=1n (xi – x̅)2 / N.

Concept note-3: -To calculate the population standard deviation, we divide the sum by the number of data points (N). But to calculate the sample deviation, the total is divided by the number of data points minus 1 (N-1). Find the square root of the final figure to determine the standard deviation.
