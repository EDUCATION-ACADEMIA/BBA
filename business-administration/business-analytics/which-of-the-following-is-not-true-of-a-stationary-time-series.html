Which of the following is not true of a stationary time series?

(A) The process generating the data has a constant mean.

(B) ** The time series plot is a straight line.

(C) The statistical properties are independent of time.

(D) The variability is constant over time.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The mean of the residuals is always zero.

Concept note-2: -variance is NOT a time series component, it refers to the spread of a data set.

Concept note-3: -A common assumption in many time series techniques is that the data are stationary. A stationary process has the property that the mean, variance and autocorrelation structure do not change over time.

Concept note-4: -A stationary time series is one whose properties do not depend on the time at which the series is observed. Thus, time series with trends, or with seasonality, are not stationary-the trend and seasonality will affect the value of the time series at different times.
