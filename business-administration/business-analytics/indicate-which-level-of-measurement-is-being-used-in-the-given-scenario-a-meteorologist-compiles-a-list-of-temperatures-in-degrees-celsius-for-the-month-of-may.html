Indicate which level of measurement is being used in the given scenario. A meteorologist compiles a list of temperatures in degrees Celsius for the month of May.

(A) nominal

(B) ordinal

(C) ratio

(D) ** Interval

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A meteorologist keeps track of the time of day that the sun sets. What is the level of measurement of the data? Interval [This is interval data because time of day is a numerical scale where differences are meaningful.
