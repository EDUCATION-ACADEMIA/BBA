It is the collected facts and figures.

(A) ** DATA

(B) DATABASE

(C) INFORMATION

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Data is a collection of facts and figures which include numbers, words, measurements and observations for a definite purpose. The purpose is usually to find a correlation between two events, one of which is called the “cause” and the other, “effect".

Concept note-2: -A collection of facts, such as values or observations is called as Data. Data can be descriptive in many ways, for e.g., it can be numbers, words, measurements, observations or even just descriptions of things. Facts and figures, collected with a definite purpose, are called data.

Concept note-3: -The Raw Facts and Figures are Called Data. The word raw means that the facts have not yet been processed to get their exact meaning. Data is collected from different sources. It is collected for different purposes. Raw data (sometimes called source data or atomic data) is data that has not been processed for use.

Concept note-4: -Data is defined as a collection of individual facts or statistics. (While “datum” is technically the singular form of “data, ” it’s not commonly used in everyday language.) Data can come in the form of text, observations, figures, images, numbers, graphs, or symbols.

Concept note-5: -These facts or figures, which are numerical or otherwise, collected with a definite purpose are called data. Data is the plural form of the Latin word datum.
