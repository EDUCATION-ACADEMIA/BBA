(A) Globalisation

(B) ** Liberalisation

(C) Privatisation

(D) All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Liberalization refers to relaxation of government restrictions usually in areas of social and economic policies. It refers to the process of eliminating unnecessary controls and restrictions on the smooth functioning of business enterprises.

Concept note-2: -Relaxing the restrictions and controls imposed on business and industry means liberalisation.
