Legal support services can assist a business with:

(A) ** Drawing up contracts.

(B) ** Legal representation in court cases.

(C) ** Advising businesses on changes in the law.

(D) Putting contracts aside and ignoring them

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A legal advisor holds a specialization in the particular areas of law. They can manage your issues related to contract disputes, compensation issues, workplace conflicts etc. They also give an expert advice in the matters related corporate decisions, mergers and acquisitions, corporate growth or other practices.

Concept note-2: -Legal advisors provide clients or organizations with advice, guidance, and support related to a range of legal issues or concerns. They have an active role in preparing and reviewing contracts and other legal agreements or documents. They also guide or manage contract negotiations.

Concept note-3: -They play a very decisive role and acts as a bridge between the needy, poor and the courts. They are the link which shows the poor the way that how can they attain justice from the court; it includes providing counsel, paying of court fees and fulfilling other court formalities by the authorities.
