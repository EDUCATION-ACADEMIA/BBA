TRUE OR FALSE:A terrorist attack in a shopping mall is likely to affect many business.

(A) ** TRUE

(B) FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The findings of this study reveal that stock market returns are negatively affected by the severity of attacks, terrorist assaults in capital city, and attacks within close proximity of capital city.

Concept note-2: -Terrorist acts can cause ripple effects through the economy that have negative impacts. The most obvious is the direct economic destruction of property and lives. Terrorism indirectly affects the economy by creating market uncertainty, xenophobia, loss of tourism, and increased insurance claims.
