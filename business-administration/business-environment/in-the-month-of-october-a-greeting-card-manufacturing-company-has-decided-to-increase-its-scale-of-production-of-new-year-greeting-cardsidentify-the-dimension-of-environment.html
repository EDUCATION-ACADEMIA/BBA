[SOURCES]
In the month of October a greeting card manufacturing company has decided to increase its scale of production of New Year Greeting Cards.Identify the dimension of environment.

(A) Economics Environment

(B) ** Social Environment

(C) Political Environment

(D) Legal Environment

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are 5 dimensions to business environment. These are economic, social, legal, political and technological.

Concept note-2: -Answer: The various features of business environment which are highlighted in the above case are: Specific and general forces.

Concept note-3: -Dimensions of the business environment include economic, social, technological, political and legal conditions which are considered relevant for decision-making and improving the performance of an enterprise. Economic Environment comprises of factors that can affect management practices ina business enterprise.

Concept note-4: -Relativity: Business environment is a relative concept whose impact differs from country to country, region to region and firm to firm. For example, a shift of preference from soft drinks to juices will be welcomed as an opportunity by juice making companies while a threat to soft drink manufacturers.
