Ease of Doing Business Report is prepared by which of the following organisation?

(A) United Nations Organisation (UNO)

(B) South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation (SAARC)

(C) ** World Bank

(D) Association of South East Asian Nations (ASEAN)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Definition: Ease of doing business is an index published by the World Bank. It is an aggregate figure that includes different parameters which define the ease of doing business in a country.

Concept note-2: -The World Bank announced in December 2022 that it would be releasing the methodology for the replacement to the index in the second quarter of 2023.

Concept note-3: -The Ease of Doing Business (EoDB) index is a ranking system established by the World Bank Group. In the EODB index, ‘higher rankings’ (a lower numerical value) indicate better, usually simpler, regulations for businesses and stronger protections of property rights.

Concept note-4: -It is published by World Bank Group.

Concept note-5: -What is the Ease of Doing Business index? It is an index designed by the World Bank to rank 190 economies. A higher rank (closer to 1) means the country’s regulatory environment is favorable to business operations.
