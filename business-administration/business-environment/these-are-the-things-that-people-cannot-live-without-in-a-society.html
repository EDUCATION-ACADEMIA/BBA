[SOURCES]
These are the things that people cannot live without in a society.

(A) Wants

(B) Desires

(C) Requirements

(D) ** Needs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Food and water, mobile phone, money is my three main necessities that we cannot live without. First and foremost, food and water are the basic need and essential part of living.

Concept note-2: -Food, water, clothing, sleep, and shelter are the bare necessities for anyone’s survival. For many people, these basic needs can not be met without the aid of charitable organizations. A reliable place to receive a meal can be what’s needed for a person to focus on obtaining higher needs.

Concept note-3: -indispensable Add to list Share. Indispensable is a strong adjective for something that you couldn’t do without. If you have asthma and you’re packing for summer vacation, your inhaler is indispensable, unless you enjoy gasping on the beach.

Concept note-4: -The three things with which we cannot live are food, water, and air.
