Which of the following is not an impact of economic policy changes?

(A) Enhanced competition

(B) ** Static technology

(C) Market orientation

(D) Necessity for change

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Economic policies are fiscal policies, Industrial policies, taxation policy, exchange rates, EXIM policy but not technological policy.

Concept note-2: -Reduction in physical restrictions on imports and import duties. Reduction in controls on foreign exchange, both current and capital account. Reform of financial system. Reduction in levels of personal and corporate taxation.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Centralization.

Concept note-4: -The factors of production are resources that are the building blocks of the economy; they are what people use to produce goods and services. Economists divide the factors of production into four categories: land, labor, capital, and entrepreneurship.
