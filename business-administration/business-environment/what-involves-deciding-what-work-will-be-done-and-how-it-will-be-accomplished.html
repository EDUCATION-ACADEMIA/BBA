What involves deciding what work will be done and how it will be accomplished?

(A) Organizing

(B) ** Planning

(C) Staffing

(D) Directing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Planning means looking ahead and chalking out future courses of action to be followed. It is a preparatory step. It is a systematic activity which determines when, how and who is going to perform a specific job.

Concept note-2: -Planning is the process we use to select goals and determine how to achieve them. It arranges the use of our resources in an orderly, economical, and goal-accomplishing manner. Itâ€™s also a description of what we want to accomplish in the future and establishes some type of agreement on the means for achieving it.

Concept note-3: -The steps involved in the planning process are as follows: Developing of objectives. Developing tasks that are required to meet those objectives. Determining resources needed to implement those tasks.

Concept note-4: -Planning is the function of management that involves setting objectives and determining a course of action for achieving those objectives.
