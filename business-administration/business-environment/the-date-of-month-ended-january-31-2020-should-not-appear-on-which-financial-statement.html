The date of “Month Ended January 31, 2020” should NOT appear on which financial statement?

(A) Income Statement

(B) ** Balance Sheet

(C) Statement of Owner’s Equity

(D) Statement of Cash Flows

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The month-end close process includes reviewing the company’s balance sheet, intercompany trades, journal entries, and other documents (like bank statements, income, and expenses) and reconciling them. Month-end close is a systematic process of maintaining a record of the business’s financial activities.

Concept note-2: -Off-balance sheet (OBS) assets are assets that don’t appear on the balance sheet. OBS assets can be used to shelter financial statements from asset ownership and related debt. Common OBS assets include accounts receivable, leaseback agreements, and operating leases.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: The correct option is (c) balance sheet. The balance sheet shows the balances of accounts at a particular date and not for a period of time.

Concept note-4: -If a company has a fiscal year-end that is the same as the calendar year-end, it means that the fiscal year ends on Dec. 31.
