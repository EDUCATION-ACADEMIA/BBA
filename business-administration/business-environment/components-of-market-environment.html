EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Competition: The rivalry between companies selling similar products and services. Direct competitors: Companies who offer the same products and services aimed at the same target market and customer base. Indirect competitors: A company that offers the same products and services, but the end goals are different.

Concept note-2: -It is made up of six components: demographic, economic, physical, technological, political-legal, and social-cultural environment.
