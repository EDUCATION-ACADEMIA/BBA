(A) Economic, Technological and Legal

(B) Social, Political and Legal

(C) ** Economic, Technological and Social

(D) Social, Technological and Political

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: (c) It helps in improving performance. Concept note-2: -Electric vehicles with zero tailpipe emissions are emerging as a good alternative to problems like climate change, surging pollution leading to ill health, crude import bill, and energy security.

Concept note-3: -Fuel Cell Electric Vehicle(FCEV): FCEVs are also known as Zero-Emission Vehicles. They employ ‘fuel cell technology’ to generate the electricity required to run the vehicle. The chemical energy of the fuel is converted directly into electric energy.

Concept note-4: -Advantages and Disadvantages of Electric Vehicles There are no local emissions in the streets. Energy costs are low, due to the high drivetrain efficiency.

Concept note-5: -Eco-friendly savings on CO2 emissions: Shifting to electric mobility will help India save nearly one giga tonne of carbon dioxide emissions by 2030. Each electric car on the road helps in reducing the harmful air pollution for the younger generations to come.
