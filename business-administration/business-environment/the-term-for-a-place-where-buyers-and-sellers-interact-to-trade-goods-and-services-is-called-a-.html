The term for a place where buyers and sellers interact to trade goods and services is called a ____

(A) shop

(B) ** market

(C) store

(D) a place of exchange

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct option is A Market. Market is a location, more like a physical place, where buyers and sellers meet to exchange their goods and services. In economics, the term ‘market’ does not refer to a particular place as such, but it refers to shops for one commodity or a set of commodities.

Concept note-2: -A market is a place where buyers and sellers can meet to facilitate the exchange or transaction of goods and services.

Concept note-3: -The factor market definition in economics is where the means of production, or resources to make goods and services, are bought and sold. Factor market can also be called a resource market.

Concept note-4: -Bartering is the exchange of goods and services between two or more parties without the use of money. It is the oldest form of commerce. Individuals and companies barter goods and services between each other based on equivalent estimates of prices and goods.

Concept note-5: -Economic market structures can be grouped into four categories: perfect competition, monopolistic competition, oligopoly, and monopoly.
