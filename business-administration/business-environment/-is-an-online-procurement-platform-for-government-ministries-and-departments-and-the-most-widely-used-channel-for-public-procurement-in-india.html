____ is an online procurement platform for government ministries and departments, and the most widely used channel for public procurement in India.

(A) Government e Manufacturing Plan

(B) Government e Model place

(C) ** Government e Marketplace

(D) Government e Manufacturing place

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Government e Marketplace (GeM) National Public Procurement Portal, Government of India.

Concept note-2: -GeM has been conceived as a platform for the sale and purchase of commonly used goods and services.

Concept note-3: -Government created one stop Government e-Marketplace (GeM) to facilitate on line procurement of common use Goods & Services required by various Government Departments / Organizations / PSUs. GeM will enhance transparency, efficiency and speed in public procurement.
