There are many techniques that can be used when doing a business environmental report. Which of the following is not a valid technique?

(A) ** Economical analysis

(B) Industry analysis

(C) PESTLE/PEST analysis

(D) SWOT analysis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The two common types of environmental analysis methods are the PESTLE analysis and SWOT analysis. These approaches help organizations assess their strategic positions based on a wide range of internal and external factors.

Concept note-2: -SWOT Analysis SWOT analysis stands for strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats analysis of a business environment. Strengths and weaknesses are an organizationâ€™s internal factor while threats and opportunities are considered as external factors.

Concept note-3: -In the process of doing business there, both threats and opportunities arise for the development of the organization. To detect them, the following methods of analysis are widely used: SWOT analysis, PEST analysis, GRID analysis, SCAN analysis, STEP analysis, ETOM analysis, EFAS analysis, SNW analysis.
