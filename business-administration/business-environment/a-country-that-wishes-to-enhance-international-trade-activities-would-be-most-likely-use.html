(A) a tariff

(B) ** a common market

(C) an embargo

(D) a quota

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which of the following would most likely promote international trade? C is correct. Improvements in transportation, including faster transport, help international trade.

Concept note-3: -International trade allows countries to expand their markets and access goods and services that otherwise may not have been available domestically. As a result of international trade, the market is more competitive. This ultimately results in more competitive pricing and brings a cheaper product home to the consumer.

Concept note-4: -For the United States, the main goal of trade agreements is to reduce barriers to U.S. exports, protect U.S. interests competing abroad, and enhance the rule of law in the FTA partner country or countries. Currently, the United States has 14 FTAs with 20 countries.
