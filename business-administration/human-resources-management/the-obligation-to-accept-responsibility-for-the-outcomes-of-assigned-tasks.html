HUMAN RESOURCES MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
The obligation to accept responsibility for the outcomes of assigned tasks.

(A) ** Accountability

(B) Employee Empowerment

(C) Unity of Command

(D) Organization Chart

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Accountability is the obligation of an individual to report formally to his superior about the work he has done to discharge the responsibility, and every employee should be held accountable for jobs assigned to them and be able to complete that job as per the standards set by the superior.

Concept note-2: -Webster’s Dictionary says the definition of accountability is: “the obligation or willingness to accept responsibility for one’s actions."

Concept note-3: -Definition of Responsibility As per Davis, “Responsibility is an obligation of an individual to perform assigned duties to the best of his ability under the direction of his leader.” In the words of Theo Haimann, “Responsibility is the obligation of a subordinate to perform the duty as required by his superior”.

Concept note-4: -It is the obligation to complete an assigned task on time, and to the best of your ability. While authority does not automatically come with every job, task, or duty, responsibility arises in every single position in a company.

Concept note-5: -Responsibility – This is an obligation incurred by individuals in their roles in the formal organization effectively perform assignments or to work on the success of the project with or without guidance or authorization.
