Entrepreneurs must delegate responsibility, or the duty, for completing assigned tasks. When it comes to delegation, which of the following is a FALSE statement?

(A) Assigned tasks must be clearly identified so that everyone will know exactly what they are responsible for.

(B) There should be no questions about who is responsible for each task.

(C) Authority is delegated from the top of the small business to others at lower levels.

(D) ** One person really can do everything if they just work hard enough.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Successful delegation allows you to hand over tasks to others whose skills are better aligned to accomplish that specific task. Passing off tasks allows you the time to reflect, develop strategies and prepare for what is ahead.

Concept note-2: -What are my goals? Do I have the right strategy? Can I execute the strategy?

Concept note-3: -Getting your business funded is one of the main issues that all business face and have to tackle in order to survive.
