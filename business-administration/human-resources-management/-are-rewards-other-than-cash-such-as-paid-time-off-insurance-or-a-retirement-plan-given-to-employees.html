____ are rewards other than cash, such as paid time off, insurance, or a retirement plan, given to employees.

(A) salaries

(B) ** Benefits

(C) commissions

(D) policies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Benefits are a form of non-cash compensation, which your employer usually states in your contract. Benefits arenâ€™t linked to employee performance, unlike Rewards. Rewards are a motivational tool used to get the best from your employees. Benefits and rewards both have very different traits, but more on that later.

Concept note-2: -A monetary reward is a specific amount of money given to an employee to complete a task or goal. These rewards can come in the form of bonuses, commissions, or incentives and can be based on performance, merit, or seniority.

Concept note-3: -The cash rewards that are paid to the employees for their work is called direct compensation.
