HUMAN RESOURCES MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
Which of the following best describes the role of human resource management within a business?

(A) The management of financial resources

(B) The relationship between a business and its suppliers

(C) The development of a marketing plan

(D) ** The management of employees

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which of the following best describes HRM? The focus of human resource management (HRM) is on managing people within the employer-employee relationship.

Concept note-2: -Human Resources manages 5 main duties: talent management, compensation and employee benefits, training and development, compliance, and workplace safety. An HR department can help provide organizational structure and the ability to meet business needs by effectively managing the employee lifecycle.

Concept note-3: -Human resource management is the function of attracting, developing, and retaining employees who can perform the activities needed to meet organizational objectives.

Concept note-4: -Human resources (HR) is the division of a business responsible for finding, screening, recruiting, and training job applicants. HR also administers employee-benefit programs. A human resources department also handles compensation and benefits, and employee terminations.

Concept note-5: -Human resources managers plan, coordinate, and direct the administrative functions of an organization. They oversee the recruiting, interviewing, and hiring of new staff; consult with top executives on strategic planning; and serve as a link between an organizationâ€™s management and its employees.
