HUMAN RESOURCES MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
A detailed outline of the duties, qualifications, and conditions required to do a specific job is called a

(A) background check

(B) job application

(C) ** job description

(D) resume

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Job description is a broad, general, and written statement of a specific job, based on the findings of a job analysis. It generally includes duties, purpose, responsibilities, scope, and working conditions of a job along with the job’s title, and the name or designation of the person to whom the employee reports.

Concept note-2: -A job description contains the following components: job title, job purpose, job duties and responsibilities, required qualifications, preferred qualifications, and working conditions.

Concept note-3: -A job description is a useful, plain-language tool that explains the tasks, duties, function and responsibilities of a position. It details who performs a specific type of work, how that work is to be completed, and the frequency and the purpose of the work as it relates to the organization’s mission and goals.
