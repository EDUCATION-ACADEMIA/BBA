What is the definition of a wage?

(A) a fixed amount of pay for each week, month, or year

(B) extra compensation

(C) a profit sharing plan

(D) ** an amount of money paid on an hourly basis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Someone’s wages are the amount of money that is regularly paid to them for the work that they do. His wages have gone up. This may end efforts to set a minimum wage well above the poverty line. Synonyms: payment, pay, earnings, remuneration More Synonyms of wage.

Concept note-2: -An hourly wage is the amount an employee is paid per hour they work. A role that’s paid hourly doesn’t come with a set or target annual pay. Instead, an employer pays an employee based on how many hours they work each pay period, which might be a week, two weeks, half a month or a month.

Concept note-3: -Hourly Basis means compensation paid according to the number of hours that employee actually works.

Concept note-4: -/weɪdʒ/ us. (also wages [ plural ]) an amount of money that is paid, usually every week, to an employee for their work: a daily/hourly/weekly wage This deal represents an hourly wage rate of $9.83.

Concept note-5: -Salaried workers earn a preset sum each pay period that isn’t determined by how many hours they work each week. Hourly workers get paid a per-hour rate, so their paychecks are based specifically on the number of hours they work.
