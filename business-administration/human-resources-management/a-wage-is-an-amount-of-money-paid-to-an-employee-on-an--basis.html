[SOURCES]
A wage is an amount of money paid to an employee on a(n) ____ basis

(A) ** hourly

(B) weekly

(C) monthly

(D) yearly

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Salary is generally paid at fixed intervals i.e. monthly. Whereas wages are paid on a daily basis for the number of hours spent. Salary is paid on the basis of the performance of an individual. Whereas wages are paid on hourly basis i.e. the amount of work done in hours.

Concept note-2: -Hourly Basis means compensation paid according to the number of hours that employee actually works.

Concept note-3: -Wages are a form of monetary compensation for employees. They are paid according to the amount of time worked by the employee.

Concept note-4: -/weɪdʒ/ us. (also wages [ plural ]) an amount of money that is paid, usually every week, to an employee for their work: a daily/hourly/weekly wage This deal represents an hourly wage rate of $9.83.

Concept note-5: -countable noun. Someone’s wages are the amount of money that is regularly paid to them for the work that they do. His wages have gone up. This may end efforts to set a minimum wage well above the poverty line. Synonyms: payment, pay, earnings, remuneration More Synonyms of wage.
