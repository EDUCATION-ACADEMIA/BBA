Self-service stores, this type of retail is sometimes larger than conventional markets.

(A) Specialized stores.

(B) ** Supermarkets (departmental).

(C) Wholesale store.

(D) Outlets.

(E) 24 hours.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: Superstores are much larger than regular supermarkets and offer a large assortment of routinely purchased food products, nonfood items, and services.

Concept note-2: -A supermarket is a large self-service retail store selling food and household supplies.

Concept note-3: -supermarket, large retail store operated on a self-service basis, selling groceries, fresh produce, meat, bakery and dairy products, and sometimes an assortment of nonfood goods.

Concept note-4: -In retail, self-serve is the practice of letting customers make a purchase at a store without the assistance of retail staff. This system is also sometimes referred to as “unattended retail, ” as it often requires customers to scan barcodes and pay for their items on their own.
