BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

RETAIL MARKETING

[SOURCES]
It is one of the characteristics of Retail businesses:

(A) They are big companies.

(B) They are small companies.

(C) They are companies where several companies are grouped.

(D) ** Buy in volume, sell in detail.

(E) They are companies that ONLY sell retail.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Functional. Price, convenience and store experience are functional characteristics that make up a strong retail brand. These functional characteristics are common to almost all retail stores. A brand may use its store experience to create an emotional bond by matching its brand’s characteristics with consumers’ values.

Concept note-2: -(i) The retailer’s interface with the customer is service-based. (ii) Retailers sell small quantities of items on a frequent basis. (iii) Retailers provide convenience in terms of location, credit facilities, range of merchandise, after – sales service, etc.
