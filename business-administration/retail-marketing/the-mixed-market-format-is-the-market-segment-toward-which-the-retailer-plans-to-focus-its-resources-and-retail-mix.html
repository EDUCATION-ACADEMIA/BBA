BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

RETAIL MARKETING

[SOURCES]
The mixed-market format is the market segment toward which the retailer plans to focus its resources and retail mix.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Definition of Retail Market Strategy When a retailer refers to a target market, it refers to the market segment(s) toward which the retailer intends to direct its resources and retail mix.

Concept note-2: -A retail marketing mix is similar to the traditional marketing mix, also known as the “4 Ps” of marketing. These include product, pricing, place, and promotion. The retail marketing mix adds two more “Ps” to the mix: people and presentation.

Concept note-3: -Once the strategic retail plan is in place, retailers devise the retail mix which includes product, price, place, promotion, personnel, and presentation.

Concept note-4: -The four Ps are: Product, Price, Place and Promotion.
