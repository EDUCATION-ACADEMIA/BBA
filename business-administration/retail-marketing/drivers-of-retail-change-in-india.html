Drivers of Retail change in India

(A) High standard of living

(B) Availability of low-cost customer credit

(C) Increase in per capita income

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reasons for rapid growth in Retail : a) Rising incomes and purchasing power : The average household income is increasing and leads to more purchasing power and ultimately consumption. The GDP is growing (and will continue) at a conservative rate 0f 6-7%. Rising affluence is the biggest driver of increasing consumption.

Concept note-2: -There are a host of factors that are propelling the expansion of modern retail in India, including the improving infrastructure, a move away from the socialistic pattern of economy, better educational opportunities, and rising consumerism.

Concept note-3: -Major factors responsible are the economic development, changing demographics, expanding disposable income, development of e-commerce and organized retailers helping the segment to change and advance quickly.

Concept note-4: -Change in income structure, consumer tastes & preference, demographic & geographic profile are some of the key factors that are driving towards growth in Retail Business.
