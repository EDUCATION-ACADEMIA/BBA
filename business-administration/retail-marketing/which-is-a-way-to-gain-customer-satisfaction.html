Which is a way to gain customer satisfaction?

(A) Slow service

(B) Not dealing with problems

(C) ** Confidence in service

(D) Long queues

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The three Cs of customer satisfaction: Consistency, consistency, consistency. It may not seem sexy, but consistency is the secret ingredient to making customers happy. However, it’s difficult to get right and requires top-leadership attention.

Concept note-2: -Communication Your customers are the keys to unlocking the secrets of excellent communication, which results in increased sales for your company, so this should be a major focal point of the customer satisfaction survey.
