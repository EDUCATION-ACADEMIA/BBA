EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Television is a system for transmitting visual images and sound that are reproduced on screens, chiefly used to broadcast programs for entertainment, information, and education. The television set has become a commonplace in many households, businesses, and institutions. It is a major vehicle for advertising.

Concept note-2: -The three main transmission systems are cable, satellite, and terrestrial transmitter. The differences arise because of the problems that are peculiar to each system. Each type of transmission requires a different set-top box (or internal conversion in the television receiver).

Concept note-3: -Non-skippable in-stream ads. In-feed video ads. Bumper ads. Outstream ads.

Concept note-4: -The first “television” system broadcast was a straight-line by Philo Farnsworth on September 7th, 1927. The press was presented with this scientific breakthrough on January 13, 1928 and it even headlined a few major nationwide papers.
