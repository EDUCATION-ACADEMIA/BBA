It is the quantity of products that producers are willing to sell.

(A) ** Offer.

(B) Commercialization channel.

(C) Intermediary.

(D) Vendor.

(E) Experience.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Supply is the total amount of a good or service that the producers in a market are willing to sell.

Concept note-2: -Definition: Quantity supplied is the quantity of a commodity that producers are willing to sell at a particular price at a particular point of time.

Concept note-3: -Supply-a schedule or a curve showing the amounts of a product a producer is willing and able to produce and make available for sale at each of a series of possible prices during a specific period of time. Quantity Supplied-the amount of a good that firms choose to sell at a particular price.

Concept note-4: -In economics, quantity supplied describes the number of goods or services that suppliers will produce and sell at a given market price.

Concept note-5: -The quantity supplied of a good or service is the quantity sellers are willing to sell at a particular price during a particular period, all other things unchanged. Ceteris paribus, the receipt of a higher price increases profits and induces sellers to increase the quantity they supply.
