Shopping malls, super markets and hypermarkets come under which type of marketing?

(A) wholesale

(B) ** retail

(C) direct marketing

(D) agent service

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Retail marketing comprises the activities related to selling products to the consumers through channels such as stores, malls, kiosks, vending machines, or other fixed locations. In contrast, direct marketing to consumers attempts to complete a sale through phone, mail, or web site sales.

Concept note-2: -A shopping mall is a type of shopping center, a North American term originally meaning a pedestrian promenade with shops along it, but in the late 1960s began to be used as a generic term for large shopping centers anchored by department stores, especially enclosed centers.

Concept note-3: -In a marketing plan, a key goal is the sales levels you want your tenants collectively to achieve.
