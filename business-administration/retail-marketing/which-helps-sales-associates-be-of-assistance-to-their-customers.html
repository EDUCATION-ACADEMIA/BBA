Which helps sales associates be of assistance to their customers?

(A) ** product knowledge

(B) non-personal selling

(C) social greeting

(D) rational selling

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Product knowledge is a highly relevant skill for customer-facing retail employees, like sales reps. Besides being able to clearly communicate details about the product, the sales rep must be able to explain product benefits, offer alternatives, and even upsell or cross-sell other products.

Concept note-2: -Product knowledge is the information needed to clearly explain a product to a customer. Product knowledge is a highly relevant skill for customer-facing retail employees, like sales reps.

Concept note-3: -Product knowledge is when sales reps have a complete understanding of the product they’re selling. Why is product knowledge necessary for salespeople? Sales reps should understand a product’s features and benefits from the customer’s point of view, so they can have more engaging conversations that lead to sales.
