There are 3 types of retailers, which one is not the types of retailers?

(A) store retailers

(B) nonstore retailers

(C) ** retail nonorganizations

(D) retail organizations

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The major types of nonstore retailing are direct selling, direct marketing, and automatic vending.

Concept note-2: -Establishments in this subsector include mail-order houses, vending machine operators, home delivery sales, door-to-door sales, party plan sales, electronic shopping, and sales through portable stalls (e.g., street vendors, except food).

Concept note-3: -Types of Retailers – Speciality Store, Department Store, Supermarket, Convenience Store, Discount Store, Corporate Chain Store, Voluntary Chain and a Few Others.
