Which of these are NOT a component of an Information System?

(A) Hardware & Software

(B) People

(C) ** Storage

(D) Procedure

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Option D-Company collectively handles the components in the information system and does not directly work as a part of the information system, hence it is a correct answer.

Concept note-2: -As discussed before, the first four components of information systems – hardware, software, network communication, and data, are all technologies that must integrate well together.

Concept note-3: -Now that you know what an information system is, let’s look at its components. It has five components – hardware, software, data, and telecommunications.

Concept note-4: -An information system is essentially made up of five components hardware, software, database, network and people. These five components integrate to perform input, process, output, feedback and control.
