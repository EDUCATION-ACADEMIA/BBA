MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM

[SOURCES]
Which of the following can provide regular information about the daily activities of a business to managers?

(A) Decision Support System

(B) Executive Support System

(C) Transaction Processing System

(D) ** Management Information System

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Management information systems (MIS) serve middle managers’ interests by providing current and historical performance information to aid in planning, controlling, and decision making at the management level. MIS typically compress TPS data to present regular reports on the company’s basic operations.

Concept note-2: -Tools such as laptops, databases, networks, and smartphones are examples of information systems.

Concept note-3: -Three basic activities-input, processing, and output-produce the information organizations need.
