Tools for collaboration and teamwork

(A) ** Wikis

(B) ** E-mail and instant messaging

(C) ** Virtual worlds

(D) ** Collaboration and social business platforms

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Wikis are ideal tools for storing and sharing company knowledge and insights.

Concept note-2: -Team collaboration tools–also known as team collaboration software–is a term used to define the different types of software and online services available to companies and individuals that enable them to feasibly work together on common projects, regardless of their physical location.

Concept note-3: -Examples of social business systems include intranets; forums; message boards; document and file-sharing tools; blogs; activity streams and news feeds; video/web conferencing systems; and other types of collaboration tools.
