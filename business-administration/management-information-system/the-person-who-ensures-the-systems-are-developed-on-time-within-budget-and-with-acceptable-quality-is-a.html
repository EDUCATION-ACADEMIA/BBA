The person who ensures the systems are developed on time, within budget, and with acceptable quality is a

(A) System designer

(B) ** Project Manager

(C) External system user

(D) System builder

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The person who ensures that systems are developed on time, within budget, and with acceptable quality is a Project manager. A project manager is the person responsible for leading a project from its inception to execution. This includes planning, execution and managing the people, resources and scope of the project.

Concept note-2: -A Project Manager is a professional in charge of ensuring their teams complete all projects on time and within budget.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Department of Economic Affairs. Union Budget: Article 112 to the Indian Constitution defines the term ‘Financial Statement’ also referred to as the Union Budget.

Concept note-4: -System Users actually use the system to perform or support the work to be completed. Define the business requirements and performance expectations for the system to be built. System Designers designs the system to meet user requirements.

Concept note-5: -14) Which scenario is an example of proactive budget management? Ans: While planning your project budget, you gather historical data and consult with industry experts. You consider fixed costs, add relevant line items, and set aside a 5% reserve for unexpected costs.
