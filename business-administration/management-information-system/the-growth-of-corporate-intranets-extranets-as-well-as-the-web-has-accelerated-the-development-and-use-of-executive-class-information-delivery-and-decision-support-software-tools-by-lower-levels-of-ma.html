Concept note-2: -An intranet is a private-internal-business network that enables your employees to share information, collaborate, and improve their communications. An extranet enables your business to communicate and collaborate more effectively with selected business partners, suppliers and customers.

Concept note-3: -Suppliers, especially wholesalers, use extranets to make online catalogs available to vendors. Extranets also allow suppliers to offer discounts to favored clients and to discount items that are overstocked, being phased out or proving hard to move.
