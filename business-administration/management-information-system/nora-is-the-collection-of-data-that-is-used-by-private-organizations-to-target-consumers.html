NORA is the collection of data that is used by private organizations to target consumers.

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Simply put, customer data is the information you collect about your clients and leads. It can be something as basic as their name and email address or as detailed as behavioral or transactional information.

Concept note-2: -Target Customers A target customer is an individual that’s most likely to buy your product. And it’s a subset of the broader target market. For example, if your target market is female athletes between the ages of 13 to 25, a target customer could be female athletes in the specific age range of 13 to 16.

Concept note-3: -Make sure you get their name, gender, age, profession, location, email address, phone number, and household income. This should be enough to develop a basic profile on your customer and get in touch with them when you need to.
