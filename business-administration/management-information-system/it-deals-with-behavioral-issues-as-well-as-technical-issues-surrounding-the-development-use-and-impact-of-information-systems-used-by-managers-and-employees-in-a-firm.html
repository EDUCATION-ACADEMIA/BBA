It deals with behavioral issues, as well as technical issues surrounding the development, use, and impact of information systems used by managers and employees in a firm.

(A) Database Management System

(B) ** Management Information System

(C) Database Information System

(D) Management System

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MIS deals with behavioral issues as well as technical issues surrounding the development, use, and impact of information systems used by managers and employees in the firm. Makes long-range strategic decisions about products and services as well as ensures financial performance of the firm.

Concept note-2: -If you’d like to study human behaviors and use this research to improve patients’ lives, consider a career in behavioral psychology. Behavioral psychologists work closely with others to evaluate any negative behaviors and provide treatment techniques to improve their mental wellbeing.

Concept note-3: -The behavioral approach, a growing part of the information systems field, does not ignore technology, but tends to focus on non-technical solutions concentrating instead on changes in attitudes, management and organizational policy, and behavior.

Concept note-4: -Information technology (IT) consists of all the hardware that a firm needs to use in order to achieve its business objectives, whereas information systems consist of all the software and business processes needed.

Concept note-5: -An information system is a set of interrelated components that collect or retrieve, process, store, and distribute information to support decision making and control in an organization. Information systems can also be used to analyze problems, visualize complex subjects, and create new products.
