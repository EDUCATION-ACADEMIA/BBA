Which is not a business competitive strategy?

(A) Being a low cost producer

(B) Differentiating products

(C) ** Using low cost materials of low quality

(D) Changing the scope of the competition

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Low-cost strategy enables the firm to sell its product/service with a lower price compared to its competitors because of lower costs of producing products/service; as a result of this, they win a competitive advantage in the industry.

Concept note-2: -Low Cost Strategy Example Low cost strategy is used by lot of domestic airlines which offer tickets at a very low price to acquire customers and first time fliers. This was made possible by adding frequent sectors in the routes and offering no-frills flying experience and more seating in one flight.
