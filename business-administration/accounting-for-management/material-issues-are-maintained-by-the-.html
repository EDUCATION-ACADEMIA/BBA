BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

ACCOUNTING FOR MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
Material Issues are maintained by the ____

(A) general accountant

(B) warehouse keeper

(C) ** store keeper

(D) cashier

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The account of stores is maintained by the store keeper in the same way as that of cash by the cashier. Main objectives of maintaining proper accounts are: (i) To indicate the quantity and values of stores held. (ii) To provide information about values of receipts and issues.

Concept note-2: -The request document is called material requisition. Store keeper issue the material on the basis of requisition only. Issue of material is done along with a document which is called bill of material.

Concept note-3: -Storekeeping is the process of maintaining safe custody of all raw materials, supplies, finished products, purchased items and other store-related inventories. Storekeepers are responsible for the handling of materials and ensuring that there is no deterioration of the material.
