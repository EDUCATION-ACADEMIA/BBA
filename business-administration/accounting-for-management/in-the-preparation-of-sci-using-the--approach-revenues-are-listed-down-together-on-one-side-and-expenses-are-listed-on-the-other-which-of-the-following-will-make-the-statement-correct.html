In the preparation of SCI using the ____ approach, revenues are listed down together on one side, and expenses are listed on the other. Which of the following will make the statement correct?

(A) ** single-step

(B) multi-step

(C) business

(D) merchandise

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Single-Step Income Statements All revenues and gains are totaled at the top of the statement, while all expenses and losses are totaled at the bottom. This simplified approach makes record-keeping easier for both the accountants who prepare the statements and the investors who read them.

Concept note-2: -There are two different types of income statement that a company can prepare such as the single-step income statement and the multi-step income statement.

Concept note-3: -The multi-step income statement is used to report revenue and expense activities for a merchandising business. It is an expanded, more detailed version of the single-step income statement. The most significant cost that a merchandise business incurs is the cost of acquiring the inventory that is sold.
