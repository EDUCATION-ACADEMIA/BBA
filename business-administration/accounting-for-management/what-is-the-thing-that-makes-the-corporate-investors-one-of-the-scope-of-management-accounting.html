What is the thing that makes the Corporate Investors one of the scope of Management Accounting?

(A) Details of Costs

(B) ** Company Financial Information

(C) Recording Financial Transactions & Company Activities that Require Funding

(D) Data containing all Cost Components

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Management accounting covers a wide range of areas, such as financial accounting, cost accounting, budgeting, and taxes. The primary goal is to assist management in performing its planning, directing, and managing tasks.

Concept note-2: -Owners. Investors use accounting information to determine their return on investment, based on the reported cash flows being generated by the business.

Concept note-3: -The scope of accounting includes all financial transactions and activities of a company. It includes recording, classifying, and summarizing financial information to provide accurate and timely reports. Accounting tracks the performance of a business and helps make crucial decisions about its future.

Concept note-4: -Knowledge of accounting helps investors determine an assets’ value, understand a company’s financing sources, calculate profitability, and estimate risks embedded in a company’s balance sheet.
