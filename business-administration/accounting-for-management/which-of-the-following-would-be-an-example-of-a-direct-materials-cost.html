Which of the following would be an example of a direct materials cost?

(A) ** engine on an airplane

(B) lubricant used to manufacture a lighting fixture

(C) glue used to build cabinets

(D) nails used to manufacture a table

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Engine on airplane is the correct answer. Direct material cost : Direct material…

Concept note-2: -Direct material costs are the costs of raw materials or parts that go directly into producing products. For example, if Company A is a toy manufacturer, an example of a direct material cost would be the plastic used to make the toys.

Concept note-3: -Direct material is the physical items built into a product. For example, the direct materials for a baker include flour, eggs, yeast, sugar, oil, and water.

Concept note-4: -Examples of direct materials Automobile manufacturers need steel, rubber and plastic to build vehicles. These materials are direct materials because they’re reflected in the final product.

Concept note-5: -Direct material can be classified as. Semi-variable cost.
