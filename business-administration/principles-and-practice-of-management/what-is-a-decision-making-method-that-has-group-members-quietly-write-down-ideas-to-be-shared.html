What is a decision-making method that has group members quietly write down ideas to be shared?

(A) ** nominal group technique

(B) dialectical inquiry

(C) devils advocacy

(D) none of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The nominal group technique (NGT) is a facilitation method used to generate ideas and reach a consensus. The process involves a small group of people coming together to discuss a topic, with each person sharing their ideas independently.

Concept note-2: -That would be brainstorming! Conducting a brainstorming session is a group decision making technique that does not place any undue stress on group participants. During a brainstorming session, the leader of the group will present a particular problem, after which every team member states their opinions freely.

Concept note-3: -Nominal (meaning in name only) group technique (NGT) is a structured variation of a small-group discussion to reach consensus. NGT gathers information by asking individuals to respond to questions posed by a moderator, and then asking participants to prioritize the ideas or suggestions of all group members.

Concept note-4: -A perfect example of a nominal group technique is that of a director of a project finding solutions to improve an organizationâ€™s performance. The director meets with staff members and explains what the meeting is about.

Concept note-5: -The three types of group decision making are nominal, Delphi technique and brainstorming.
