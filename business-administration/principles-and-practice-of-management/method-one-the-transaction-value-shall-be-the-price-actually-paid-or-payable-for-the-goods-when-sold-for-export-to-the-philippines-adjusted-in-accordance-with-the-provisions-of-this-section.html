BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

PRINCIPLES AND PRACTICE OF MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
Method one-The transaction value shall be the price actually paid or payable for the goods when sold for export to the Philippines adjusted in accordance with the provisions of this section.

(A) SEC 700

(B) ** SEC 701

(C) SEC 702

(D) SEC 703

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Method 1-Transaction value There must be evidence of a sale for export to the country of importation (i.e. commercial invoices, contracts, purchase orders, etc.).

Concept note-2: -(5) “the price actually paid or payable” means the total amount paid or payable to the seller directly or indirectly, for the goods imported by the buyer or for his favour .

Concept note-3: -For Method 4 you can choose to use the price of each item from one of the following: the actual imported goods. identical goods-check Method 2 (transaction value of identical goods) for the definition of identical goods.

Concept note-4: -Method 5 is based on the costs of production of the goods. Usually this method can only be used if the importer and supplier are related in a business sense. Check ‘If the buyer and seller are related for valuation purposes’ in Method 1 (transaction value of the imported goods).
