PRINCIPLES AND PRACTICE OF MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
The principles of management enhance the understanding of relationship between human and material resources for the achievement of organisational goals. Identify the feature of the principles of management being described in the given statement

(A) Cause and effect relationships

(B) Optimum utilisation of resources and effective administration

(C) Formed by practice and experimentation

(D) ** Mainly behavioural

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: The management principles are framed to influence and guide the conduct of employees. These standards demand further developing connections between subordinates, superiors, and all other individuals from the organisation. They also set up relations among human and material assets.

Concept note-2: -Principles of Management are Universal Management principles are applicable to all kinds of organizations-business & non business. They are applicable to all levels of management. Every organization must make best possible use by the use of management principles. Therefore, they are universal or all pervasive.

Concept note-3: -Basic Principles of Management. As an organization is structured, there are some basic principles of management that are necessary to run business operations effectively. The four main functions include planning, organizing, leading, and controlling.

Concept note-4: -The management principles are said to be universal in application because these are applicable to all types of organisations, irrespective of the size or nature.

Concept note-5: -The Principles of Management emphasise logical decision making and avoiding bias or prejudice at all costs.
