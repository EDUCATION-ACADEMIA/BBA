The principles of management are intended to apply to all types of organisations, business as well as non-business, small as well large, public sector as well as privatesector, manufacturing as well as the services sectors.Identify the nature of principle of management described in the above para.

(A) General guidelines

(B) flexible

(C) ** Universal applicability

(D) mainly behavioural

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Principles of management can be applied to any kind of organisation whether it is private or government, not for profit organisation, small, medium, large organisation etc. But their applicability depends upon nature, size of the organisation and different situations.

Concept note-2: -Universal-Universality of management principles implies that they can be applied to every firm, irrespective of its size, nature or location. ii. Act as guidelines-Management principles act as guidelines; they are considered general premises for decision making.

Concept note-3: -True or False “Management principles can be applied to all types of activities.” Ans. True; management is pervasive.

Concept note-4: -Basic Principles of Management. As an organization is structured, there are some basic principles of management that are necessary to run business operations effectively. The four main functions include planning, organizing, leading, and controlling.
