Which statements are TRUE about management and managers? Select all that apply

(A) A manager avoids conflict with employees and other stakeholders at all costs

(B) ** Management is a process that is used to achieve what an organization wants to achieve

(C) ** Management is the process of dealing with or controlling things or people

(D) ** A manager is an individual who is in charge of a certain group of tasks, or a certain subset of a company

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The management decisions are influenced by the values, opinions, beliefs & decisions of the managers-this statement is true.

Concept note-2: -They were initially identified as five functions by Henri Fayol in the early 1900s. Over the years, Fayol’s functions were combined and reduced to the following four main functions of management: planning, organizing, leading, and controlling.

Concept note-3: -Managers’ roles fall into three basic categories: informational roles, interpersonal roles, and decisional roles.

Concept note-4: -management skills apply to managers at top levels in an organisation is true about managers within an organisation.
