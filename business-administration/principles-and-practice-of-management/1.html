The ability to influence individuals and groups to achieve organizational goals is

(A) management

(B) effective human relations

(C) an autocratic style

(D) ** leadership

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Leadership is the ability of an individual or a group of individuals to influence and guide followers or other members of an organization.

Concept note-2: -Leadership has been described as the ability to influence others. An effective leader moves followers into action not with coercion but by eliciting their desire and conviction in the vision and goals articulated by the leader.

Concept note-3: -Leadership is defined as an ability to influence a group toward the achievement of goals.

Concept note-4: -Leadership has a direct cause and effect relationship upon organizations and their success. Leaders determine values, culture, change tolerance and employee motivation. They shape institutional strategies including their execution and effectiveness.

Concept note-5: -Leadership, both as a research area and as a practical skill, encompasses the ability of an individual, group or organization to â€œlead", influence or guide other individuals, teams, or entire organizations.
