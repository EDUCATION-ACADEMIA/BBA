[SOURCES]
Management

(A) ** THE PROCESS OF GETTING THINGS DONE, THROUGH AND WITH OTHER PEOPLE, WITH EFFICIENCY AND EFFECTIVENESS

(B) THE PROCESS OF CONTROLLING RESOURCE

(C) ALL OF THESE ANSWERS ARE INCORRECT

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Management is the process of planning and organising the resources and activities of a business to achieve specific goals in the most effective and efficient manner possible. Efficiency in management refers to the completion of tasks correctly and at minimal costs.

Concept note-2: -According to Harold Koontz, “Management is an art of getting things done through and with the people in formally organized groups. It is an art of creating an environment in which people can perform and individuals and can co-operate towards attainment of group goals”.

Concept note-3: -Management is the “art of getting things done through other people” Mary Parker Follett.

Concept note-4: -Hick defined management as “ the process of getting things done by people and through the people. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-5: -"Management is the function of getting things done through people and directing the efforts of individuals towards a common objective".
