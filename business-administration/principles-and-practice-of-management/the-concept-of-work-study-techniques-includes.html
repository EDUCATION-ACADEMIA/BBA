The concept of Work Study techniques includes

(A) Time study

(B) Motion study

(C) Fatigue study

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: Taylor’s work-study techniques incorporate – time study, fatigue study, motion study, and method study. These techniques of scientific management are directed to discover the ‘one best technique or method’ of playing out a specific undertaking.

Concept note-2: -(i) Time Study. (ii) Motion Study. (iii) Fatigue Study. (iv) Method Study.

Concept note-3: -Taylor’s work study techniques include-time study, motion study, fatigue study and method study.

Concept note-4: -(a) Time Study. (b) Motion Study. (c) Fatigue Study. (d) Method Study.

Concept note-5: -Work Study consists of two techniques, Method Study and Work Measurement, This PPT covers in detail Method Study also known as Motion Study. Work Study consists of two techniques, Method Study and Work Measurement, This PPT covers in detail Method Study also known as Motion Study.
