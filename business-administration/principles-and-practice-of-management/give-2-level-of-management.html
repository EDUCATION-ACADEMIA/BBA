Give 2 level of management

(A) First line and front line

(B) ** Top line and middle line

(C) Front line and back line

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Top-level managers are responsible for controlling and overseeing the entire organization. Middle-level managers are responsible for executing organizational plans which comply with the companyâ€™s policies. They act as an intermediary between top-level and low-level management.

Concept note-2: -Middle management includes all of the management positions in a hierarchical company structure underneath the top management positions and above the non-management workers. These managers generally answer to top management, those who have executive titles such as CEO or CFO.

Concept note-3: -Top management is made up of senior-level executives of an organization, or those positions that hold the most responsibility. Jobs titles such as Chief Operating Officer (COO), Chief Executive Officer (CEO), Chief Financial Officer (CFO), President, or Vice President are commonly used by top managers in organizations.

Concept note-4: -Middle level managers hold a unique position in the organization. Examples of middle level managers include general managers (divisional heads), functional managers (department heads) and team or project based executives or project leaders.
