Prior to the introduction of ____, five workers each given an identical task could use five different methods to perform the task with some methods being significantly more efficient than others.

(A) systems management

(B) information management

(C) contigency management

(D) ** scientific management

(E) bureaucratic management

Concept note-1: -Scientific management emphasized the scientific study of work methods to improve productivity by individual workers. It was pioneered by Frederick W.

Concept note-2: -Scientific Management. Scientific management is a management theory that analyzes work flows to improve economic efficiency, especially labor productivity. This management theory, developed by Frederick Winslow Taylor, was popular in the 1880s and 1890s in U.S. manufacturing industries.

Concept note-3: -Henri Fayol was known as the father of modern management. He gave us the famous 14 principles of management. According to him, the 5 main functions of management are Planning, Organizing, Commanding, Coordinating and Controlling.
