The management function that involves identifying and arranging the work and resources needed to achieve the goals that have been set is

(A) planning

(B) ** organizing

(C) implementing

(D) controlling

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Organizing is the function of management that involves developing an organizational structure and allocating human resources to ensure the accomplishment of objectives.

Concept note-2: -Planning. Planning means defining performance goals for the organization and determining what actions and resources are needed to achieve the goals.

Concept note-3: -The organizing function brings resources together to achieve the goals established in the planning function. Resources include materials, personnel and financial backing. Leaders need to identify what activities are necessary, assign those activities to specific personnel, effectively delegating tasks.

Concept note-4: -The organizing function is utilized by a manager to determine how they will distribute resources and organize employees according to a plan. Plans are necessary and required to be established before the organizing function can take place.

Concept note-5: -Leading. Leading consists of motivating employees and influencing their behavior to achieve organizational objectives.
