(A) ** organization

(B) forms

(C) business

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Teamwork is the collaborative effort of a team to achieve a common goal or to complete a task in the most effective and efficient way. This concept is seen within the greater framework of a team, which is a group of interdependent individuals who work together towards a common goal.

Concept note-2: -A team (or a work team) is a group of people with complementary skills who work together to achieve a specific goal.

Concept note-3: -A team is a group with a common goal, but lots of groups have a common goal. And a team works together, but lots of groups work together.
