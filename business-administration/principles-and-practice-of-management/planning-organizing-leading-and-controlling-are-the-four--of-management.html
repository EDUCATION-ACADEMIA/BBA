Planning, organizing, leading, and controlling are the four ____ of management.

(A) Characteristics

(B) ** Functions

(C) Objectives

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The four functions of management are planning, organizing, leading and controlling. In order to be a successful manager, you must do all four while managing your work and team. These are the foundations of any professional managerial position.

Concept note-2: -The four most common types of managers are top-level managers, middle managers, first-line managers, and team leaders.

Concept note-3: -Controlling may be the most important of the four management functions. It provides the information that keeps the corporate goal on track. By controlling their organizations, managers keep informed of what is happening; what is working and what isnâ€™t; and what needs to be continued, improved, or changed.
