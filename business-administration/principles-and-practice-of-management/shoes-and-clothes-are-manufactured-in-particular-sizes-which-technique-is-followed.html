Shoes and Clothes are manufactured in particular sizes. Which technique is followed.

(A) Standardisation

(B) ** Simplification

(C) Work study

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sewing, knitting, and weaving are all common practices for making clothes.

Concept note-2: -Apparel manufacturing process involves Product Design, Fabric Selection and Inspection, Patternmaking, Grading, Marking, Spreading, Cutting, Bundling, Sewing, Pressing or Folding, Finishing and Detailing, Dyeing and Washing, QC etc.

Concept note-3: -Cutting, marking, pinning, basting, stitching, seam finishing, trimming, pressing, stabilizing-all of these techniques are used in sewing over and over no matter what it is you are making. While there is a general order of construction to follow, and some guidelines, there happen to be a lot of exceptions too.
