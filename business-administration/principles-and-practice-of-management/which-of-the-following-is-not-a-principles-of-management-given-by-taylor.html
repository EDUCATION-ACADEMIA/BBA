Which of the following is Not a principles of management given by Taylor?

(A) Science, not rule of the Thumb

(B) ** Functional foremanship

(C) Maximum not restricted output

(D) Harmony not discord

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Among the options given above, Functional Foremanship is not a principle of management rather it is a technique of scientific management as given by Taylor. It comprises of the methods or steps to be taken to achieve a desired goal.

Concept note-2: -Functional foremanship is not a Principle of management given by Taylor.

Concept note-3: -Taylor supported and advocated separation of planning and execution functions. This concept is known as Functional Foremanship. Functional Foremanship is a management technique that spreads duties of a traditional industrial foreman among several different individuals.

Concept note-4: -Solution. Explanation: Functional foremanship is not a principle of scientific management or principle given by Taylor. it is a technique of scientific management.
