According to Taylor, “even a small production activity like loading figures of iron into boxes can be scientifically planned and managed. This can result in tremendous savings of human energy as well as wastage of time and materials.” Identify the related principle of scientific management

(A) Harmony, not discord

(B) ** Science, not rule of thumb

(C) Development of each and every person to get his/her greatest efficiency and prosperity

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to Taylor, “even a small production activity like loading figures of iron into boxes can be scientifically planned and managed. This can result in tremendous savings of human energy as well as wastage of time and materials.” Identify the related principle of scientific management.
