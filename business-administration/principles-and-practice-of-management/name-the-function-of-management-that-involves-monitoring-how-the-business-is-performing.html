Name the function of management that involves monitoring how the business is performing.

(A) Leading

(B) ** Controlling

(C) Planning

(D) Organizing

Concept note-2: -Controlling. The controlling function consists of monitoring performance and progress through project execution and making adjustments as needed.

Concept note-3: -Controlling: involves ensuring that performance does not deviate from standards. Controlling consists of three steps, which include (1) establishing performance standards, (2) comparing actual performance against standards, and (3) taking corrective action when necessary.
