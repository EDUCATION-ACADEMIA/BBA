What are the variances or differences in ability, experience, personality, or any other factor on a team?

(A) ** Team diversity

(B) Team level

(C) Structural accommodation

(D) De-storming

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Team diversity is the variances or differences in ability, experience, personality, or any other factor on a team.

Concept note-2: -The variances or differences in ability, experience, personality, and other factors of a team are collectively referred to as: Team level; Team level represents the average level of ability, experience, personality, and other factors of a team.
