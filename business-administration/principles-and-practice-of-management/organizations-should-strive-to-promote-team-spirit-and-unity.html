[SOURCES]
Organizations should strive to promote team spirit and unity.

(A) ** Esprit de Corps

(B) Unity of Command

(C) Unity of Direction

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Esprit de Corps. Esprit de Corps means “Team Spirit”. This henry fayol principle of management states that the management should strive to create unity, morale, and co-operation among the employees. Team spirit is a great source of strength in the organization.

Concept note-2: -Literally speaking, the phrase ‘esprit de corps’ means the spirit of loyalty and devotion which unites the members of the group. According to Fayol, management should promote a team spirit of unity and harmony among employees.

Concept note-3: -Espirit De Corps principle of management promotes “a team spirit of unity".

Concept note-4: -Esprit de corps is a sense of group cohesion in the organization and between employees. It is an important concept to keep an organization and employees loyal to each other. This group cohesion makes it easier for employees to work together to achieve common objectives.
