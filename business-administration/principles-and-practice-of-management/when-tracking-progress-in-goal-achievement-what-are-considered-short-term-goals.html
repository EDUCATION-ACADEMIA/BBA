When tracking progress in goal achievement, what are considered short term goals?

(A) distal goals

(B) ** proximal goals

(C) action plan

(D) goal commitment

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Proximal goals are short-term goals that can be achieved sooner rather than later. They also tend to be more detailed and specific. An example of a proximal goal would be passing an exam at the end of the module.

Concept note-2: -Proximal goals are objectives that are attainable in a fairly short time. For example, studying a page in the textbook may be considered a proximal goal.

Concept note-3: -A short-term goal is something you want to accomplish soon. A short term goal is a goal you can achieve in 12 months or less.
