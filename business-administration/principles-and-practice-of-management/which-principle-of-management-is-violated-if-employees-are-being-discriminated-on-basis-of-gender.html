Which principle of management is violated if employees are being discriminated on basis of gender?

(A) PRINCIPLE OF DIVISION OF WORK

(B) PRINCIPLE OF INITIATIVE

(C) ** PRINCIPLE OF EQUITY

(D) PRINCIPLE OF DIRECTION

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Principle of ‘unity of command’ is violated.

Concept note-2: -Ans. (i) Equity The principle of equity implies a sense of fairness and justice to all workers working in an organisation.

Concept note-3: -Discrimination happens when an employer treats one employee less favourably than others. It could mean a female employee being paid less than a male colleague for doing the same job, or an employee from a minority ethnic community being refused the training opportunities offered to other colleagues.

Concept note-4: -Definition: The Job Discrimination means making an adverse decision against the employee or the job applicant on the basis of his membership in a certain group.
