BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

PRINCIPLES AND PRACTICE OF MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
Plans created and implemented by middle managers that direct behavior, efforts, and attention are what kind of plans?

(A) Strategic plans

(B) Operational plans

(C) ** Tactical plans

(D) Standing plans

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tactical planning is intermediate-range (one to three years) planning that is designed to develop relatively concrete and specific means to implement the strategic plan. Middle-level managers often engage in tactical planning.

Concept note-2: -middle and top. Tactical planning means the method that breaks downs the strategic plan of business into small and short term plans in order to achieve the results. Tactical planning is used both in the top and middle-level management to attain their goal.

Concept note-3: -Tactical goals and plans are the responsibility of middle managers-heads of major divisions or functional units. Operational plans identify the specific procedures or processes needed at lower levels of the organization-frontline managers and supervisors.

Concept note-4: -Strategic Alignment Middle managers can be effectively involved in strategic planning in other ways. In a process known as strategic alignment, senior management develops the overview and then middle managers develop objectives for their departments that support this overarching strategy.
