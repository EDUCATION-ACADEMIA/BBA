(A) tasks are simple

(B) ** tasks require multiple perspectives

(C) synergy will complicate the problem solution process

(D) the company has very limited resources

(E) any of these conditions are met

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Teams are typically required when tasks are complex, require multiple perspectives, or require repeated interaction with others to complete. Autonomy is the degree to which workers have the discretion, freedom, and independence to decide how and when to accomplish their jobs.

Concept note-2: -Good teams benefit individual members by offering added support and opportunities for development. Successful teams typically have the following characteristics: Goal-oriented mindset: The most effective teams set, implement and track goals together to increase efficiency and improve productivity.
