Which function of management is involved in correcting the performance of individuals, work groups, or teams to ensure that they are all working toward the previously set goals.

(A) Planning

(B) Organizing

(C) Leading

(D) ** Controlling

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Controlling is the process of monitoring activities, measuring performance, comparing results to objectives, and making modifications and corrections when needed.

Concept note-2: -According to Koontz & O’Donell “Controlling is the measurement & correction of performance activities of subordinates in order to make sure that the enterprise objectives and plans desired to obtain them as being accomplished”. Therefore controlling has following steps: Establishment of standard performance.

Concept note-3: -Controlling. Involves evaluating and, if necessary, correcting the performance of the individuals or work groups or teams to ensure that they are all working toward the previously set goals and plans of the organization.

Concept note-4: -Planning: is the function of management that involves setting objectives and determining a course of action for achieving those objectives.

Concept note-5: -Organizing is the function of management that involves developing an organizational structure and allocating human resources to ensure the accomplishment of objectives.
