Who makes the decisions that middle managers carry out?

(A) Operational Managers

(B) ** Top-Level Managers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Middle managers carry out the decisions of top management. Operational managers are responsible for the daily operations of the business. Name the four managerial functions. What does good planning require?

Concept note-2: -Senior managers are more involved in strategic decisions; lower managers take operational decisions. In contrast, as asserted by Rouleau & Balogun (2011) middle managers are involved in various types of organisational decisions both strategic and operational.

Concept note-3: -Typically, there are three levels of management: top managers, who are responsible for overall performance; middle managers, who report to top managers and oversee lower-level managers; and first-line managers, who supervise employees to make sure that work is performed correctly and on time.
