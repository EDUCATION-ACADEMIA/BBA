BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

PRINCIPLES AND PRACTICE OF MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
A group of employees meets everyday at lunch to chat about what’s going on in the office. This is an example of

(A) outside communication

(B) ** informal communication

(C) vertical communication

(D) external communication

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An example of grapevine communication is when an employee hears a rumor about a change in company policy from another employee, and then spreads the rumor to other employees.

Concept note-2: -Strategic Management: When a group of employees is experienced and works well together, a manager does not have to be as directive and controlling. If there is enough time to bring a team together to help plan a work assignment, team members will often prefer being involved in the decision-making process.

Concept note-3: -Planning involves the effort to direct and lead people to accomplish the planned work of the organization. Work groups are an ineffective way for employees to gain experience with management activities. Management is the ability to motivate individuals and groups to achieve increased morale.

Concept note-4: -Company core values are the principles guiding and often driving an organization’s mission and goals. They guide crucial actions and behaviors, such as how business decisions are made and successful relationships are formed.
