The principles of management are the underlying factors for successful management.

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At the most fundamental level, management is a discipline that consists of a set of five general functions: planning, organizing, staffing, leading and controlling. These five functions are part of a body of practices and theories on how to be a successful manager.

Concept note-2: -Answer/Explanation (c) Principles of Management are said to be evolutionary in nature as these principles have evolved over a long period of time with continuous practice and experimentation.

Concept note-3: -Answer. Answer: An important feature of management principles is that they are universal in nature. So, it means that answer of this question is TRUE.

Concept note-4: -Answer: It is wrong that Only some principles of management are important. Explanation: All principles of management are important because every principles guide us to know how to manage the management or organisation.
