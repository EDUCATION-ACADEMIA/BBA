Power is the ability to control behavior

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Power is the ability to influence the behavior of others with or without resistance by using a variety of tactics to push or prompt action. Power is the ability to get things done. People with power are able to influence others behavior to achieve a goal or objective.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: The correct answer is: c) If A has the capacity to influence the behavior of B, then A is said to have power over B. Power is described as the ability of an individual to influence or impact other individuals.

Concept note-3: -Power is the ability to influence the behavior of others to get what you want. It is often visible to others within organizations. Conformity manifests itself in several ways, and research shows that individuals will defer to a group even when they may know that what they are doing is inaccurate or unethical.

Concept note-4: -power implies possession of ability to wield force, authority, or influence.

Concept note-5: -Power-Political Power. Power is having control and influence over other people, or possessing the authority to exercise power and influence over other people.
