Financial planning is needed by a small business only until it begins to make a profit.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Financial planning is needed by a small business only until it begins to make a profit. A cash budget is an estimate of the actual money received and paid out for a specific period. The two most common and important financial records for businesses are the income statement and the balance sheet.

Concept note-2: -Financial planning is the task of determining how a business will afford to achieve its strategic goals and objectives. Usually, a company creates a Financial Plan immediately after the vision and objectives have been set.
