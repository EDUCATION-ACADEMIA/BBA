Which of the following is not a feature of a financial plan?

(A) Simplicity

(B) ** Cost

(C) Flexibility

(D) Foresight

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cost is not a feature of financial planning as the plan deals with determining the cash flow of the organisation.

Concept note-2: -Adequate availably of funds is not a characteristic. The purpose of financial planning is to figure out how a company can afford to fulfil its strategic goals and objectives.

Concept note-3: -The financial planning process does not involve the process of consulting price with the competitors as no organization consults its financial plan with another organization.
