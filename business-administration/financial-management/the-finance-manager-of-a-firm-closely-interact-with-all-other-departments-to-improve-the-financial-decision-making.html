The finance manager of a firm closely interact with all other departments to improve the financial decision-making.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The finance manager and material manager in a firm may come together while determining Economic Order Quantity, safety level, storing place requirement, stores personnel requirement, etc. The costs of all these aspects are to be evaluated so the finance manager may come forward to help the material manager.

Concept note-2: -Financial managers generally oversee the financial health of an organization and help ensure its continued viability. They supervise important functions, such as monitoring cash flow, determining profitability, managing expenses and producing accurate financial information.

Concept note-3: -Financial managers are responsible for the financial health of an organization. They create financial reports, direct investment activities, and develop plans for the long-term financial goals of their organization.
