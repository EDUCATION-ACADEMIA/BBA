FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
preparation of a detailed, comprehensive and personalized plan for each company in which the objectives to be achieved and the costs that this will entail are determined, the resources that will be necessary are identified and a deadline is set to make them a reality.

(A) Valued added

(B) Economic indicators

(C) ** Financial planning

(D) Efficiency

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Strategic planning is a process in which an organization’s leaders define their vision for the future and identify their organization’s goals and objectives.

Concept note-2: -A budget is a plan showing the company’s objectives and how management intends to acquire and use resources to attain those objectives. Companies, nonprofit organizations, and governmental units use many different types of budgets.

Concept note-3: -Planning is ascertaining prior to what to do and how to do. It is one of the primary managerial duties. Before doing something, the manager must form an opinion on how to work on a specific job. Hence, planning is firmly correlated with discovery and creativity. But the manager would first have to set goals.

Concept note-4: -There are several tools that could be involved while conducting strategic planning. The most commonly used tools are: Visioning, Metrics or charts, SWOT analysis, Pestle analysis, Affinity diagrams, Portfolio analysis and Interrelationship digraph.
