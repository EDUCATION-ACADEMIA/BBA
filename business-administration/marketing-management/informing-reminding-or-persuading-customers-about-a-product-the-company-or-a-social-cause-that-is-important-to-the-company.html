Informing, reminding or persuading customers about a product, the company or a social cause that is important to the company.

(A) ** Promotion

(B) Marketing Information Systems/Market Research

(C) Product/Service Management

(D) Pricing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Promotion is an attempt by marketers to inform, persuade, or remind consumers and B2B users to influence their opinion or elicit a response. Most firms use some form of promotion.

Concept note-2: -Promotion is the communication link between sellers and buyers for the purpose of influencing, informing or persuading a potential buyer’s purchasing decision.

Concept note-3: -Promotion is any form of communication a business uses to inform, persuade, or remind people about its products and its image.

Concept note-4: -Promotion mix can be defined as “the element in an organisations marketing mix that serves to inform, persuade and remind the market of a product and/ or the organisation selling it, in hope of influencing the recipient’s feelings, beliefs or behaviour”.
