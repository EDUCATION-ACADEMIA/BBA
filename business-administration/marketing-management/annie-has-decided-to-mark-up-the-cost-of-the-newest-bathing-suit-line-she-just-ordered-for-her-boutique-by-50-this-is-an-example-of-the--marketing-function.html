Annie has decided to mark up the cost of the newest bathing suit line she just ordered for her boutique by 50%. This is an example of the ____ marketing function.

(A) ** Pricing

(B) Market research

(C) Financing

(D) Marketing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is (b) distribution The distribution marketing function determines the best ways for customers to check what product is available for them and at what location.

Concept note-2: -What Are the 4 Ps of Marketing? Product, price, promotion, and place form the four Ps of the marketing mix. These are the key factors that are involved in introducing a product or service to the public.

Concept note-3: -Marketing Management has the responsibility of to perform many functions in the field of marketing such as planning, organizing, directing, motivating, coordinating and controlling.

Concept note-4: -Financing is a marketing function that involves securing funding-either internally or externally-to create marketing campaigns.
