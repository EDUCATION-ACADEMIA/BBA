The famous VW commercial with its famed the tag line “Drivers Wanted” is an example of

(A) direct marketing.

(B) ** transformational appeal.

(C) interactive marketing.

(D) informational appeal.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Endorsement Appeal attempts to persuade people by using celebrities or credible people to endorse their products. The idea with the endorsement strategy is to convince consumers that the people they trust and admire use a certain product, so consumers should, too.

Concept note-2: -Importance. Celebrity branding has become a marketing strategy to help appeal a brand to new consumers. They are a way for brands to increase their sales and grow their market, having a huge influence on society as they are seen as opinion leaders.

Concept note-3: -What is an example of a celebrity endorsement? An example of a celebrity endorsement is H.E.R. becoming L’Oreal Paris’ global ambassador and promoting their hair and makeup products through commercials and other marketing content.

Concept note-4: -For example, a company that sells headphones might reach out to a celebrity musician for an endorsement, as the musician’s fans can use the headphones to listen to their songs.
