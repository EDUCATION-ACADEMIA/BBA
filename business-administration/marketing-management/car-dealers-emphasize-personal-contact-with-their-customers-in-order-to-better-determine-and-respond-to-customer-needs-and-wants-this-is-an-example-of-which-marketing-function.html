Car dealers emphasize personal contact with their customers in order to better determine and respond to customer needs and wants. This is an example of which marketing function?

(A) product/service management

(B) marketing information management

(C) ** selling

(D) distribution

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A car salesperson interacting with a client is an example of ( ) selling because it is a two-way communication between a salesperson and a potential buyer.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is (b) distribution The distribution marketing function determines the best ways for customers to check what product is available for them and at what location.

Concept note-3: -These four elements are known as the four Ps of the marketing mix: Product: a good, service, or idea to satisfy the consumerâ€™s needs. Price: what is exchanged for the product. Promotion: a means of communication between the seller and buyer.
