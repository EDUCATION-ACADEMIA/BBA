The dimension of product mix which measures how closely the different product lines are related is referred to as:

(A) Width of product mix

(B) Length of product mix

(C) Depth of product mix

(D) ** Consistency of product mix

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Consistency refers to how closely related product lines are to each other. It is in reference to their use, production, and distribution channels. The consistency of a product mix is advantageous for firms attempting to position themselves as a niche producer or distributor.

Concept note-2: -4 Dimensions of a Product Mix You can visualize a company’s product mix by breaking it down into four dimensions: length, width, depth, and consistency.

Concept note-3: -The consistency of the product mix refers to how closely relate the various product lines. are in end-use, production requirements, distribution channels or in some other way. For example:-P&G product lines are consistent insofar as they are consumer products that go through. the same distribution channels.

Concept note-4: -Depth. A product mix’s depth refers to the number of variations in each product line.

Concept note-5: -Product mix consistency is the close relationship between different product lines.
