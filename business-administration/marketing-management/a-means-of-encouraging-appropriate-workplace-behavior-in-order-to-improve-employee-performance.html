A means of encouraging appropriate workplace behavior in order to improve employee performance.

(A) ** remedial action

(B) preventive discipline

(C) Corrective Discipline

(D) Effective Management Techniques

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -remedial action. a means of encouraging appropriate workplace behavior in order to improve employee performance. exit interview. when an employee leaves a company this is arranged so that the employee and manager will get feedback.

Concept note-2: -Managers have numerous ways to impact employee performance through behavior modeling, constructive feedback, and performance reviews, among other methods. However, these techniques won’t succeed unless the manager tries to understand his employees’ motivations.

Concept note-3: -What recommendation would you give to a manager who wants to optimize team performance? Focus on improving the quality of team interdependence. Which of the following is accurate about norms? Norms allow employees to understand their roles more clearly.

Concept note-4: -A vertical organization is a chain-of-command, hierarchical structure where the tasks and responsibilities of each level of the organization are clearly defined. In a horizontal organization, top management shares decision making with self-managing teams of workers who set their own goals and make their own decisions.
