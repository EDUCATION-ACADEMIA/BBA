____ is the set of all actual and potential buyers of products or services.

(A) Segment

(B) Need

(C) Value

(D) ** Market

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The marketer defines a market as a collection of all the potential and actual buyers of a product or service. A market is the set of buyers and the industry is the set of sellers.

Concept note-2: -5th concept: MarketThe market is a set of actual and potentialbuyers of a product. These buyers share a particular need or want that can besatisfied through exchange relationships. Marketing means managing markets to bring about profitablecustomer relationships.

Concept note-3: -In a general sense, a prospective buyer is any potential buying party that could be a fit for the purchase of a company that is being marketed in a sell-side transaction. In the world of M&A, a prospective buyer is one of the buyers that gets put on a prospective buying list.

Concept note-4: -Definition: A market is defined as the sum total of all the buyers and sellers in the area or region under consideration. The area may be the earth, or countries, regions, states, or cities. The value, cost and price of items traded are as per forces of supply and demand in a market.
