MARKETING MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
Income is a psychological variable.

(A) true

(B) ** false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Motivation, perception, learning, attitude, trust, and lifestyle are some of the psychological variables which affect consumer buying. Culture is not a psychological variable; it is a social influence.

Concept note-2: -Activities, Interests, and Opinions are the AIO variables used in lifestyle analysis. Attitudes, Income and Opinions are the AIO variables used in lifestyle analysis. Psychographics is the analysis of a person’s day-to-day pattern of living as expressed in that person’s Activities, Interests, and Opinions.

Concept note-3: -Discretionary income is the amount of an individual’s income that is left for spending, investing, or saving after paying taxes and paying for personal necessities, such as food, shelter, and clothing. Discretionary income includes money spent on luxury items, vacations, and nonessential goods and services.

Concept note-4: -The economic-buyer theory says that consumers decide what to buy based on economic needs, which are concerned with making the best use of a consumer’s time and money. Economic buyers are people who know all the facts and logically compare choices to get the greatest satisfaction from spending their time and money.
