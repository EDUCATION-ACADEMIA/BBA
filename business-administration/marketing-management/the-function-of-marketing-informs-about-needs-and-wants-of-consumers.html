MARKETING MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
The function of marketing informs about needs and wants of consumers

(A) Market Planning

(B) Customer support services

(C) Promotion

(D) ** Gathering and Analysing information

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Marketing identifies customers, their needs, and how much value they place on getting those needs addressed. Marketing informs the design of the product to ensure it meets customer needs and provides value proportional to what it costs.

Concept note-2: -1) Gathering and Analysing Market information: One of the important functions of a marketer is to gather and analyse market information. It is important to identify the needs and wants of the customers and thus to produce such products which makes the customers satisfied.

Concept note-3: -The 7 functions of marketing are promotion, selling, product/service management, marketing information management, pricing, financing and distribution.

Concept note-4: -It’s called marketing research. Marketing research is the process of collecting, analyzing, and reporting marketing information that can be used to answer questions or solve problems so as to improve a company’s bottom line.

Concept note-5: -These functions are : <br> ‘(i)‘ <b>Gathering and anlaysing market information/market research:</b> In order to identify the needs and wants of customers, the marketer gathers market information. He analyses the strengths, weakness, opportunities and threats of the enterprise.
