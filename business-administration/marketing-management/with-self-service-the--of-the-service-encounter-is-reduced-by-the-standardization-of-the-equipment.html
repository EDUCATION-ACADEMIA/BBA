With self-service, the ____ of the service encounter is reduced by the standardization of the equipment.

(A) purchaser

(B) provider

(C) desirability

(D) ** variability

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -one of the four characteristics (with inseparability, intangibility and perishability) which distinguish a service; variability expresses the notion that a service may vary in standard or quality from one provider to the next or from occasion to the next. Also referred to as Heterogeneity.

Concept note-2: -Reliability is the ability to perform the promised service dependably and accurately.

Concept note-3: -Equipment-based services do not have the marketing concerns of inconsistency because people are removed from the provision of the service. Electric utilities, for example, can provide service without frequent personal contact with customers.

Concept note-4: -In determining the hypotheses, consider the role of the five types of customer-introduced variability and state hypotheses for each as the cause.
