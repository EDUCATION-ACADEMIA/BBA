(A) Promotion

(B) People

(C) Process

(D) ** Purpose

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Therefore, from the above explanation, Politics is not included in the seven Ps of the marketing mix given by Booms and Bitner.

Concept note-2: -The 7Ps of marketing are – product, pricing, place, promotion, physical evidence, people, and processes.

Concept note-3: -The four Ps of marketing are Product, Price, Place and Promotion. Patience does not come under 4 Ps of marketing.

Concept note-4: -Target market is not an element of the marketing mix. A target market refers to a group of potential customers to whom a company wants to sell its products and services.
