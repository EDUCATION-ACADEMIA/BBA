Select the explanation for merchandising-related discounts, Discounts given by manufacturers or retailers given to a company in the same trade

(A) Cash discounts

(B) Quantity discounts

(C) ** Trade discounts

(D) Seasonal discounts

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A trade discount is the amount by which a manufacturer reduces the retail price of a product when it sells to a reseller, rather than to the end customer.

Concept note-2: -An early payment discount is one form of trade finance in which a buyer pays less than the full invoice amount due by paying the supplier earlier than the invoice maturity date. An early payment discount is also commonly referred to as a cash discount or prompt payment discount.

Concept note-3: -Trade discount is referred to as the discount that is offered by a seller to the buyer of the product in the form of reduction in the price of the item. Trade discounts are offered to increase the sales of the product and make the customers feel that they are getting the best offer.

Concept note-4: -Meaning of trade discount and purpose However, when a reseller offers to buy the product in bulk, the manufacturer reduces the listed price of the product. This is called a trade discount.
