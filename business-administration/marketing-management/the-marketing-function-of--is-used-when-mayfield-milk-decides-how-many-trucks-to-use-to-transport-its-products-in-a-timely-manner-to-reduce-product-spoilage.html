The marketing function of ____ is used when Mayfield Milk decides how many trucks to use to transport its products in a timely manner to reduce product spoilage.

(A) Selling

(B) ** Distribution

(C) Financing

(D) Pricing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is (b) distribution The distribution marketing function determines the best ways for customers to check what product is available for them and at what location.

Concept note-2: -Environmental scanning helps management and marketing team to identify events and relationship among them to figure out the organization’s future or product’s success. This process also includes collecting data. Its main factors are events, trends, issues, and expectations.

Concept note-3: -The marketing concept rests on four pillars: target market, customer needs, integrated marketing and profitability.

Concept note-4: -Agent-An intermediary who does not take title to merchandise but facilitates exchanges by bringing buyers and sellers together.
