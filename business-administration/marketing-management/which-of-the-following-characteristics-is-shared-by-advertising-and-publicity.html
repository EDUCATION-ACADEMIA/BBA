MARKETING MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
Which of the following characteristics is shared by advertising and publicity:

(A) ** Is impersonal since there is no interaction with the customer

(B) Must be paid for

(C) Receives close attention from all customers in the target market.

(D) Presents the same message to all members if the target market

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Advertising involves an impersonal mode of communication in lieu of payment for the promotion of products or services. It is one of the most commonly used elements of promotion. There is no face-to-face contact between the target customers and advertiser, which leads to the impersonal nature of the promotion.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Advertising is defined as the impersonal form of communication which is paid form by the marketer to promote some goods or services. It is commonly use as the promotional tool of the company.

Concept note-3: –Explanation: Promotion is an element of the marketing mix. The promotional element consists of communication tools that include advertising, personal selling, sales promotion, public relations, and direct marketing.

Concept note-4: -The main difference between advertising and publicity is that advertising is a paid activity whose main objective is increasing sales of a company’s product or service. On the other hand, publicity is a free-of-cost activity whose main objective is to make a company’s product or service popular among the common mass.
