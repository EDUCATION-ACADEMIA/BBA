The ____ holds that consumers will favor products that are available and highly affordable (therefore, work on improving production and distribution efficiency).

(A) ** Product concept

(B) Production concept

(C) Production cost expansion concept

(D) Marketing concept

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This concept is the oldest of the concepts in business. It holds that consumers will prefer products that are widely available and inexpensive. Managers focusing on this concept concentrate on achieving high production efficiency, low costs, and mass distribution.

Concept note-2: -The production concept holds that consumers will favor products that are available and highly affordable. Therefore, it dictates that management should focus on improving production and distribution efficiency.

Concept note-3: -The Product concept holds that consumers will favour those products that offer the most quality, performance, or innovative features. The product concept proposes that consumers will prefer products that have better quality, performance and features as opposed to a normal product.

Concept note-4: -The Product concept holds that consumers will favour products that oer the most in quality performance and innovative features.
