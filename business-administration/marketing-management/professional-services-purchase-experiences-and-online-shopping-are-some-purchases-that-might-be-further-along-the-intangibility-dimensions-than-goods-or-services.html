Professional services, purchase experiences, and online shopping are some purchases that might be further along the intangibility dimensions than goods or services.

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Examples of service intangibility include: Going to see a surgeon about your back pain. It’s impossible to be sure exactly how you’ll feel after the surgery for your back pain. You could feel like a new person, or you might be in worse shape.

Concept note-2: -What are products with intangible features such as professional advice? A) Personal goods B) Services C) Industrial goods D) Psychographics E) Reseller goods Answer: B Explanation: B) Services are products having nonphysical features such as information, expertise, or an activity that can be purchased.

Concept note-3: -Services are intangible because they can often not be seen, tasted, felt, heard, or smelled before they are purchased.
