EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Outsourcing is part of a chain reaction emanating out of international trade. Outsourcing does not operate, or even begin, in a vacuum. International trade is all about exchange of resources to get the best results from them and outsourcing is part of the same phenomenon.

Concept note-2: -International business refers to the trade of goods, services, technology, capital and/or knowledge across national borders and at a global or transnational scale. It involves cross-border transactions of goods and services between two or more countries.

Concept note-3: -Globalization is the sharing and trading of concepts, ideas, people, products and services, and goods from one country to another. While outsourcing refers to a company or individual delegating tasks (typically reserved for an in-house employee) to a third party for them to complete on behalf of the company.
