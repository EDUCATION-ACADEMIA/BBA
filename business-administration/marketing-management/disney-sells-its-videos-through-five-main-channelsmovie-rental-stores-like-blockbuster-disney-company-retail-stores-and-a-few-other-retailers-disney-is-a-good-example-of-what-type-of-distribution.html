Disney sells its videos through five main channels:movie rental stores like Blockbuster, Disney company retail stores, and a few other retailers. Disney is a good example of what type of distribution?

(A) ** Selective distribution

(B) Intensive distribution

(C) Multi-channel distribution

(D) Integrated distribution

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Department stores. This type of retailing is a large-scale retailinginstitution that has a very broad and deep product assortment (both hard and soft goods), withthe provision of a wide array of customer service facilities for store customers.

Concept note-2: -Intensive distribution. This involves a large number of intermediaries. The vendor tries to place its product in as many sales outlets as possible. This method is used with products that have a high consumption frequency and a low cost of production.

Concept note-3: -Legitimate power. The manufacturer requests a behavior that is warranted under the contract. As long as the intermediaries view the manufacturer as a legitimate leader, legitimate power works.

Concept note-4: -However, logistics is really one link in the supply chain. Logistics deals with the planning and control of the movement and storage of goods and services from their point of origin to their final destination.
