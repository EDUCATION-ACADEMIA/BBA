Raising the price of the hot new Christmas toys because of predicted increase demand is an example of which marketing function?

(A) marketing information management

(B) ** pricing

(C) promotion

(D) distribution

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An example of a company using status quo pricing would be if a company chose to match the price of a competitor’s product when they began selling a similar product. Another example would be if a company kept its current prices the same while waiting for more information about a competitor’s pricing strategy.

Concept note-2: -Demand-Oriented Approaches include: skimming pricing. penetration pricing. prestige pricing.

Concept note-3: -Customer cost refers not only to the price of a product, but it also encompasses the purchase costs, use costs and the post-use costs.

Concept note-4: -Pricing strategies are the methods and procedures companies employ to determine the rates they charge for their goods and services. Pricing is the amount you charge for your items; pricing strategy is how you calculate that number. Pricing strategy can encompass anything from: The state of the market.
