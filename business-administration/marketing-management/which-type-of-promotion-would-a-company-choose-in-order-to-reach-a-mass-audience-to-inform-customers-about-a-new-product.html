Which type of promotion would a company choose in order to reach a mass audience to inform customers about a new product?

(A) ** Advertising

(B) Publicity

(C) Personal Selling

(D) Sales Promotion

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This strategy is most often used when a company is introducing a new product or would like to increase sales of a product they already have.

Concept note-2: -Advertising. Advertising is a paid promotion method where a sponsor calls for public attention through paid announcements. This promotional mix component uses paid media channels like TV, radio, newspaper, billboards, or even digital advertising channels like social media platforms and search engines.

Concept note-3: -Cross-promotion may involve two or more companies working together in promoting a service or product, in a way that benefits both.

Concept note-4: -Social media. Social media allows the brands to reach a wide base of the target audience while promoting their products and company values through videos, ads, photos, videos, and great content. For example, Tiktok has become a broad platform for brands to reach consumers through video makers.
