The change in climate of the city, where a product is being sold will affect the ____ region of the marketing plan.

(A) ** Geographic

(B) Demographic

(C) Psychographic

(D) Telepathic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A great example of geographic segmentation is a clothing retailer that presents online customers with different products based on the weather or season in the region they reside in. A customer in New York will require much different clothing in the winter months than one living in Los Angeles.

Concept note-2: -Geographic segmentation is the process of placing your customers into groups or categories based on their locations. Apart from physical location, this type of market segmentation also categorizes customers using geographical variables like climate, population, food habits, and clothing, etc.

Concept note-3: -Geographic segmentation is when a business divides its market on the basis of geography. You can geographically segment a market by area, such as cities, counties, regions, countries, and international regions. You can also break a market down into rural, suburban and urban areas.

Concept note-4: -Geographic segmentation Geographic segmentation is about grouping guests based on their physical location such as city, state, country, or economic status). The major benefit of this segmentation is it helps properties in targeting the varying preferences of people from different regions.
