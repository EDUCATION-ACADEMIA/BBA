A ____ analysis is based on a customers changes in attitudes, lifestyles, and opinions.

(A) Demographic

(B) ** Social-Cultural(Psychographics)

(C) Geographic

(D) Behavioral

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Psychographic segmentation divides people into groups based on their personality, lifestyle, social status, activities, interests, opinions, and attitudes. Psychographics is an excellent complement to demographics because they identify the motivations behind why people make particular choices.

Concept note-2: -Psychographic segmentation is the research methodology used for studying consumers and dividing them into groups using psychological characteristics including personality, lifestyle, social status, activities, interests, opinions, and attitudes.

Concept note-3: -21 Lifestyle and Psychographics Lifestyle has been generally defined as the attitudes (or attitudes), interests, and opinions (AIOs) of the potential customer.
