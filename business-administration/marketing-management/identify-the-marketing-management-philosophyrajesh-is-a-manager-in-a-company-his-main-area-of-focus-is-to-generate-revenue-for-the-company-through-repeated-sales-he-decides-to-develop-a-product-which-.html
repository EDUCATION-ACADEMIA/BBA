He decides to develop a product which generate revenue in the long run. For this he meets a team of R&D of his company. After a long meeting they decided to do a survey. Based on the survey a product is made to fulfill needs of the customers. The product later produces a lot of revenue through repeated sales.

(A) Production concept

(B) Product concept

(C) Selling concept

(D) ** Marketing concept
