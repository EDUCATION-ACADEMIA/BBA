Cadbury’s Dairy milk targeting youth after covering Children as their target market, refers to which step of process of marketing

(A) Identifying the target market

(B) ** Getting, keeping and growing customers

(C) creating, developing and communicating superior values

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cadbury India uses Youtube as its primary medium for campaigning its marketing activities. It has separate channels for its various brands and is updated regularly. So let’s now take a glimpse at the overall subscriber count of their brands on Youtube.

Concept note-2: -Cadbury has no distinct segmentation based on religion, gender, education, or disposable income. There is some age targeting used, though their use of bright colours and emphasis on fun the brand is targeting younger age groups-children and teenagers.

Concept note-3: -Stage in the Product Life Cycle Cadbury Dairy Milk is in the maturity stage of the product Life cycle.

Concept note-4: -Advertising. Cadbury uses a range of advertising techniques to spread the word about its products. They ensure that their chocolate is always in the spotlight by featuring their recognizable Dairy Milk bars in print and television promotions.
