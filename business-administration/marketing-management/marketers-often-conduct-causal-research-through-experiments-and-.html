[SOURCES]
Marketers often conduct causal research through experiments and ____

(A) informal discussions

(B) questionnaires

(C) ** simulations

(D) literature reviews

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Causal Research relies on experiments-test markets-where the researcher can conduct real-world or simulated experiments to ascertain how consumer attitudes, brand market share, and brand sales among other variables respond to changes in marketing mix strategies.

Concept note-2: -In causal research, the hypothesis uses variables to understand if one variable is causing a change in another. Experimental design: A type of design researchers use to define the parameters of the experiment. They may sometimes use it to categorize participants into different groups, if applicable.

Concept note-3: -There are three types of market research-exploratory, descriptive, and causal.

Concept note-4: -Causal Research is a type of conclusive research, which attempts to establish a cause-and-effect relationship between two or more variables. of a change in process and existing methods.
