BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

MARKETING MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
The particular assortment of goods and services that a business offers to meet the needs of its market(s) and its company goals.

(A) Product line

(B) ** Product mix

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The particular assortment of products a business offers to meet its market’s needs and its company’s goals is its product mix.

Concept note-2: -Product mix, also known as product assortment or product portfolio, refers to the complete set of products and/or services offered by a firm.

Concept note-3: -A product mix is the total number of product lines and individual products or services offered by a company. Additionally referred to as product assortment or product portfolio.

Concept note-4: -A product mix strategy is a marketing strategy that analyzes the company’s existing products and allocates resources and efforts on product lines and individual products to best promote them to a specific market or audience and in this way to maximize the company’s growth and market share.
