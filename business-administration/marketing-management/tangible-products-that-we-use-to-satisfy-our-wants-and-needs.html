Tangible products that we use to satisfy our wants and needs

(A) ** Goods

(B) Services

(C) Marketing

(D) Tangible

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Goods are tangible things that satisfy our wants (like boats, computers, cars, etc.), services are intangible things that satisfy our wants (like the services of an accountant, or a dentist, or a lawyer).

Concept note-2: -Tangible assets are physical things. Examples include land, buildings, vehicles, furniture, and equipment.

Concept note-3: -A tangible product is a physical object that can be perceived by touch such as a building, vehicle, gadget, or clothing.

Concept note-4: -Products are anything we use to satisfy needs and wants. Marketing experts divide it into two: goods and services. Goods represent tangible products. In business, products are goods or services that can be bought and sold.
