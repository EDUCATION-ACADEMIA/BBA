It means the extent to which industry sales are dominated by only a few firms.

(A) economies of scale

(B) ** consolidation

(C) merger

(D) acquisition

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Industrial concentration‚Äù refers to a structural characteristic of the business sector. It is the degree to which production in an industry-or in the economy as a whole-is dominated by a few large firms.

Concept note-2: -Oligopoly markets are markets dominated by a small number of suppliers. They can be found in all countries and across a broad range of sectors. Some oligopoly markets are competitive, while others are significantly less so, or can at least appear that way.

Concept note-3: -An oligopoly refers to a market structure that consists of a small number of firms, who together have substantial influence over a certain industry or market.

Concept note-4: -A monopoly is when one company and its product dominate an entire industry whereby there is little to no competition and consumers must purchase that specific good or service from the one company.

Concept note-5: -A duopoly exists when two companies dominate a market for a given product or service. A duopoly can have the same impact on the market as a monopoly if the two players collude on prices or output.
