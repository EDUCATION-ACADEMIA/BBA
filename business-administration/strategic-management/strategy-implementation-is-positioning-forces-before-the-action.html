Strategy implementation is positioning forces before the action

(A) TRUE

(B) ** FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Strategy formulation is the managing of forces during the action, whereas strategy implementation is the positioning of forces before the action. Strategy formulation requires coordination among a few individuals, but strategy implementation requires coordination among many.

Concept note-2: -Strategy Implementation involves all those means related to executing the strategic plans. In short, Strategy Formulation is placing the Forces before the action. In short, Strategy Implementation is managing forces during the action.

Concept note-3: -The correct option is 4. Strategy implementation should be kept separate and distinct from strategic management. The continuous planning, monitoring, implementing and controlling of strategies is a process. It is known as strategic management.

Concept note-4: -Strategy implementation is the process of turning plans into action to reach a desired outcome. Essentially, itâ€™s the art of getting stuff done.
