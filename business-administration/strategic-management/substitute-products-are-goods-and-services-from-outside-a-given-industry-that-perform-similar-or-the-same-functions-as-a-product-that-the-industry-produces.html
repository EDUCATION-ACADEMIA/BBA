Substitute products are goods and services from outside a given industry that perform similar or the same functions as a product that the industry produces.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Option A) In the extreme, substitutes can ultimately replace an industry’s products or services.

Concept note-2: -The answer to the given question is True. The substitute products can be found in the same industry because substitute products have very similar characteristics to a given product.

Concept note-3: -A substitute product is defined as a product or service that is used in place of another. It is also similar enough to be used for the same function.

Concept note-4: -A substitute is a product or service that can be easily replaced with another by consumers. In economics, products are often substitutes if the demand for one product increases when the price of the other goes up.
