Which of the followings does not belong to global strategy?

(A) Product is standardised in global scope

(B) ** Integration of competitive moves to benefit one market

(C) Subsidiaries coordinate to take advantage of each location

(D) All is true for global strategy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Four main global strategies form the basis for global firms’ organizational structure. These are domestic exporter, multinational, franchiser, and transnational. Each of these strategies is pursued with a specific business organizational structure (see Table 16-3).

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: The answer is: e)-Action plan to defeat only local competitors. Global strategy refers to an action plan to compete against the global market, not local competitors.

Concept note-3: -The four primary methods of gaining a competitive advantage are cost leadership, differentiation, defensive strategies and strategic alliances.

Concept note-4: -There are three main international strategies available: (1) multidomestic, (2) global, and (3) transnational (Figure 7.23 “International Strategy”).
