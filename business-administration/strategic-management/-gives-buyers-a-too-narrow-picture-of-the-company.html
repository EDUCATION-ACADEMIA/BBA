____ gives buyers a too narrow picture of the company

(A) Under positioning

(B) ** Over positioning

(C) Confused positioning

(D) Narrowed positioning

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Therefore, when buyers have too narrow an image of a brand, is known as over positioning error.

Concept note-2: -Over Positioning: When a brand differentiates itself to the extent that its products only appeal to a section of the target audience due to the over emphasis of certain aspects of the product.

Concept note-3: -In other words, brand positioning describes how a brand sits in customers’ minds according to the company. A brand image on the other had according to Kotler “is the set of beliefs, ideas, and impression that a person holds regarding an object".

Concept note-4: -These three types of positioning strategies are known as comparative, differentiation, and segmentation.
