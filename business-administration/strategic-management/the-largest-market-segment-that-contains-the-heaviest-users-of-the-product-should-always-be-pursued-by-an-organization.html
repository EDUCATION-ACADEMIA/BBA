(A) True

(B) ** false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -14. The largest market segment that contains the heaviest users of the product should always be pursued by an organization. Incorrect.

Concept note-2: -Value-based Customer Segmentation Segmenting customers into promoters and detractors can help you increase the value of your most loyal customers. It can also help you make sure that unhappy customers get the support they need.

Concept note-3: -Demographic, psychographic, behavioral and geographic segmentation are considered the four main types of market segmentation, but there are also many other strategies you can use, including numerous variations on the four main types.

Concept note-4: -Market segmentation is the process of dividing the market into subsets of customers who share common characteristics. The four pillars of segmentation marketers use to define their ideal customer profile (ICP) are demographic, psychographic, geographic and behavioral.

Concept note-5: -Market segmentation is a marketing strategy that uses well-defined criteria to divide a brandâ€™s total addressable market share into smaller groups. Each group, or segment, shares common characteristics that enable the brand to create focused and targeted products, offers and experiences.
