Choose the word that best completes the sentence below. A ____ usually requires a lot of time and planning to accomplish.

(A) goal setting

(B) goal

(C) ** long-term goal

(D) mid-term goal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Long Term goals-Usually life goals, Can take 3 years and out to accomplish, requires a lot of time and planning to accomplish.

Concept note-2: -A long-term goal is something you want to do further in the future. Long-term goals require time and planning. They are not something you can do this week or even this year. Long-term goals usually take 12 months or more to achieve.

Concept note-3: -Key Differences Between Short Term and Long Term Goals Typically, short-term goals are defined as accomplishments that take 3 months to a few years. Long-term goals are usually completed in 3 to 5 years, or longer. This is not a set practice, simply a common guideline that makes sense when laying out your plans.
