Bose Corporation, Massachusetts, focuses on developing innovative audio products and has become one of the world’s leading manufacturers of speakers for home entertainment, automotive, and pro audio markets. This is an example of:

(A) Horizontal Integration Strategy

(B) Stability Strategy

(C) Renewal Strategy

(D) ** Concentration Strategy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A corporate-level strategy is when a business makes a decision that affects the company’s finances, management, human resources, and where the products are sold. Focusing on a single industry is when a corporation focuses its resources on just one business.

Concept note-2: -Conglomerate growth is the opposite of basis diversification. It means that an organization looks to expand into businesses which are not (or are but very loosely) linked to its core.

Concept note-3: -Strategic management provides overall direction by developing plans and policies designed to achieve objectives and then allocating resources to implement the plans. Ultimately, strategic management is for organisations to gain a competitive edge over their competitors.

Concept note-4: -Horizontal integration is a business strategy in which one company grows its operations at the same level in an industry. Horizontal integrations help companies grow in size and revenue, expand into new markets, diversify product offerings, and reduce competition.
