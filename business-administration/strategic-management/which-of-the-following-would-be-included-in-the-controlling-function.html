Which of the following would be included in the “controlling function”?

(A) explaining routines

(B) ** measuring results against corporate objectives.

(C) giving assignments.

(D) setting standards.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Coordinating and controlling functions are carried out in order to achieve organisational goals effectively. It includes measuring the results against the specific organisational objectives. After measurement, corrective measures are taken for reaching the goal efficiently. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-2: -Measuring results against corporate objectives would be included in the “controlling function".

Concept note-3: -The basic control process, wherever it is found and whatever it is found and whatever it controls, involves three steps: (1) establishing standards. (2) measuring performance against these standards. and (3) correcting deviations from standards and plans.

Concept note-4: -Three basic types of control systems are available to executives: (1) output control, (2) behavioral control, and (3) clan control. Different organizations emphasize different types of control, but most organizations use a mix of all three types.

Concept note-5: -Coordination of action is not a controlling function in the organisation.
