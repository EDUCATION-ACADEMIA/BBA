EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The purpose of a strategic analysis is to analyze an organization’s external and internal environment, assess current strategies, and generate and evaluate the most successful strategic alternatives.

Concept note-2: -Strategic analysis refers to an evaluation of an organization’s work environment. This work environment generally defines how the organization operates its business. It helps to determine the mood functioning of the organization and whether the goals and objectives set by the organization can be met.

Concept note-3: -Strategic business analysis requires a focus on all aspects of the organization. It leverages business analysis, change leadership, and program and project management. Strategic business analysis focuses on ‘what and why’, not the ‘how’ of solution implementation.
