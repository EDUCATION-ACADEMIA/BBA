STRATEGIC MANAGEMENT

[SOURCES]
A firm using a ____ strategy targets several market segments and designs separate offers for each segment.

(A) concentrated marketing

(B) mass marketing

(C) ** differentiated marketing

(D) undifferentiated marketing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Using a differentiated marketing (or segmented marketing) strategy, a firm decides to target several market segments and designs separate offers for each.

Concept note-2: -Differentiated Marketing Strategy: The firm targets several market segments and designs separate offers for each.

Concept note-3: -A segmentation strategy is a marketing concept that refers to a company’s plan for identifying each section of its target market. Businesses develop a segmentation strategy to appropriately categorize their customers and choose the best niches for their products and advertising.

Concept note-4: -Demographic, psychographic, behavioral and geographic segmentation are considered the four main types of market segmentation, but there are also many other strategies you can use, including numerous variations on the four main types.
