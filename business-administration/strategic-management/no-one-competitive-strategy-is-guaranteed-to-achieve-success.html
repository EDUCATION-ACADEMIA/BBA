[SOURCES]
No one competitive strategy is guaranteed to achieve success

(A) ** True

(B) False

(C) Can be possible

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Differentiation leadership strategy. This is a killer strategy that allows brands to stand out among competitors. It requires identifying a unique quality that makes a company different. With this strategy, businesses become superior to their rivals on the market, which allows them to charge more for their products.

Concept note-2: -Having a competitive strategy is most important when a company has a competitive marketplace and several similar products are available for consumers. This strategy helps you create a defensive position in your industry, along with generating a superior return on investment.

Concept note-3: -A company or product or service can certainly survive without strategy, but it will never thrive.
