Which of the following is NOT a Vision Statement?

(A) Better health and wellbeing for all Australians, now and for future generations.

(B) Create economic opportunity for every member of the global workforce.

(C) ** To give customers the most compelling shopping experience possible.

(D) Connect with friends and the world around you on Facebook.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You can think of a vision statement as a rough road map, but a vision statement won’t include specific milestones, revenue goals, or strategies for achieving those goals. Your vision statement is your first step toward developing those goals and plotting your milestones.

Concept note-2: -A CX vision is a compelling description of the ideal future state that everyone is working towards, and that engages and excites both customers and team members. The CX vision guides the development of tactics and plans.

Concept note-3: -A clear and compelling vision statement tells people inside the organization what you aspire to achieve – why you are there – what is the good you seek to create in the world. It also tells those you want to convince to join you – funders, partners, clients/audiences – why what you are doing is important.

Concept note-4: -It’s comprised of three parts: what you do, how you do it, and why you do it. A vision statement outlines the company’s long-term goals and aspirations for the future in terms of its long-term growth and impact on the world.
