By focusing on your strengths you can use these to overcome your what?

(A) Threats

(B) Strengths

(C) ** Weaknesses

(D) Opportunities

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -People who focus on their strengths are more confident, have higher levels of energy and vitality. They are also more resilient, perform better at work and are more likely to achieve their goals. When focusing on our strengths our body language naturally reflects this.

Concept note-2: -Knowing one’s strengths and our weaknesses gives you a better understanding of yourself and how you best operate. Finding Your Strengths to keep your head in a lot of things, if you were looking at career options you could be able to narrow down a specific job based on things you know you were good at.

Concept note-3: -Using your strengths builds resilience and can help you overcome obstacles. Make a list of your current challenges and the strengths you have that could help you move forward with them. You don’t need a long-term plan – just a few clear steps to keep you moving forward with your current tasks or goals.
