What two major end results are managers seeking on a daily basis?

(A) ** A product or service

(B) Management and efficiency

(C) Energy and utilities

(D) Facilities and equipment

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: The two major end results managers seek on a daily basis are management and efficiency. Explanation: Management is an effective process of getting things done by the employees.

Concept note-2: -Management always work by keeping an end result or aim of the organization. The activities of managers are directing towards the end result. It is related to which feature of management (a) Management is multidimensional.

Concept note-3: -Originally identified by Henri Fayol as five elements, there are now four commonly accepted functions of management that encompass these necessary skills: planning, organizing, leading, and controlling. 1 Consider what each of these functions entails, as well as how each may look in action.
