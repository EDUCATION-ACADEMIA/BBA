Concept note-2: -The changing environment of HRM includes work force diversity, economic and technological change, globalisation, organisational restructuring, changes in the nature of jobs and work and so on.

Concept note-3: -A human resource department is also in charge of keeping employees safe, healthy, and satisfied. With proper HR management, workplace policies keep up with necessary protective measures and implementation and provide solutions to issues between team members, avoiding risk for the company and its employees.

Concept note-4: -Human resource planning is important for dealing with changes associated with external environmental factors. It assesses current human resources through an HR inventory and adapts them to changing technological, political, socio-cultural and economic forces.
