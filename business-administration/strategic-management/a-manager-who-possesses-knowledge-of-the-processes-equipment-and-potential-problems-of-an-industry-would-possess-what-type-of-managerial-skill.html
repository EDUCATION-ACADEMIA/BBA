A manager who possesses knowledge of the processes, equipment, and potential problems of an industry would possess what type of managerial skill?

(A) ** Technical

(B) Administrative

(C) Interpersonal

(D) Organizational

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: A manager who possesses knowledge of the processes, equipment, and potential problems of an industry would possess technical skills.

Concept note-2: -Some of the most important technical skills in modern management are based in mathematics, science, information technology, and mechanics. They include administrative and office skills, technical writing skills, communication and team management skills, analysis skills, and computer literacy skills.

Concept note-3: -The skills needed by managers vary according to level. Top managers need strong conceptual skills, while those at midlevels need good interpersonal skills and those at lower levels need technical skills. All managers need strong communication, decision-making, and time-management skills.
