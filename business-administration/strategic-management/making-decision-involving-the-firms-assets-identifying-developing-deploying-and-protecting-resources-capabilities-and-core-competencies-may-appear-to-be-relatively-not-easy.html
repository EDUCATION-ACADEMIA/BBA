making decision involving the firm’s assets-identifying, developing, deploying, and protecting resources, capabilities and core competencies may appear to be relatively not easy

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Similar to VRIO, the Value Chain Analysis is a great tool to identify and help establish a competitive advantage for your organization. The Value Chain framework achieves this by examining the range of activities in the business to understand the value each brings to the final product or service.

Concept note-2: -Core competencies are resources and capabilities that serve as a source of a firm’s competitive advantage over rivals. Core competencies distinguish a company competitively and reflect its personality.

Concept note-3: -A resource or capability is a core competency if it is valuable, rare, costly to imitate, and non-substitutable. A capability or resource is valuable when it allows the company to capitalize on opportunities or defend against external threats.

Concept note-4: -A capability is a combination of behaviours, skills, processes and knowledge that affects an outcome. Competency is the measure of how a person performs a capability. Both can be developed, but only one has strategic impacts. Competence is best used to support employee development.
