[SOURCES]
It includes customer-related services such as marketing or technical support.

(A) Knowledge Process Outsourcing (KPO)

(B) Information Technology-Enabled Services (ITES)

(C) ** Front Office Outsourcing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Outsourcing services BPO is often divided into two categories: back-office BPO, which includes internal business functions such as billing or purchasing, and front-office BPO, which includes customer-related services such as marketing or tech support.

Concept note-2: -Front Office Outsourcing: : Front office BPO refers to outsourcing customer-facing tasks such as marketing, sales, tech support, customer services, grievances redressal, etc.

Concept note-3: -Front-Office BPO This term refers to customer-facing services, including tech support, customer service, sales, and marketing.

Concept note-4: -Front Office Business Process Outsourcing Front office BPO refers to the outsourcing of client or customer-facing services to external businesses or third-party vendors. These services include sales, marketing, customer service, tech support, and any business process requiring direct contact with customers.
