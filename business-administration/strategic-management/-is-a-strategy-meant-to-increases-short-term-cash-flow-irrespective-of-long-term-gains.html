____ is a strategy meant to increases short term cash flow irrespective of long term gains?

(A) Divest

(B) ** Harvest

(C) Throw

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A harvest strategy is a marketing and business strategy that involves a reduction or a termination of investments in a product, product line, or line of business so that the entities involved can reap-or, harvest-the maximum profits.

Concept note-2: -Reduce your investment and take out the maximum cash flow from a product, which increases its overall profitability. This strategy is best used for Cash Cows.

Concept note-3: -There are four strategies based on BCG Matrix: Build, Hold, Harvest and Divest.

Concept note-4: -Stars: Products with high market growth and a high market share. Dogs: Products with low market growth and a low market share. Cash cows: Products with low market growth but a high market share.
