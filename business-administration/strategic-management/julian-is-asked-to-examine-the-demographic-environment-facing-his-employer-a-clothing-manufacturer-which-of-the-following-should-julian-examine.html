Julian is asked to examine the demographic environment facing his employer, a clothing manufacturer. Which of the following should Julian examine?

(A) Government regulations

(B) Inflation

(C) Economy

(D) ** Aging population

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A fragmented industry consists of a large number of small or medium-sized firms, none of which is in a position to determine industry price.

Concept note-2: -3. Oligopoly. An oligopoly market consists of a small number of large companies that sell differentiated or identical products. Since there are few players in the market, their competitive strategies are dependent on each other.

Concept note-3: -The maturity phase of the industry lifecycle often begins with a shakeout period, during which growth slows, focus shifts toward expense reduction, and consolidation occurs. Some firms achieve economies of scale, hampering the sustainability of smaller competitors.

Concept note-4: -At the maturity stage, the majority of the companies in the industry are well-established and the industry reaches its saturation point.
