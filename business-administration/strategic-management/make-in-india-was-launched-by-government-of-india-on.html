Make in India was launched by Government of India on

(A) ** 25 September, 2014

(B) 1 July, 2015

(C) 15 July, 2015

(D) 1 June, 2015

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Make in India initiative was launched by Prime Minister in September 2014 as part of a wider set of nation-building initiatives. Devised to transform India into a global design and manufacturing hub, Make in India was a timely response to a critical situation.

Concept note-2: -The initiative was formally introduced on September 25, 2014 by Mr Modi at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi, in the presence of business giants from India. The focus of Make in India programme is on 25 sectors.

Concept note-3: -Launched in 2014 under the dynamic leadership of the Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, ‘Make in India’ is transforming the country into a leading global manufacturing and investment destination.

Concept note-4: -But it is Narendra Modi, who within a matter of months, launched the ‘Make in India’ campaign to facilitate investment, foster innovation, enhance skill development, protect intellectual property & build best in class manufacturing infrastructure.

Concept note-5: -Telangana became the 29th state of India formed in 2014. Telangana was formed after reorganizing Andhra Pradesh state. Hyderabad is the capital of Telangana.
