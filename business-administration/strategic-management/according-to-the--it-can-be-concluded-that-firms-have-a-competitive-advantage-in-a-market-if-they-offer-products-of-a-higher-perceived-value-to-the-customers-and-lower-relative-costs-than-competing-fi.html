According to the ____, it can be concluded that firms have a competitive advantage in a market if they offer products of a higher perceived value to the customers and lower relative costs than competing firms

(A) ** Competitive triangle

(B) Blue ocean strategy

(C) Red ocean strategy

(D) Analysis of national competitiveness

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which of the following sentences best summarizes the differences between the red and blue oceans strategies? Red oceans are about avoiding head-to-head competition while blue oceans are about rivals fighting over a shrinking profit pool.

Concept note-2: -A blue ocean is an analogy to describe the wider, deeper potential to be found in unexplored market space.
