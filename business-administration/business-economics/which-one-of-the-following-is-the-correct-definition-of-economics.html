Which one of the following is the correct definition of Economics?

(A) ** How the limited resources are allocated to satisfy human wants

(B) How individuals, consumers and firms make economic decisions

(C) How a government manages its domestic economy, and how the global economy operates

(D) How a business is organised and run to achieve its corporate goals

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One of the defining features of economics is scarcity, which deals with how people satisfy unlimited wants and needs with limited resources. Scarcity affects the monetary value people place on goods and services and how governments and private firms decide to distribute resources.

Concept note-2: -economics. the study of how individuals and societies choose to allocate scarce resources. scarcity. the fact that there is a limited amount of resources to satisfy unlimited wants.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: The correct option is: c) The study of how individuals, businesses, governments, and entire societies make choices as they cope with scarcity and the incentives that influence and reconcile those choices. This is the best option when it comes to defining definition.

Concept note-4: -Economics is the study of scarcity and its implications for the use of resources, production of goods and services, growth of production and welfare over time, and a great variety of other complex issues of vital concern to society.
