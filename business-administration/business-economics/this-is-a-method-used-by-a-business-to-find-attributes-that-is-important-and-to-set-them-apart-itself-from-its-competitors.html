Concept note-1: -Product differentiation is a strategy used by businesses to highlight the unique features and benefits of its product or service to separate it from competitors. The marketing team communicates these unique qualities through their campaigns and promotions.

Concept note-3: -The elements of differentiation include product design, marketing, packaging, and pricing.
