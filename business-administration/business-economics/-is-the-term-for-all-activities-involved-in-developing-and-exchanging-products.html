____ is the term for all activities involved in developing and exchanging products.

(A) ** Business

(B) Entrepreneurship

(C) Economics

(D) Corporation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Commerce refers specifically to the exchange of products or services between two or more parties.

Concept note-2: -Product development typically refers to all stages involved in bringing a product from concept or idea through market release and beyond. In other words, product development incorporates a productâ€™s entire journey.

Concept note-3: -Marketing refers to activities a company undertakes to promote the buying or selling of a product or service. Marketing includes advertising, selling, and delivering products to consumers or other businesses. Some marketing is done by affiliates on behalf of a company.

Concept note-4: -Commerce is defined as the exchange of goods and services between two or more entities. It typically involves buying and selling things of value. Commerce can take place between businesses, between consumers, or between businesses and consumers.

Concept note-5: -Finance is a broad term that describes all the activities that go into managing money. In an academic setting, finance is a branch of economics that focuses on things like resource distribution, risk, and return. In a practical context, finance includes all of the activities we do to make, manage, and save money.
