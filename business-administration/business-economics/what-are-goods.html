What are goods?

(A) Actions, skills or knowledge provided by someone

(B) ** Tangible items used by consumers to satisfy their needs and wants

(C) Items we need in order to survive

(D) Companies who create services

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Goods are tangible items that are manufactured and sold for consumption. They are physical items that can be seen, touched, and used. Examples of goods include food, clothing, furniture, and electronics. Goods are typically sold in bulk, and their main purpose is to meet the needs of consumers.

Concept note-2: -Goods are visible and tangible items that satisfy human wants and needs. These goods can be bought in stores, transported to other places and sold in stores by others. Daily use items like brushes, soaps, clothes you see are all examples of goods.

Concept note-3: -Tangible assets are physical things. Examples include land, buildings, vehicles, furniture, and equipment.

Concept note-4: -Consumer goods are those goods, which satisfy the want of consumer directly. They are goods, which are used for consumption. For example bread, fruits, milk, clothes etc.
