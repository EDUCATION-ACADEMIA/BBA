A medium business/enterprise would have ____

(A) 10-49 persons employed.

(B) 250 or more persons employed.

(C) ** 50-249 persons employed.

(D) less than 10 persons employed.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SMEs are further subdivided into micro enterprises (fewer than 10 employees), small enterprises (10 to 49 employees), medium-sized enterprises (50 to 249 employees). Large enterprises employ 250 or more people.

Concept note-2: -Medium-sized enterprises: 50 to 249 employees. Large enterprises: 250 employees or more.

Concept note-3: -Some countries dock the total number of employees at 200. The United States defines an SME, among other characteristics, as those with no more than 500 workers.

Concept note-4: -Some examples are manufacturers, wholesalers, engineers, transport companies and labour hire/recruitment service providers.
