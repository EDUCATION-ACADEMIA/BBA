The word ‘Economics’ originates from the word ____

(A) ** Oikomnomikos

(B) Oikonomia

(C) Eikonomikos

(D) Ekconomics

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Oikonomikos is a Greek word which means household management. It deals with the finance and position handling within an household. The term ‘economics’ which deals with national management of economic factors was, thus, further derived.

Concept note-2: -Etymology. From economy, from Latin oeconomia, from Ancient Greek (oikonomía, “management of a household, administration”), from (oîkos, “house”) + (nómos, “management”).

Concept note-3: -The term economics is derived from the Greek word ‘oikonomos’ meaning the management of household. The term defines that how all resources are optimally used in an household so that maximum satisfaction is derived.

Concept note-4: -Economics comes from the ancient Greek word “oikonomikos” or “oikonomia.” Oikonomikos literally translates to “the task of managing a household.” French mercantilists used “economie politique” or political economy as a term for matters related to public administration.

Concept note-5: -"Oikonomia” is a Latin word which means household as it is related to the financial management of household activities through which the concept of economics was derived.
