Which of the following is an economic activity?

(A) Friends playing Chess.

(B) Boy drawing a picture.

(C) ** A man polishing shoes

(D) Mother scolding her children for not doing study.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The four essential economic activities are resource management, the production of goods and services, the distribution of goods and services, and the consumption of goods and services.

Concept note-2: -1. Agriculture, forestry and fishing are classified as primary activities. 2. Manufacturing, mining as secondary activities.

Concept note-3: -One can broadly classify five distinct examples of economic activities. These activities are producing, supplying, buying, selling, and the consumption of goods and services.

Concept note-4: -The activities of earning and spending money are known as economic activities.
