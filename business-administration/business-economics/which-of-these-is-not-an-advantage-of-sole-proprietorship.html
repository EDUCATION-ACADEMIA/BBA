Which of these is not an advantage of Sole Proprietorship

(A) If someone has the idea to make a profit he or she will be able to

(B) ** Complicated for beginners

(C) Do not require co-workers

(D) Do not have to pay separate business income taxes.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer and Explanation: Unlimited liability (option a) is not an advantage of a proprietorship. Unlimited liability is an undesirable feature for a proprietorship because it means that the owner of the enterprise is personally responsible for all debts.

Concept note-2: -Sole proprietorships are easy to establish and dismantle due to a lack of government involvement, making them popular with small business owners and contractors. Most small businesses start as sole proprietorships and end up transitioning to a limited liability entity or corporation as the company grows.
