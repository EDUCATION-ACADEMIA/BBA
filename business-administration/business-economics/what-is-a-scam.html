what is a scam?

(A) a criminal

(B) ** fake scheme to steal money or goods

(C) making profit

(D) legally allowed due to law

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A Ponzi scheme is a fraudulent investing scam which generates returns for earlier investors with money taken from later investors.

Concept note-2: -Types of Financial Crimes: Investment Fraud This type includes selling investments or securities with false, misleading information. It could be false promises, hiding facts, and insider trading tips. Mortgage and Lending Fraud A third-party may open a mortgage or loan using your information or using false information.

Concept note-3: -Fraud is another category of theft. A person commits fraud when they obtain something of monetary value through deception. Convincing people to give you money under false pretenses, such as the “Nigerian Prince” email scam, is fraud. Lying about your income or employment to obtain a mortgage loan is fraud.

Concept note-4: -The scam itself is a way to electronically launder money obtained from online scams and fraud or from crimes such as human and drug trafficking.

Concept note-5: -Fraudulence is telling lies or hoaxing people in some way. One example of fraudulence might be pretending to be a police officer, and another is claiming to have the ability to communicate with people who have died, especially if you’re taking money from relatives who hope to send them messages.
