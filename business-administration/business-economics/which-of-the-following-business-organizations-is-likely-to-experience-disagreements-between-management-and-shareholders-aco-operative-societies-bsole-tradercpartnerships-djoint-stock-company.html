(A) A

(B) B

(C) C

(D) ** D

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation. joint-stock company is a company whose stock is owned jointly by the shareholders.

Concept note-2: -In joint stock company there is separation of ownership and management and it involves placing the management of the firm under the responsibility of professionals who are not its owners. Owners of a company may include shareholders, directors, government entities and initial founders.

Concept note-3: -There is no seperation of ownership and management in Joint Stock Company.-Secretarial Practice | Shaalaa.com.
