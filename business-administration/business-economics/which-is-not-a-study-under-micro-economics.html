Which is not a study under micro economics?

(A) Consumer

(B) producer

(C) Firm

(D) ** General Employment

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -General price level is related to the prices of most of the commodity in a nation at a certain period of time. Therefore, it is not a micro variable.

Concept note-2: -The determination of the cost of a product is not a part of the microeconomic study. Explanation: The study of cost/price is part of macroeconomics rather than microeconomics. Microeconomics is defined as the study of the behavior of individuals and making decisions on resource allocation.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is option 2, i.e. the determination of prices in the agricultural sector. Agricultural prices do affect macroeconomics, but it is not a direct issue studied, unlike the other three.

Concept note-4: -Consumer behaviour, profit maximization, market mechanism all come under the study of microeconomics.
