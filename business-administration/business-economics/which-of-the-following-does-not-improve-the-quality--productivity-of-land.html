Which of the following does not improve the quality / productivity of land?

(A) ** Migration

(B) Drainage

(C) Genetically-modified crops

(D) Reclamation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The productivity of land depends on its natural qualities. If the land is flat and levelled, it will be more productive than an undulating land; similarly, land in a hilly area is more productive than a land in the desert. Its productivity also depends on the soil and climatic conditions.

Concept note-2: -The agricultural sector in India has low productivity because of Traditional farming methods; Lack of adequate machinery; Lack of finances for farmers; Lack of good quality seeds and fertilizers and ownership of land.

Concept note-3: -Cutting off the forests is not an eco-friendly way of increasing the agricultural production. Hence, the correct answer is the option C.
