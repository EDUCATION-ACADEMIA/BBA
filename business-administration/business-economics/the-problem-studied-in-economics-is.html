The problem studied in economics is

(A) unlimited wants

(B) unlimited means

(C) ** scarcity

(D) strategy to meet all our wants

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Scarcity is one of the key concepts of economics. It means that the demand for a good or service is greater than the availability of the good or service. Therefore, scarcity can limit the choices available to the consumers who ultimately make up the economy.

Concept note-2: -Economics is the study of scarcity and its implications for the use of resources, production of goods and services, growth of production and welfare over time, and a great variety of other complex issues of vital concern to society.

Concept note-3: -Scarcity involves making choices. Scarcity means individuals, businesses and governments have to deal with the problem of unlimited wants, but limited resources. Every economic system, from capitalism to socialism, has to deal with the problem of scarcity whereby the demand is greater than the supply.

Concept note-4: -Scarcity of resources refers to the situation where resources are limited in quantity and have alternative uses in production of various commodities.
