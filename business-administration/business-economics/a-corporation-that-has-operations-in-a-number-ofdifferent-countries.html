EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A multinational corporation (MNC) is a company that has business operations in at least one country other than its home country.

Concept note-2: -multinational corporation (MNC), also called transnational corporation, any corporation that is registered and operates in more than one country at a time. Generally the corporation has its headquarters in one country and operates wholly or partially owned subsidiaries in other countries.

Concept note-3: -A multinational corporation (MNC) is a company that operates in its home country, as well as in other countries around the world. It maintains a central office located in one country, which coordinates the management of all its other offices, such as administrative branches or factories.

Concept note-4: -Examples of multinational corporations include Apple, Amazon, Microsoft, McDonaldâ€™s, and Volkswagen. These companies are headquartered in one nation but operate divisions in many other countries in order to expand their business and reach more customers.
