What is the keyboard shortcut used to select all text?

(A) Ctrl+V

(B) Ctrl+Enter

(C) ** Ctrl+A

(D) Ctrl+C

(E) Ctrl+Y

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Press Ctrl+A on your keyboard to select all text in the document.

Concept note-2: -Select all of the text in your document or on your screen by holding down the “Ctrl” key and pressing the letter “A".

Concept note-3: -For example, if you want to select everything in a Word document (including images and other objects), you can use this keyboard shortcut. Here’s how it works: Click the window or page that you want to select. Press Ctrl and A at the same time.
