The ____ feature of Word Processor allows us to link our document with an external webpage

(A) Macro

(B) Vlookup

(C) ** Hyperlink

(D) Hypertext

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A hyperlink in Word is an electronic link to a hypertext file, website, recording or document that’s outside the document you’re writing. The text that you link from, known as the anchor text, is the material that relates to the link content.

Concept note-2: -Typical features of a modern word processor include multiple font sets, spell checking, grammar checking, a built-in thesaurus, automatic text correction, web integration, HTML conversion, pre-formatted publication projects such as newsletters and to-do lists, and much more.

Concept note-3: -To create a hyperlink, click Insert > Link. In the Display text box, type the text that people will click on. To link to a web address, type or paste the address in the Address box.

Concept note-4: -In computing, a hyperlink, or simply a link, is a digital reference to data that the user can follow or be guided by clicking or tapping. A hyperlink points to a whole document or to a specific element within a document. Hypertext is text with hyperlinks. The text that is linked from is known as anchor text.
