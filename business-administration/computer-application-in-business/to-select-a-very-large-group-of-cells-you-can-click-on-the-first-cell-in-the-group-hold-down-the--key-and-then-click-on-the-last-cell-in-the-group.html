To select a very large group of cells, you can click on the first cell in the group, hold down the ____ key, and then click on the last cell in the group.

(A) ** Shift

(B) Alt

(C) Tab

(D) Ctrl

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Select the letter at the top to select the entire column. Or click on any cell in the column and then press Ctrl + Space.

Concept note-2: -Copy cells by using Copy and Paste Select the cell or range of cells. Select Copy or press Ctrl + C. Select Paste or press Ctrl + V.

Concept note-3: -Alternatively, you can press Ctrl+Tab to bring up the Workbook Tabs dialog box, which allows you to select a sheet from a list. You can also quickly switch between sheets using the Sheet Tab bar at the bottom of the Excel window. Just click on the tab of the sheet you want to switch to.
