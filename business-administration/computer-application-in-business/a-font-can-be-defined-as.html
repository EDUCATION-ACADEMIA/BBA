(A) paragraph in a specific style

Concept note-1: -The word font refers to a set of printable or displayable typography or text characters in a specific style and size. Font styles are used in both print and digital text.

A font is typically measured in a point (pt) size, which is the vertical measurement of the lettering. There are approximately 72 (72.272) points in one inch or 2.54 cm.
