Instead of a mouse, many laptops use a(n) ____

(A) optical drive

(B) ** touchpad

(C) scanner

(D) laser pointer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You will often interact with the computer by moving the mouse pointer over something on the computer screen, then clicking one of the buttons. On laptops, you can use the trackpad, located below the keyboard, instead of a mouse. Simply drag your finger across the trackpad to move the pointer on the screen.

Concept note-2: -However, all modern laptops have USB ports that allow for any USB mouse (wired or wireless) to connect to them.

Concept note-3: -The touchpad on a laptop does the same job as the mouse on a desktop computer. It is sometimes also called a trackpad. The touchpad is used to control the mouse pointer, also known as the cursor.

Concept note-4: -A mouse is a handy accessory if you use a laptop, PC, or even a TV. They help you be more productive than if you were using just a trackpad.
