A flight attendant who is expected to act cheerful and happy as a part of the job would likely experience:

(A) emotional deviance

(B) positive emotional contagion

(C) ** emotional laboran

(D) increase in emotional intelligence

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Emotional labor is the process of managing feelings and expressions to fulfill the emotional requirements of a job. More specifically, workers are expected to regulate their emotions during interactions with customers, co-workers, clients and managers.

Concept note-2: -Surface acting involves faking the required emotions: that is, when people engage in surface acting, they do not actually try to feel the emotions they wish to portray. They may put on “fake smiles” or other required emotional displays that do not reflect their true feelings.

Concept note-3: -When high job satisfaction is matched with a high organizational commitment, employees are very likely to have a positive work attitude. If both job satisfaction and organizational commitment are low, employees are not nearly as likely to work as diligently as possible.

Concept note-4: -Acting in ways consistent with one’s personal values and the commonly held values of the organization and society is called: a. emotional behavior.
