What management theory states that employees are inherently motivated and should be left to complete their work without supervision?

(A) Theory X

(B) ** Theory Y

(C) Maslows theory

(D) Herzberg, s theory

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Management influenced by this theory assumes that employees are ambitious, self-motivated and anxious to accept greater responsibility and exercise self-control, self-direction, autonomy and empowerment. Management believes that employees enjoy their work.

Concept note-2: -Theory Y – people are self-motivated and enjoy the challenge of work. Managers with this assumption have a more collaborative relationship with their people, and motivate them by allowing them to work on their own initiative, giving them responsibility, and empowering them to make decisions.

Concept note-3: -Theory Y-the integration of goals-emphasizes the average person’s intrinsic interest in his work, his desire to be self-directing and to seek responsibility, and his capacity to be creative in solving business problems.
