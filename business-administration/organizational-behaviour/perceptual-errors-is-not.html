Perceptual errors is not

(A) Stereotype

(B) Halo Effect

(C) Similarity error

(D) ** None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A perceptual error is the inability to judge humans, things or situations fairly and accurately. Examples could include such things as bias, prejudice, stereotyping, which have always caused human beings to err in different aspects of their lives.

Concept note-2: -The answer is B) Skewness errors Perceptual error is the inability to judge a human, situation or things fairly and accurately resulting in strains in relationships, transactions and other activities.

Concept note-3: -It involves the following phenomena: primacy effect, selective perception, stereotyping, halo effect, projection and expectancy effect. They are the types of perceptual errors.
