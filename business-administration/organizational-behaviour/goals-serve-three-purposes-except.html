Goals serve three purposes except

(A) To provide challenges and standards which the individual or unit can be assessed

(B) ** To guide towards a better life in the next 10 years towards the company

(C) To guide and direct behaviour toward overall organisational goals and strategies

(D) To define what is important and provide a framework for planning

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The hierarchy of Business Goals. When building a business, you should concentrate on a different hierarchy: Survive. Sustain. Profit.

Concept note-2: -“WHY” Goals: Achieving Your Work-Life Balance The second types of goals you need are your personal, family, and health goals. In reality, these are the most important goals of all in determining your happiness and well-being.
