The ____ is the most popular typology of personality traits.

(A) Hofstede Cultural Profile

(B) Holland Code

(C) Big Five Model

(D) ** Myers-Briggs Type Indicator

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ISFJ stands for Introversion, Sensing, Feeling and Judging. This combination of traits means that people with the ISFJ personality type tend to be compassionate, caring and conventional.

Concept note-2: -Why is it so popular? The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator was created to help people better understand themselves and those around them. It allows people to appreciate and respect differences for better communications, relationships, teamwork and a better life.

Concept note-3: -Because the Myers-Briggs Personality Type Indicator is relatively easy to use, it has become one of the most popular psychological instruments currently in use today. Approximately two million U.S. adults complete the inventory each year.

Concept note-4: -In personality typology, the Myers–Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is an introspective self-report questionnaire indicating differing psychological preferences in how people perceive the world and make decisions.
