What are the Assumptions of S-R Model?

(A) Internal feeling (Influenced by maintenance factors)

(B) ** Internal feeling (Influenced by motivational factors)

(C) External environment (Includes environment in various forms)

(D) ** External environment (Includes stimulus in various forms)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The simplest type of response is a direct one-to-one stimulus-response reaction. A change in the environment is the stimulus; the reaction of the organism to it is the response.

Concept note-2: -Major Assumptions of Roy’s Adaptation Model “Persons have mutual relationships with the world and God. Human meaning is rooted in the omega point convergence of the universe. God is intimately revealed in the diversity of creation. Persons use human creative abilities of awareness, enlightenment, and faith.

Concept note-3: -The external stimulus includes touch and pain, vision, smell, taste, sound, and balance (equilibrium). These sensory stimuli are activated by external changes. Pain and touch: Pain is the stimulus that can cause a major response from the body. Pain can also change the behavior of the organism.

Concept note-4: -excited by three types of stimuli-mechanical, thermal, and chemical; some endings respond primarily to one type of stimulation, whereas other endings can detect all types.
