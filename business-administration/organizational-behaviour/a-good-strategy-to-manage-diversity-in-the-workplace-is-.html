A good strategy to manage diversity in the workplace is ____

(A) ** Flexible schedules

(B) Salary reduction

(C) Promoting one political ideology

(D) Gender differentiation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Zero-tolerance policies – One of the most immediate, effective strategies to manage diversity in the workplace is to introduce a zero-tolerance policy against harassment, bullying, and discrimination. This ensures that any such allegations are taken seriously and investigated.

Concept note-2: -Adopting a flexible approach to work will assist in attracting and retaining talent in a diverse workforce.

Concept note-3: -Published Jan 1, 2020. + Follow. Workplace flexibility refers to situation whereby employees have choice as to how, when and where they work and accomplish their task/s. Diversity refers to the process of a diverse range of people with different circumstances and backgrounds within the workplace.

Concept note-4: -Diversity Management is a branch of Human Resources Management. It adopts specific actions and strategies to promote diversity in the workplace and to benefit from the creation of a diverse workforce. These include hiring employees from all backgrounds, with unique individual characteristics, attitudes and skills.
