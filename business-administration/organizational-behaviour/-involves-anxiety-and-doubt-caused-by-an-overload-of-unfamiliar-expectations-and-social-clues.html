[SOURCES]
____ involves anxiety and doubt caused by an overload of unfamiliar expectations and social clues

(A) Organizational culture

(B) Organizational climate

(C) ** Cultural shock

(D) Cross culture

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“Culture shock” is a normal process of adapting to a new culture. It is a time when a person becomes aware of the differences and/or conflicts in values and customs between their home culture and the new culture they are in. Common feelings may be anxiety, confusion, homesickness, and/or anger.

Concept note-2: -Scientists have studied culture shock for over a century. It is referred to as “acculturative stress” and rather than focusing on the purely negative aspects, psychologists describe it as a process of stress and adjustment.

Concept note-3: -There are obvious examples of culture shock such as getting used to a different language, a different climate, a different transport system and different food customs. Less obvious examples of culture shock include acclimatising to: different hand gestures. different facial expressions and levels of eye contact.
