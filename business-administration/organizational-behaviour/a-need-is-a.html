A need is a

(A) ** Pull

(B) Push

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Need pull is another important element of source innovation. As the expert says “need pull” innovation is captured in the saying ‘necessity is the mother of invention’. But, this concept means?. Any innovative idea is often the response to a real or perceived need for change.

Concept note-2: -Knowledge Push – New knowledge or research suggest some new opportunities • Need Pull – When an unfulfilled need is present and someone comes up with an idea to fill that need. – Note that these two are often related. Frequently a new technology enables a previously unfulfilled need to be fulfilled.

Concept note-3: -A two-pronged approach to maximizing value may be optimal-pushing the design of value generation through HTAs at an early stage via joint value definition and then later pulling the value through via your local teams to ensure that value is realized at launch.

Concept note-4: -Examples of enterprises/tools that “pull” knowledge or information inward: Enterprises that offer research assistance services. Customized news alerts. Help desks.
