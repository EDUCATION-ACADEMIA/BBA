The definition of annuity?

(A) ** A series of equal amount of payment /deposits made at equal intervals time

(B) A series of equal amount of discount made at equal intervals time

(C) A series of equal amount of annuity made at equal intervals time

(D) A series of marvel movie !

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An annuity is a series of equal cash flows, or payments, made at regular intervals (e.g., monthly or annually). The payments must be equal, and the interval between payments must be regular.

Concept note-2: -A sequence of equal payments made at equal periods of time is called an annuity.

Concept note-3: -An annuity is a contract between you and an insurance company that requires the insurer to make payments to you, either immediately or in the future. An Annuity plan offers a fixed amount of money for the rest of your life in return for a lump sum payment or a series of instalments.

Concept note-4: -What Is an Ordinary Annuity? An ordinary annuity is a series of equal payments made at the end of consecutive periods over a fixed length of time.

Concept note-5: -The term of the annuity is the time from the beginning of the first payment interval to the end of the last payment interval. A payment interval is the time between successive payments.
