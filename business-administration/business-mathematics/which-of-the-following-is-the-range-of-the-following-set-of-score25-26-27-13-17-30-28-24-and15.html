Which of the following is the range of the following set of score:25, 26, 27, 13, 17, 30, 28, 24, and15.

(A) 13

(B) 15

(C) ** 17

(D) 24

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solution: The range of the given data is 20. To answer this question, we will do the following steps: First of all, we should know that the range of a given data is equal to the difference between the highest value and the lowest value in the given data set.
