Find the actual time and approximate time between April 22, 2021 to August 25, 2021

(A) ** Actual time = 125 days; Approx. Time = 123 days

(B) Actual time = 126 days; Approx. Time = 122 days

(C) Actual time = 123 days; Approx. Time = 125 days

(D) Actual time = 145 days; Approx. Time = 143 days

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An approximate number, time, or position is close to the correct number, time, or position, but is not exact.

Concept note-2: -Find the exact time from May 15 on Year 1 to March 15 in Year 2. The exact time is 304 days. Note: If Year 2 is a leap year, the exact time is 305 days.

Concept note-3: -You should also know that April, June, September and November have 30 days, February has 28 days (29 days during a leap year) and the remaining months have 31 days.
