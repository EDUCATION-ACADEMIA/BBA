Arif invested RM 4, 500 at 10.5% simple interest in a bank. He withdrew RM 2, 500 from the account 3 years later. How much was left in the account?

(A) RM 5, 917.00

(B) RM 2, 500.00

(C) ** RM 3, 417.50

(D) RM 4, 000.00

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ans Amount after 5 years = Rs. 16105. 1.

Concept note-2: -500/-for 3 years at simple interest at 5% per annum. ∴ The amount is Rs. 575.

Concept note-3: -∴ The amount of Rs. 500 will become Rs. 625 at the rate of 5% per annum in 5 years.

Concept note-4: -Hence, the simple interest on the given amount is Rs 1500.
