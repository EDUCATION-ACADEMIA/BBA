A manufacturer lists a desk at $600 with a discount series of 20/15/10. What is the final cost of the desk after the discounts?

(A) ** $367.20

(B) $451.42

(C) $232.80

(D) $388.12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Get the the discount percentage (for example 20% ) Calculate the savings: 20% of $90 = $18. Subtract the savings from the original price to get the sale price: $90-$18 = $72.

Concept note-2: -In that 20/10/5 split, the 20 percent is taken off the entire purchase amount, while the 10 percent is deducted from what remains, and the five percent deducted from whatâ€™s left over after the 10 percent is deducted.
