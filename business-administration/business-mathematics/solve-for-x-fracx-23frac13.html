Solve for x: x − 2 3 = 1 3

(A) x =-3

(B) x = 6

(C) x = 2

(D) ** x = 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2x+1=x−3 which are on the Cartesian plane are infinitely many solutions.

Concept note-2: -Pick any two pairs of equations from the system. Eliminate the same variable from each pair using the Addition/Subtraction method. Solve the system of the two new equations using the Addition/Subtraction method. Substitute the solution back into one of the original equations and solve for the third variable.

Concept note-3: -Hence, required solution is x=12.
