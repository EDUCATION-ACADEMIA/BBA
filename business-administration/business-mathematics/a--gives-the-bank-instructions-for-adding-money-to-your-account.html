A ____ gives the bank instructions for adding money to your account.

(A) Check Register

(B) Check

(C) Statement

(D) ** Deposit Slip

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The customer is required to fill out the deposit slip before approaching the bank teller to deposit funds. If the customer uses a deposit slip in the bank, the account number will need to be written at the bottom of the slip where indicated.

Concept note-2: -Filling Out a Deposit Slip It includes the name of the depositor and the account number, along with the name of the account holder to whom the deposit is being made to. Pre-printed deposit slips from checkbooks usually already have the information filled in for the checkbook holder.

Concept note-3: -The deposit slip works as a proof for the bank acknowledging the payment received from the customer. Upon entering a bank, a customer can find a pile of deposit slips with designated fields to fill in the required information to complete the deposit process.
