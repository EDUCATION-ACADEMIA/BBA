A bag is filled with red marbles and blue marbles. There are 115 total marbles in the bag, and 1/5 of the marbles are blue. How many blue marbles are in the bag

(A) 92

(B) ** 23

(C) 100

(D) 15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are 35 marbles in all (10 red + 8 blue + 10 yellow + 7 pink = 35). Therefore the probability of drawing a red or blue marble is 18/35.

Concept note-2: -Hence, the total number of ways =280.
