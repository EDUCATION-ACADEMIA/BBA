It is the fraction which is the numerator of a fraction is either equal or more than its denominator

(A) PROPER FRACTION

(B) ** IMPROPER FRACTION

(C) MIXED NUMBERS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An improper fraction is a fraction where the numerator is greater than (or equal to) the denominator. Q. In an improper fraction, the numerator is greater than or equal to the denominator and the denominator is not equal to zero. State whether the statement is true or false.

Concept note-2: -An improper fraction is a fraction whose numerator is equal to or greater than its denominator. 3/4, 2/11, and 7/19 are proper fractions, while 5/2, 8/5, and 12/11 are improper fractions.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: A fraction whose numerator is greater than the denominator is called an improper fraction. We can also call it a mixed number.

Concept note-4: -A complex fraction is a rational expression that has a fraction in its numerator, denominator or both. In other words, there is at least one small fraction within the overall fraction.

Concept note-5: -We also know that when you have the same numerator and denominator in a fraction, it always equals 1.
