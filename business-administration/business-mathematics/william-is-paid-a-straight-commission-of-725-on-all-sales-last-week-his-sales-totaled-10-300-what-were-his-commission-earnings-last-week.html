William is paid a straight commission of 7.25% on all sales. Last week his sales totaled $10, 300. What were his commission earnings last week?

(A) $647.75

(B) $757.75

(C) ** $746.75

(D) $547.75

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -William is paid a straight commission of 7.25% on all sales. The total sale in last week is $10, 300. We need to find the commission earnings of william. Hence 746.25 was her commission earnings.

Concept note-2: -Straight commission Salespeople who work on straight commission only earn money when they complete a sale. No sale equals no income. Since the company doesn’t provide a base salary, it can offer higher commission rates which often attracts the best salespeople.

Concept note-3: -Straight Commission. Straight Commission is calculated to be the person’s wage based solely on sales.
