What is 5% of 20?

(A) ** 1

(B) 2

(C) 5

(D) 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -5 percent is one half of 10 percent. To calculate 5 percent of a number, simply divide 10 percent of the number by 2. For example, 5 percent of 230 is 23 divided by 2, or 11.5.

Concept note-2: -We can see that this gives us the exact same answer as the first method: 20/1 as a percentage is 2000%.

Concept note-3: -Solution: 7/20 as a percent is 35%

Concept note-4: -A fifth is one out of five equal parts. It can also be written as 20% or 0.2.
