Write 0.65 as a percent

(A) 66%

(B) ** .65%

(C) 65%

(D) .66%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -[Or simply, since percentages are numerators of fractions with denominator 100, 0.65=65100=65%] Q.

Concept note-2: -Solution: 0.65 as a fraction is 13/20.

Concept note-3: -To convert a decimal to a percentage, multiply by 100 (just move the decimal point 2 places to the right). For example, 0.065 = 6.5% and 3.75 = 375%.

Concept note-4: -Solution. 0.625 is equal to 62.5%.

Concept note-5: -Solution: 65/100 as a percent is 65%
