Haru Umkei’s taxable income is $44, 200. Find her state income tax if she pays $1, 600 plus 7% of her taxable income over $40, 000.

(A) ** $1, 894

(B) $1.89

(C) $18, 940

(D) $1, 890.4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Your gross income minus all available deductions is your taxable income. Compare that amount to your tax bracket to estimate the amount you’ll owe before applying any available tax credits.

Concept note-2: -If you make $104, 000 a year living in the region of California, USA, you will be taxed $31, 662. That means that your net pay will be $72, 338 per year, or $6, 028 per month. Your average tax rate is 30.4% and your marginal tax rate is 42.6%.
