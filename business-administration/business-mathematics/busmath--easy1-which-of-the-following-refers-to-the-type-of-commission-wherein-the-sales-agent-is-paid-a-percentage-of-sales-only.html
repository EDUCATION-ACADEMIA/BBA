BusMath = Easy1. Which of the following refers to the type of commission wherein the sales agent is paid a percentage of sales only?

(A) Simple

(B) ** Straight

(C) Graduated

(D) Salary Plus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Straight Commission All compensation is based on an agreed-upon percentage of sales.

Concept note-2: -A graduated commission is a system of compensation for sales people where the percentage of sales earned increases as the sales volume increases. Therefore, graduated commission is also one kind of commission.

Concept note-3: -Straight Commission. Straight Commission is calculated to be the person’s wage based solely on sales. Example: 1. Patrick works at the Brick, and is paid based on his weekly sales.

Concept note-4: -How it works: Also known as a Straight Commission plan, the Commission Only structure refers to paying reps a set commission whenever they make a sale. Reps don’t earn a base salary or have the opportunity to increase their commission percentage. Example: A sales rep earns a 25% commission on every product he sells.
