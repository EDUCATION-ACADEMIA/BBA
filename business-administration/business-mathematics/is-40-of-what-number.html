IS 40% OF WHAT NUMBER?

(A) 180

(B) 75

(C) ** 112.50

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You get 40 percent of a number by multiplying it by . 40 or the fractional equivalent, 2/5. So, 40 percent of 20 is 8. 40 percent of 30 is 12.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: 50 is 40 percent of 125.

Concept note-3: -Let us assume the number whose 40 percent is 32 to be x. Hence, we solve the equation (i) and get the required solution. Hence, we obtain the value of the unknown quantity to be 80. Thus, 32 is 40 percent of the number 80.

Concept note-4: -The total answers count 55-it’s 100%, so we to get a 1% value, divide 55 by 100 to get 0.55. Next, calculate the percentage of 40: divide 40 by 1% value (0.55), and you get 72.73%-it’s your percentage grade.
