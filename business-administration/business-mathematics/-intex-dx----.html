∫ ⁡ e x dx = ____

(A) e x

(B) a x

(C) log x

(D) ** e x +c

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Constant of Integration is added to the answer of the integration. This is written to represent the constant term of the original function, which could not be obtained through this anti-derivative process. The constant of integration can have arbitrary values and is written as +C.
