Two equivalent ratios joined by an equal sign

(A) current ratio

(B) ** proportion

(C) quick ratio

(D) quotient

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A proportion is an equality of two ratios.

Concept note-2: -A proportion is created when two ratios are equal, or we can say that two equal ratios form a proportion. You can write a proportion when we know that two ratios are equivalent. These two ratios are equivalent. You can say that the two ratios form a proportion.

Concept note-3: -AN EQUATION THAT STATES TWO RATIOS ARE EQUAL IS CALLED A PROPORTION.
