Mr. Sarmiento aims to have his investment grow to P500, 000 in 4 years. how much should he invest in an account that pays 5% compounded annually?

(A) ** 411, 351.24

(B) 607, 753.13

(C) 389, 451

(D) 745, 212.90

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Summary: An investment of $10000 today invested at 6% for five years at simple interest will be $13, 000.

Concept note-2: -Using the rule of 72, you would estimate that an investment with a 5% compound interest rate would double in 14 years (72/5).

Concept note-3: -1 Answer. Joe D. It will approximately take 18 years 10 months.
