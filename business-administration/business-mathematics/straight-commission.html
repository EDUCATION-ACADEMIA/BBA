[SOURCES]
straight commission

(A) ** Specified amount of money paid for each sale or percent of the total value of sales.

(B) a unit rate

(C) Offers a different rate of commission for each level of sales.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Straight Commission. Straight Commission is calculated to be the person’s wage based solely on sales.

Concept note-2: -1. Revenue Commission Structure. One of the simplest and most commonly used sales commission structures is variable pay as a percentage of a single sale’s revenue. Under this incentive structure, reps earn a flat percentage for every sale.

Concept note-3: -Just take the sale price, multiply it by the commission percentage, and divide it by 100. An example calculation: a blue widget is sold for $70 . The salesperson works on a commission-he/she gets 14% out of every transaction, which amounts to $9.80 .

Concept note-4: -A graduated commission is a system of compensation for sales people where the percentage of sales earned increases as the sales volume increases. Therefore, graduated commission is also one kind of commission.
