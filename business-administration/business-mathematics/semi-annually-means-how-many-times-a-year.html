Semi-Annually means how many times a year?

(A) 4

(B) ** 2

(C) 1

(D) 6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -: occurring every six months or twice a year.

Concept note-2: -Semiannual is an adjective that describes something that is paid, reported, published, or otherwise takes place twice each year, typically once every six months.

Concept note-3: -Semiannual means an event that happens twice a year, every six months.

Concept note-4: -Semi-Annually means once every six months. Semi-Annually or “Semi-annual” means every six months.

Concept note-5: -For most semiannual periods, the length of the period is either 181 days or 184 days in years with 365 days. An extra day for February 29 may lengthen the semiannual period.
