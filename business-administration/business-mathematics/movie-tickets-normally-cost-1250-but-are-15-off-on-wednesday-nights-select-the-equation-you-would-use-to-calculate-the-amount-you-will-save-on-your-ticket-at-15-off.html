Movie tickets normally cost $12.50 but are 15% off on Wednesday nights. Select the equation you would use to calculate the amount you will save on your ticket at 15% off.

(A) Savings = 12.50 * 15%

(B) Savings = $12.50 * .85

(C) Savings = $12.50 * 1.15

(D) ** Savings = $12.50 * .15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -While multiplex owners discuss ticket prices with the producer or studio, single screen owners discuss it with the distributor of the film in the state.In most cases, it is the single screen owner who decides the price but for big budget films, sometimes even the distributor has a say.

Concept note-3: -Movie ticket prices in India resurged in 2022 with the average cost for a ticket amounting to 119 Indian rupees. This was a significant recovery from the previous year when ticket prices dipped to 87 rupees.

Concept note-4: -India will celebrate National Cinema Day on September 16. Announced by the Multiplex Association of India (MAI), the event will take place across 4, 000 screens in the country. Tickets will be priced at Rs 75 in all the participating cinemas.
