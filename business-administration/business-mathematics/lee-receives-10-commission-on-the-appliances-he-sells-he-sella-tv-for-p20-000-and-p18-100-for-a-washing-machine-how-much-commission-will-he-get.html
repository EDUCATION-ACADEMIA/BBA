Lee receives 10% commission on the appliances he sells. He sella TV for P20, 000 and P18, 100 for a washing machine. How much commission will he get?

(A) 2, 000

(B) 1, 810

(C) ** 3, 810

(D) 2, 810

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, basic price of the washing is Rs 12, 300. Q. The price of a washing machine, inclusive of sales tax is Rs. 13, 530.

Concept note-2: -The most effective way to determine the right load capacity of the washing machine for your family is to weigh the average load of the laundry you wash in one cycle. This may sound obscure, but it is a handy tip that works! Once you know this, you can look for a washing machine with a suitable load capacity.

Concept note-3: -The main washing machine types include front-load washing machines, top-load models with an agitator or impeller, stacked laundry centers, all-in-one combination washers and dryers, as well as compact or portable washing machines.
