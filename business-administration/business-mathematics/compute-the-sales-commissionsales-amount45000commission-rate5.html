Compute the sales commission:Sales amount:$450.00Commission rate:5%

(A) $25.00

(B) $32.00

(C) ** $22.50

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To calculate the payable commission, multiply the sales revenue by the sales commission rate. A 10 percent commission rate on a $10, 000 product deal would pay $1, 000 in commission.

Concept note-2: -Salespeople receive a pre-defined percentage of all the sales they make. For example, if you sell $50, 000 in revenue while working with a company that pays out 5% of revenue, your commission will be $2, 500.

Concept note-3: -A commission, in its simplest form, is some percentage of revenue. For example, a salesperson may earn 3% of whatever they sell. If a product is sold for $100, the salesperson would earn $3 from that sale.

Concept note-4: -Sales commission rates range from 5% to as much as 50%, but most companies pay between 20-30%. To find the right fit that aligns with your sales goals, start by estimating how much it would cost to hire people under different sales commission structures-both for full-time staff and independent contractors.
