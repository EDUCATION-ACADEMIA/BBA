Two numbers are in the ratio 5:6. If 8 is subtracted from each, the new numbers are in the ratio of 3:4. What are the two numbers?

(A) 25 and 30

(B) 12 and 16

(C) ** 20 and 24

(D) 9 and 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, the two numbers are 40 and 48 .

Concept note-2: -Two numbers are in the ratio of 5 : 6. If 8 is subtracted from them, they become in the ratio of 4 : 5. The number are 5 × 8 = 40, 6 × 8 = 48. ∴ The numbers are (40, 48).

Concept note-3: -Two numbers are in the ratio of 5:6. if 5 is subtracted from each number the ratio becomes 4:5.

Concept note-4: -Answer: The two numbers are 40 and 48, which are in the ratio 5 : 6.
