The hourly rate on rest day which fall on a regular holiday is 260% of the regular rate. Assuming that the regular rate is 56.25 per hour. What should be the hourly rate?

(A) ** Php. 146.25

(B) Php.147.25

(C) Php.148.25

(D) Php.149.25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The hourly rate for a normal day overtime is equal to 125% of the normal hourly wage of the employee. For a rest day overtime, the rate is 169% of the regular rate. The same goes if the date of the overtime falls on a special holiday.

Concept note-2: -For Daily-rate employees, the hourly rate is simply calculated as the daily rate divided by the standard # of working hours per day. So if the daily rate is P500. 00 and you have 8 working hours per day, the Hourly Rate is calculated as P500. 00 / 8 = P62.
