To change mixed number to improper, multiply the denominator to the whole number then add it to the numerator.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To convert a mixed number into an improper fraction, multiply the whole number by the denominator and add it to the numerator. This becomes the numerator of the improper fraction; the denominator of the new fraction is the same as the original denominator.
