What are the formula of Selling Price?

(A) ** Selling Price= Cost + Markup

(B) ** SP=C+Msp for markup based on selling price

(C) ** SP=C+Mc for markup based on cost price

(D) ** SP=C+M

(E) ** SP=C+OE+NP

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Divide the total cost by the number of units bought to obtain the cost price. Use the selling price formula to find out the final price i.e.: SP = CP + Profit Margin.

Concept note-2: -⇒ SP = (100+Profit% 100)×CP. 2. When loss% is given. ⇒SP = (100+Loss% 100)×CP. If discount is provided on the marked price then the formula of SP can be written as-

Concept note-3: -CP = ( SP * 100 ) / ( 100 – percentage loss ).

Concept note-4: -If a discount is expressed as a percentage (rate) in that case, the discount formula is: Discount = Listed Price × Discount Rate. Rate of Discount = Discount% = (Discount/Listed Price) ×100. Selling Price = Listed Price [(100−discount%)/100]
