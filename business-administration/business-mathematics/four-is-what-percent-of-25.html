FOUR IS WHAT PERCENT OF 25?

(A) ** 16%

(B) 16

(C) 4

(D) 4%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The calculator will give you your grade. First, you need to calculate your grade in percentages. Next, calculate the percentage of 4: divide 4 by 1% value (0.25), and you get 16.00%-it’s your percentage grade.
