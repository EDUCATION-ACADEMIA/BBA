Write 72% as a decimal

(A) 7, 2

(B) , 722

(C) 72

(D) ** .72

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To convert 72% to a decimal simply divide 72 by 100. 72 divided by 100 is 0.72!

Concept note-2: -Solution: 72/75 as a decimal is 0.96 As a result, you get 0.96 as your answer when you convert 72/75 to a decimal.

Concept note-3: -Solution: 72/100 as a percent is 72%

Concept note-4: -And finally, you get 0.73 as your answer when you convert 73/100 to a decimal.
