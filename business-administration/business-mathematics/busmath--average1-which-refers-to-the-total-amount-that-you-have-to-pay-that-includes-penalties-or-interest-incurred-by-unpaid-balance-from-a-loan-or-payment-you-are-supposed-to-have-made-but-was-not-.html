BusMath = Average1. Which refers to the total amount that you have to pay that includes penalties or interest incurred by unpaid balance from a loan or payment you are supposed to have made but was not able to do so on time?

(A) Gross Balance

(B) ** Current Increased Balance

(C) A. Commission

(D) Down payment

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The amount of interest earned on an investment or due on a loan is calculated using I = Prt. This formula can also be used to determine: the amount of principal (P) that needs to be invested in order to earn a certain amount of interest over a certain period of time.

Concept note-2: -The time period or term is the length of the financial transaction for which interest is charged or earned.

Concept note-3: -✓ CURRENT INCREASED BALANCE refers to the total amount you have. to pay that includes penalties or interest incurred by unpaid balance from a loan or payment you are supposed to have made but was not able to do so on time.

Concept note-4: -Just take the sale price, multiply it by the commission percentage, and divide it by 100. An example calculation: a blue widget is sold for $70 . The salesperson works on a commission-he/she gets 14% out of every transaction, which amounts to $9.80 .
