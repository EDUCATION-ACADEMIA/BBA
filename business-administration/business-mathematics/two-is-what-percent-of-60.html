TWO IS WHAT PERCENT OF 60%

(A) 33%

(B) 9%

(C) ** 3.3%

(D) 6.7%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solution: 60/80 as a percent is 75%

Concept note-2: -Convert 2/60 to Percentage by Converting to Decimal We can see that this gives us the exact same answer as the first method: 2/60 as a percentage is 3.33%.

Concept note-3: -To calculate a percentage of a percentage, convert both percentages to fractions of 100 or decimals and multiply them. For example, 50% of 40% is calculated; (50/100) x (40/100) = 0.50 x 0.40 = 0.20 = 20/100 = 20%.
