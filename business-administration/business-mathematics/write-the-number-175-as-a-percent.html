WRITE THE NUMBER 1.75 AS A PERCENT.

(A) 1.75%

(B) 17.5%

(C) ** 175%

(D) .175%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. The decimal 1.75 equals 175%To convert from decimal to percent, just multiply the decimal value by 100. In this example we have: 1.75 × 100 = 175% (answer).

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: Converting 1.75 into a percentage gives us 175%.

Concept note-3: -Solution: 175% as a fraction is 7/4.

Concept note-4: -To convert 1.7 to percent multiply 1.7 by 100. The result is 170 percent, or, using the percent sign, 170 %.
