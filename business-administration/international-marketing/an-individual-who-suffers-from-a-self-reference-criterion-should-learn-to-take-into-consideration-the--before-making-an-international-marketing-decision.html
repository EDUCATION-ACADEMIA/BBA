BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

INTERNATIONAL MARKETING

[SOURCES]
An individual who suffers from a self-reference criterion should learn to take into consideration the ____ before making an international marketing decision.

(A) total cost of goods sold

(B) home country values

(C) competitor stance

(D) ** culture of the other country

(E) global resources

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Self Reference Criterion is often defined as an unconscious reference to one’s own cultural values, experiences and knowledge as a basis for decisions. That is to say that one’s own culture or company knows the best how to do things (citeman.com).

Concept note-2: -In local markets on the basis of self-reference criterion; the marketer can predict the future responses of customers to a particular product but in the markets of some other country or culture the responses of customers will be based on their unique cultural environment.

Concept note-3: -Self-Reference Criterion (SRC) is defined as “the unconscious reference to one’s own cultural values” (Lee 1966: 106). Download conference paper PDF. There are many examples where markers have been able to successfully overcome the challenges encountered when tackling nondomestic markets.

Concept note-4: -A self-reference criterion is closely related to ethnocentrism.
