In which countries are new product innovations usually introduced first?

(A) less developed countries

(B) the developing countries

(C) ** very developed countries

(D) developed countries

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 50 Most Innovative Countries in 2022 Switzerland ranks at the top⁠ for the 12th year in a row-above the U.S., South Korea, and Israel.

Concept note-2: -First world countries included Australia, Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, New Zealand, Norway, the United Kingdom, and the United States, among others.

Concept note-3: -Switzerland is the most innovative economy in the world, according to a report published on 29 September 2022 by the World Intellectual Property Organization’s (WIPO’s) Global Innovation Index 2022 (GII 2022).

Concept note-4: -Norway has highest Human Development Index. It also is considered the most developed country.
