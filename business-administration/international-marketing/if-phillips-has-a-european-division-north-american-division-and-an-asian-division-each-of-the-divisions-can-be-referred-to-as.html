If Phillips has a European division, North American division and an Asian division, each of the divisions can be referred to as

(A) Strategic planning units

(B) ** Strategic business units

(C) Remote business units

(D) Business administration units

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A strategic business unit (SBU) is a subgroup of a single business or a collection of related businesses within the larger organization. Ansoff’s Opportunity Matrix: one of the most commonly used tools to determine a company’s strategic direction. It matches products with markets.

Concept note-2: -The widely accepted theory of corporate strategic planning is simple: using a time horizon of several years, top management reassesses its current strategy by looking for opportunities and threats in the environment and by analyzing the company’s resources to identify its strengths and weaknesses.
