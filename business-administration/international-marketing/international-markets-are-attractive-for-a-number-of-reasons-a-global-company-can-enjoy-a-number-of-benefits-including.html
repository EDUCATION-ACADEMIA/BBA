INTERNATIONAL MARKETING

[SOURCES]
International markets are attractive for a number of reasons. A global company can enjoy a number of benefits including:

(A) ** A larger target market, rapid growth in sales revenue, economies of scale, spreading risk, increased brand reputation, access to a global network

(B) Social and cultural differences, legal differences, economic differences, political, ethical and technological reasons.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Why is international marketing important? International marketing is important because it opens your business to larger, international audiences. On a brand level, international marketing is an opportunity for wider exposure, product awareness, and increased sales.

Concept note-2: -A global marketing strategy (GMS) is a strategy that encompasses countries from several different regions in the world and aims at coordinating a companyâ€™s marketing efforts in markets in these countries. A GMS does not necessarily cover all countries but it should apply across several regions.
