INTERNATIONAL MARKETING

[SOURCES]
Which of the following is NOT an orientation that describes international marketing approaches?

(A) ethnocentric

(B) polycentric

(C) regiocentric

(D) ** globalcentric

(E) geocentric

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Global Marketing Orientation is the marketing one for all. A company utilizes marketing mix strategies across the world in the same manner. Global marketing focuses on a single global market. There will be the same strategy, quality, features, and standards for maintaining brand value.

Concept note-2: -International marketing refers to any marketing activity that occurs across borders. Types of international marketing include export, licensing, franchising, joint venture, and foreign direct investment.
