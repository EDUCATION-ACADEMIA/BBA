INTERNATIONAL MARKETING

[SOURCES]
Organizations that conduct business activities in at least one other country that differs from the home country are:

(A) born-global corporations

(B) ** multinational corporations

(C) nationalized corporations

(D) domestic corporations

(E) rational corporations

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What Is a Multinational Corporation? A multinational corporation (MNC) is a company that has business operations in at least one country other than its home country. By some definitions, it also generates at least 25% of its revenue outside of its home country.

Concept note-2: -multinational corporation (MNC), also called transnational corporation, any corporation that is registered and operates in more than one country at a time. Generally the corporation has its headquarters in one country and operates wholly or partially owned subsidiaries in other countries.

Concept note-3: -The multinational corporation is a business organ-ization whose activities are located in more than two countries and is the organizational form that defines foreign direct investment.

Concept note-4: -There are four types of multinational companies: decentralised multinational corporations, global centralised corporations, international companies, and transnational enterprises.

Concept note-5: -Critical Business Skills For Success The primary distinction between an MNC and an international organization would be the fact that, for an MNC, a sizable percentage of the conducted business is handled outside the nation. In general, international business deals with both home and foreign nations.
