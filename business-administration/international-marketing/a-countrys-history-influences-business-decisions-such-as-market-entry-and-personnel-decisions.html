Concept note-1: -Answer and Explanation: ‘Exports tariffs are used by countries when they believe an export’s price is lower than it should be’ is a true statement. Export tariffs are used when the country wants to limit the sale of domestically produced goods in foreign countries.

Concept note-2: -The core principle of any market is free, voluntary exchange. That is just as true in the labor market as it is in the market for goods.

Concept note-3: -The U.S. – Mexico – Canada Agreement (USMCA) is a trade agreement between the named parties that entered into force on July 1, 2020.
