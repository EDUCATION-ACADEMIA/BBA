____ is the study of the earth’s surface, climate, continents, countries, peoples, industries, and resources.

(A) Geophysics

(B) ** Geography

(C) Cartography

(D) Demography

(E) Sociology

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Geography is the study of places and the relationships between people and their environments. Geographers explore both the physical properties of Earth’s surface and the human societies spread across it.

Concept note-2: -The study of the surface of Earth is called geography. One of the many aspects of the planet’s surface that geographers study is the variability of the environment from place to place.

Concept note-3: -Geography is the study of the earth’s physical and cultural features. These features include mountains, rivers, people, cities, and countries. Physical geography is the study of the earth’s land and features. People who work in this field are called physical geographers.

Concept note-4: -Geographers use maps and global positioning systems in their work. Geographers study the Earth and the distribution of its land, features, and inhabitants. They research the interactions between the physical aspects of a region and the human activities within it.
