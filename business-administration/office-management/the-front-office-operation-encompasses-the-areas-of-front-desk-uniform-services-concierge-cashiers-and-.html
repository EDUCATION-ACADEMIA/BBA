The front office operation encompasses the areas of front desk, uniform services, concierge, cashiers, and ____

(A) Night audio

(B) ** Night audit

(C) Night desk

(D) Night operation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The front office represents the customer-facing division of a firm. For example, customer service, sales, and industry experts who provide advisory services are considered part of a firm’s front office operations. The functions of the front office generally generate the majority of revenue for a firm.

Concept note-2: -Accounts − It mainly includes a front office cashier and a Night Auditor. The cashier is responsible for handling guest payments. He typically reports to the accounts manager rather than the front office manager.

Concept note-3: -Front office is the first department of hotel which comes in guest contact, at the time of guest arrival and the last department when they depart from the hotel. This department performs the various functions like reservation, reception, registration, room assignment and bills settlement of a resident guest.
