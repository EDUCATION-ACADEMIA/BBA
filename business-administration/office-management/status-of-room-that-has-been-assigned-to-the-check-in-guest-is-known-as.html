Status of room that has been assigned to the check in guest is known as

(A) Vacant

(B) Dirty

(C) ** Occupied

(D) Out of order

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is a room that has already been assigned to a guest who has already checked in the hotel. No one else can take that room before the current guest leaves.

Concept note-2: -Occupied: A guest is currently registered to the room. Complimentary: The room is occupied, but the guest is assessed no charge for its use. Stay Over: The guest is not expected to check out today and will remain at least one more night.

Concept note-3: -Occupied / Clean. Guest currently occupies the room, and room has been serviced. by housekeeping. Typically, there is no inspection for rooms. cleaned during the length of an individual guestâ€™s stay.

Concept note-4: -VC-Vacant and Clean-Room is Vacant and Cleaned by the housekeeper. VD-Vacant and Dirty-Room is Vacant and Dirty. OC-Occupied and Clean-Room is Occupied and Cleaned by the Housekeeping. OD-Occupied and Dirty-Room is Occupied and yet to be cleaned by the housekeeping.

Concept note-5: -VCI-Vacant, Cleaned & Inspected. HL-Heavy Luggage. LL-Light Luggage. NL-No Luggage. DL-Double Lock.
