The four stages of the hotel guest cycle include prearrival, arrival, ____, and departure.

(A) forecasting

(B) guest activities

(C) ** occupancy

(D) reservations

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is the Guest Cycle in a Hotel? The guest cycle is the complete experience that a guest goes through in a hotel: from making the booking to departure and the interaction with the hotel even after departure.

Concept note-2: -The arrival stage of the guest cycle includes registration and rooming functions. After the guest arrives, he or she establishes a business relationship with the hotel through the front office.
