[SOURCES]
An executive’s responsibilities related to a meeting include:

(A) determining who should attend

(B) setting the objectives

(C) determining the purpose of the meeting

(D) ** All of the answers are correct

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An executive meeting with five people who report to him or her is an example of a(n): Staff meeting.

Concept note-2: -Check-in meetings are one of the most common types of meetings. Organizations regularly hold these progress-check meetings for several reasons: Share project updates and progress. Ensure everyone is carrying out their roles and responsibilities.

Concept note-3: -Meeting minutes are the written record of everything that happened during a meeting. They highlight the key issues discussed, motions proposed or voted on, and activities to be undertaken.

Concept note-4: -An agenda lists the items of business to be taken up during a meeting or session. It may also be called a “calendar".
