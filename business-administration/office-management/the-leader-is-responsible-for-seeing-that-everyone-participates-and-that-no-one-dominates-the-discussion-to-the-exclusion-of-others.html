(A) ** true

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The meeting leader is responsible for seeing that everyone participates and that no one dominates the discussion. Employees should not make their online calendars accessible to members of their work group or to their supervisors. With technology use growing, traditional face-to-face meetings are very uncommon.

Concept note-2: -Web conferencing is any type of online meeting that involves two or more participants in different locations. These participants could be anywhere in the world. With a reliable internet connection and conferencing software, they can see, talk, and hear each other in real-time.

Concept note-3: -A teleconference is a live audio or audiovisual meeting with two or more participants. With the ability to teleconference, remote teams in an organization can collaborate and communicate, even when geographically dispersed. The process involves technology more sophisticated than a simple two-way phone connection.
