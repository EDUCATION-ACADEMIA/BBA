Long term planning covers a period of

(A) ** 5to 15 years

(B) Above 15 years

(C) Below 15 years

(D) below 5 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is long-term planning? Long-term planning involves goals that take a longer time to reach and require more steps; they usually take a minimum of a year or two to complete. They aim to permanently resolve issues and reach and maintain success over a continued period.

Concept note-2: -Lastly, long-term planning for EYFS can be defined as a span of up to a year in advance, or up until the end of the current academic/nursery year. This last category is concerned with the transition to primary school.

Concept note-3: -Planning periods. Planning periods are defined time intervals that are used for all planning data. Planning periods and notifications. The planning year and planning period that is defined in the Planning Period Config field in the General section populates the Planning Periods tab.
