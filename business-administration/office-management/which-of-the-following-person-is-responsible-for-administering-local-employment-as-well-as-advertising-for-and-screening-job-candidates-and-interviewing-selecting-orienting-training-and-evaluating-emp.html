Which of the following person is responsible for administering local employment as well as advertising for and screening job candidates and interviewing, selecting, orienting, training, and evaluating employees.

(A) ** Human Resources Manager

(B) Salas and Marketing Manager

(C) Assistant General Manager

(D) Parking Garage Manager

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hiring managers are responsible for hiring an employee, or employees, to fill open positions in an organization. Hiring managers are typically people from the hiring department and often serve as the new hireâ€™s future supervisor.

Concept note-2: -HR will be responsible for selecting agencies, negotiating rates, receiving candidate profiles and all communication with the agency/third party.

Concept note-3: -Recruitment is a key responsibility of the HR department. While HR works in many areas including employee engagement, employee development, statutory compliance, data management and many others, one of the key areas of focus for HR is to attract, select and onboard suitable candidates for the organization.

Concept note-4: -The human resource team is responsible for hiring, training, and managing compensation and employee relations. They are also involved in strategic decision-making.
