It is a rational approach firms use to achieve strategic competitiveness and earn above-average returns

(A) ** Strategic Management

(B) Total Quality Management

(C) Human Resource Management

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -RATIONAL MODEL: It is a logical step-by-step approach. It requires organisation to analyse its existing circumstances. It requires the organisation to generate possible strategies.

Concept note-2: -Strategic competitiveness is achieved when the firm successfully formulates and implements a value-creating strategy. Above-average returns are returns in excess of what investors expect to earn from other investments with similar risk levels.

Concept note-3: -The strategic management process is the full set of commitments, decisions, and actions required for a firm to achieve strategic competitiveness and earn above-average returns.

Concept note-4: -Strategic competitiveness is accomplished when a firm successfully integrates a value-creating strategy. The key to having a complete value-creating strategy is to adopt a holistic approach that includes business strategy, financial strategy, technology strategy, marketing strategy and investor strategy.
