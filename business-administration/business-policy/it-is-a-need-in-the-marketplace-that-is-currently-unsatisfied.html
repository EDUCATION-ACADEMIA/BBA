BUSINESS POLICY

[SOURCES]
It is a need in the marketplace that is currently unsatisfied

(A) Objectives

(B) Business Strategies

(C) Differentiation

(D) ** Niche

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A propitious niche is a need in the marketplace that is currently unsatisfied. The first firm through a strategic window can occupy a propitious niche and discourage competition (if the firm has the required internal strengths).

Concept note-2: -When you focus on costs, then you try to become a low-cost provider. When you focus on differentiation, then you look to compete by adding extra value to your customers that they won’t find in your competitors. In other words, you are focusing on areas other than cost to set yourself apart from the competition.

Concept note-3: -In summary, the strategies were as follows: Cost Leadership-Minimizing the costs incurred in providing value (product or service) to a customer or client. Differentiation-This means making ones product unique or special, compared to other competitors or substitute products in the market.

Concept note-4: -Retrenchment. This strategy can be used by an organization when its products are currently in the declining stage of the product’s life cycle.
