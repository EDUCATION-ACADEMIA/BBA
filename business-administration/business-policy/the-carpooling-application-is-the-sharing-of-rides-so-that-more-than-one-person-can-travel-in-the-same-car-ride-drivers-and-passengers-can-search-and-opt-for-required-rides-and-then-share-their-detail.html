The Carpooling application is the sharing of rides so that more than one person can travel in the same car ride. Drivers and passengers can search and opt for required rides and then share their details. In this application, once the drivers or passengers have agreed upon a ride, they can also choose to cancel the ride at any point of time. In the above scenario, which will be the best possible option to cancel the ride?

(A) Cancellation stage will be the part of the primary or happy path of the case life cycle

(B) Add cancellation stage as an alternate stage

(C) ** Add cancellation as an optional process

(D) Configure the cancellation stage as a resolution stage

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Instead of paying someone a fixed rate to pick and send you as you wish, you will be hitching a ride from someone who’s already traveling on the same route. Instead, the app will suggest a certain amount you can pay the driver, taking into account the distance travelled, petrol, toll and car depreciation costs.

Concept note-2: -Carpooling (also car-sharing, ride-sharing and lift-sharing) is the sharing of car journeys so that more than one person travels in a car, and prevents the need for others to have to drive to a location themselves.

Concept note-3: -Carpooling is the s haring of car rides so that more than one person travel in the car, mainly to reduce the cost of travel, fuel consumption and environmental pollution. Q.
