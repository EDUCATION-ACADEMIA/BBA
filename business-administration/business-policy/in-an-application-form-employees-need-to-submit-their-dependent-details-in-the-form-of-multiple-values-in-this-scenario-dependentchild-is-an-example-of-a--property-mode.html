In an application form, employees need to submit their dependent details in the form of multiple values. In this scenario, Dependent(Child) is an example of a ____ property mode.

(A) ** page group

(B) page list

(C) value group

(D) value list

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is something that depends on other factors. For example, a test score could be a dependent variable because it could change depending on several factors such as how much you studied, how much sleep you got the night before you took the test, or even how hungry you were when you took it.

Concept note-2: -We’ll begin by exploring the components of a bivariate regression model, which estimates the relationship between an independent and dependent variable.

Concept note-3: -The type of soda – diet or regular – is the independent variable. The level of blood sugar that you measure is the dependent variable – it changes depending on the type of soda.

Concept note-4: -Overview. By far the most common approach to including multiple independent variables in an experiment is the factorial design. In a factorial design, each level of one independent variable (which can also be called a factor) is combined with each level of the others to produce all possible combinations.
