Which of these is NOT a monetary policy tool?

(A) a. Discount Rate

(B) b. Open Market Operation

(C) ** c. Balance Accounts

(D) d. Reserve Requirements

(E) Unattempted

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Out of the given options, deficit financing is not a monetary tool.

Concept note-2: -The Fed has traditionally used three tools to conduct monetary policy: reserve requirements, the discount rate, and open market operations. In 2008, the Fed added paying interest on reserve balances held at Reserve Banks to its monetary policy toolkit.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is option 2, i.e., MSP. MSP is NOT an instrument of RBI’s Monetary Policy.
