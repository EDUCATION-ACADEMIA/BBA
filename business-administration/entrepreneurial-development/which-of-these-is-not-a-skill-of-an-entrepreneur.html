Which of these is NOT a skill of an entrepreneur?

(A) Creativity

(B) Interpersonal Skills

(C) Organisation

(D) ** Sleeping

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -no ability to bear risk Answer: Being an entrepreneur comes with so many challenging tasks and risks so if one is not prepared with facing risks then it is not helpful for the company in long term. So, if one has “no ability to bear risk” then it is not an entrepreneurial skill.

Concept note-2: -A great entrepreneur must be able to effectively communicate, sell, focus, learn, and strategize. An ability to continuously learn is not just a key entrepreneurial skill, but also a very valuable life skill. Growing a business requires a sound strategy based on inherent business sense and skills.
