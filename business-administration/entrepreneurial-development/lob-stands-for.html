EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Line of Balance (LOB) is a management control process for collecting, measuring, and presenting facts relating to time (see Schedule Control), cost, and accomplishment – all measured against a specific plan.

Concept note-2: -Line of balance (LOB) is a management control process used in construction where the project contains blocks of repetitive work activities, such as roads, pipelines, tunnels, railways and high-rise buildings.

Concept note-3: -Line of business (LOB) is a general term that describes the related product or services a business or manufacturer offers. A company that manufactures solid-state disk drives, for example, might claim its LOB is data storage.

Concept note-4: -The lob is the most effective way to take the net position, which is the main objective during the point. Hitting the ball over your opponents to force them to the back of the court gives you time to move forward and changes a defensive position to an offensive.
