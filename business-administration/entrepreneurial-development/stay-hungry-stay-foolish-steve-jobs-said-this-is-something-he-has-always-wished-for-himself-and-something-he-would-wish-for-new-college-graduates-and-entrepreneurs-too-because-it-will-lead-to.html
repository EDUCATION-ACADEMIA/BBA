“Stay Hungry. Stay Foolish.” Steve Jobs said this is something he has always wished for himself, and something he would wish for new college graduates and entrepreneurs too because it will lead to

(A) 1.Entrepreneurs go without food and keep making rash decisions.

(B) 2. Will lead to endless goal searching for entrepreneurs.

(C) ** 3. Will support to aspire for growth so entrepreneurs constantly keep improving themselves.

(D) 4. Remaining foolish refers to them daring to think just different all the time.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He wished that for the graduating students, he added, just as he’d always wished it for himself: Stay hungry. Stay foolish. That’s advice we can all follow.

Concept note-2: -Don’t be trapped by dogma-which is living with the results of other people’s thinking. Don’t let the noise of others’ opinions drown out your own inner voice. And most important, have the courage to follow your heart and intuition.

Concept note-3: -There is always more to do/gain (“stay hungry” for knowledge) and more to learn (“stay foolish”, meaning never consider oneself an expert but a fool in order to learn more).

Concept note-4: -Quote by Steve Jobs: “Stay hungry. Stay foolish.
