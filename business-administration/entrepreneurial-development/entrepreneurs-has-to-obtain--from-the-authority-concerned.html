(A) ** Municipal license

(B) Project report

(C) Provisional registration certificate

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Business licenses are permits issued by government agencies that allow individuals or companies to conduct business within the governmentâ€™s geographical jurisdiction. It is the authorization to start a business issued by the local government.

Concept note-2: -Shop and Establishment Act license or registration is issued by the State Governments and varies from States. Hence, based on the State in which the business is situated, the concerned State Government authority must be approached for obtaining Shop and Establishment Act License.

Concept note-3: -A business within a state, corporation, or municipality will require trade license registration from the concerned central or state government to commence with any business in a particular area or location.
