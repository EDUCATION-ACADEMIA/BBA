The most common way to acquire property is by

(A) contract

(B) gift

(C) inheritence

(D) ** all of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Acquisition by purchase is the most common way we acquire personal property, but there are at least five other ways to legally acquire personal property: (1) possession, (2) finding lost or misplaced property, (3) gift, (4) accession, and (5) confusion.

Concept note-2: -The most common form of real property ownership is fee simple or fee simple absolute. Fee simple means the right to exclusivepossession of a particular piece of land for an indefinite time, as well as the right to dispose of the land as the owner pleases.
