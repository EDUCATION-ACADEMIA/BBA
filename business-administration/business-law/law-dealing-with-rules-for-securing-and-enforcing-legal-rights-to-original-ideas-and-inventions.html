Concept note-1: -IPR Law: means intellectual property law and this intellectual property law deals with the rules for securing and enforcing legal rights to inventions, designs, and artistic works.

Concept note-2: -Intellectual property rights (IPR) refers to the legal rights given to the inventor or creator to protect his invention or creation for a certain period of time. [1] These legal rights confer an exclusive right to the inventor/creator or his assignee to fully utilize his invention/creation for a given period of time.
