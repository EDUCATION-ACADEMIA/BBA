Your co-worker is having trouble finishing a report. He’ll pay you double your normal hourly rate to do it and not tell anyone. What do you do?

(A) Agree to it since you have time. But decline the money because it’s wrong.

(B) Agree to it. You could use the money!

(C) ** Decline, explaining that you have your own report to write.

(D) Decline, but ask for the money or you’ll tell his boss about the situation.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -your co-worker is having trouble finishing a report. he’ll pay you double your normal hourly rate to do it and not tell anyone. what do you do? decline, explaining that you have your own report to write.

Concept note-2: -An unethical decision that is not illegal would be lying to your friends. A legal decision can also be an unethical one because you can do lots of things, like bribe or lie, however those things are not illegal unless performed on an officer or person of law.

Concept note-3: -Misrepresentation of finances: In maintaining and reporting its financial status, every corporation must conform to generally accepted accounting principles.

Concept note-4: -A safe and inclusive work environment. Trust in business relationships. Security and integrity of our Group and third-party assets. Corporate social responsibility.
