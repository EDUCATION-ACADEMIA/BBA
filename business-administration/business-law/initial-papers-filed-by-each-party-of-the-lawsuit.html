EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pleadings: The court documents filed with the court by the parties in a civil or criminal case.

Concept note-2: -The institution of suits is governed under Section 26 of the CPC, read along with Order IV, CPC. According to Section 26 of the CPC: Every lawsuit must be initiated by the filing of a plaint or in any other manner as prescribed by law. An affidavit must be used to substantiate the facts in every plaint.

Concept note-3: -Four fundamental rules of pleading are; (1) Pleadings should state facts and not law; (2) The facts stated in pleadings should be material facts; (3) Pleadings should not state the evidence; and (4) The facts in pleadings should be stated in a concise form.
