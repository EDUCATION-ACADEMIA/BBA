An clickwrap contract is example of?

(A) Traditional contract

(B) ** E-contract

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Clickwrap agreements are a form of agreement used for software licensing, websites, and other electronic media. When the user logs in to a website the terms and conditions or the privacy policies of the website are to be accepted by the user as legal consent.

Concept note-2: -Examples of E-Contracts A contract that you write on your computer and email to a business associate, and that the business associate emails back with an electronic signature indicating acceptance. A contract using an encrypted form and digital signatures, such as something completed with DocuSign or Adobe Sign.

Concept note-3: -Some examples of transactions that would require the userâ€™s assent to a clickwrap agreement are: Downloading or installing software. Purchasing an airline ticket or online music. Registering an account on a social media website.

Concept note-4: -Three common kinds of electronic contract are browse wrap, shrink wrap and click wrap contracts.
