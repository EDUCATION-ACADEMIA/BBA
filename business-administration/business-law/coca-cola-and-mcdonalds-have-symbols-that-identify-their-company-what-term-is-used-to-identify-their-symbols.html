Coca-Cola and McDonald’s have symbols that identify their company. What term is used to identify their symbols?

(A) Copyright

(B) Patent

(C) Service mark

(D) ** Trademark

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There is no specific typographic symbol for a patent approved by worldwide countries. In the USA, when you have applied for a patent, you can use the phrase “Patent Pending” or “Pat.

Concept note-2: -The Coca-Cola Corp owns the trademark to the name Coca-Cola, as well as the trademark on the bottle shape, and the graphic representation of their name. These are all things that help distinguish them from other cola brands and define their individual product. Coca-Cola also owns the patent on their formula.
