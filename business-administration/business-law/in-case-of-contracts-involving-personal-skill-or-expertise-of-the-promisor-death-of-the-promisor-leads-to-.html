In case of contracts involving personal skill or expertise of the Promisor, death of the Promisor leads to ____

(A) ** Discharge of a Contract

(B) Breach of a Contract

(C) Rescission of a Contract

(D) Waiver of a Contract

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If the promisor dies before performing the promise, then the legal representatives become responsible for the same. If the promise involves the utilization of personal skills or expertise, then the consideration ceases with the death of the promisor.

Concept note-2: -For example 1: A, a singer made a contract with B to sing at the concert organised by B. but, while going for the concert, A met with an accident and unfortunately he died. In this situation, the contract gets discharged due to the death of the promisor, because it involves the personal skills of the promisor.

Concept note-3: -Death-In case of contracts, which involve personal skill or ability, the contract is discharged or terminated on the death of the promisor. However, in other contracts, the rights and liabilities of a deceased person shall pass on to the legal representatives of the deceased person.

Concept note-4: -Section 6 in The Indian Contract Act, 1872.

Concept note-5: -Discharge of contract by breach of contract: Breach of contract is concerned with the termination of the original contract due to the failure of performing obligations by either or all of the parties, which discourages each of the other parties. It relates to void or terminating the original contract completely.
