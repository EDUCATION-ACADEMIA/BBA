At what day and time do you become a majority?

(A) 12:00am on the day before your 18th birthday

(B) 12:00am on the day of your 18th birthday

(C) ** 12:01am on the day before your 18th birthday

(D) 12:01am on the day of your 18th birthday

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What time does your age change on your birthday? You are 18 ( for example) as soon as the date is the same day as the date of your birthday. This happens at midnight. At 11:59:59, twelve fifty nine and fifty nine seconds, it’s still the previous day’s date.

Concept note-2: -Age means how many years old you are. You reach a particular age on the day before your birthday. For example, if your sixty-second birthday is on July 1, 1979, you became age 62 on June 30, 1979.

Concept note-3: -No. Your “age” is the time that has elapsed since you were born, not since you were conceived. Your birthday is the day you were born. When a baby is born exactly at midnight, which day is the baby’s birthday?

Concept note-4: -Does your birthday start at midnight or the time you were born? It’s your birth day, not your birth hour or your birth minute. It starts at midnight, both legally and culturally (at least in the United States).
