A valid offer must be communicated to the offeree and

(A) ** the terms must match

(B) must be in writing

(C) must be on legal paper

(D) must exchange money

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Further, when the offeree accepts the proposal, he must have known that an offer was made. He cannot communicate acceptance without knowledge of the offer. So when A offers to supply B with goods, and B is agreeable to all the terms.

Concept note-2: -OFFER MUST BE COMMUNICATED TO THE OFFEREE A person cannot accept an offer without knowing about it. general public cannot be accepted by someone who has never seen or heard of the offer.

Concept note-3: -The basic elements required for the agreement to be a legally enforceable contract are: mutual assent, expressed by a valid offer and acceptance; adequate consideration; capacity; and legality.

Concept note-4: -When the terms of acceptance must exactly match the terms contained in the offer, it is called. Mirror Image Rule.
