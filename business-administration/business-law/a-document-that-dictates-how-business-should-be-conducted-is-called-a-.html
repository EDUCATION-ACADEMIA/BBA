A document that dictates how business should be conducted is called a ____

(A) ** code of ethics

(B) code of rights

(C) code of profitability

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Code of Ethics. a document that dictates how business should be conducted. Code of Conduct. handbook that outlines expectations of employee behavior. Confidentiality.

Concept note-2: -A partnership agreement is the legal document that dictates the way a business is run and details the relationship between each partner.

Concept note-3: -A contract is a legally enforceable agreement between two or more persons. The agreement may be to do, or not do, something. It may be oral or written, or may be formed completely by conduct.

Concept note-4: -Business ethics is an evolving topic. Generally, there are about 12 ethical principles: honesty, fairness, leadership, integrity, compassion, respect, responsibility, loyalty, law-abiding, transparency, and environmental concerns.

Concept note-5: -An employee handbook (also known as an employee manual or staff handbook) is a document outlining a businessâ€™s rules, policies, and expectations for their employees. It also lists what employees can expect from the employer.
