A ____ must name a board of directors that will make the major policy and financial decisions for the business.

(A) limited liability company

(B) proprietorship

(C) ** corporation

(D) limited liability partnership

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Disadvantages of sole trading include that: you have unlimited liability for debts as there’s no legal distinction between private and business assets. your capacity to raise capital is limited. all the responsibility for making day-to-day business decisions is yours.

Concept note-2: -A corporation is able to buy and sell property, sue and be sued, and protect its owners from liability. The owners, called shareholders, are individuals who own shares of the corporation’s stock. The stock can be owned by one individual or many individuals.

Concept note-3: -A corporation is a legal entity created by individuals, stockholders, or shareholders, with the purpose of operating for profit. Corporations are allowed to enter into contracts, sue and be sued, own assets, remit federal and state taxes, and borrow money from financial institutions.

Concept note-4: -A corporation, sometimes called a C corp, is a legal entity that’s separate from its owners. Corporations can make a profit, be taxed, and can be held legally liable. Corporations offer the strongest protection to its owners from personal liability, but the cost to form a corporation is higher than other structures.
