____ means the voluntary transfer of possession from one person to another

(A) ** Delivery

(B) Deliverable state

(C) Sale

(D) Delivery order

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -III. Delivery. Delivery of goods signifies the voluntary transfer of possession from one individual to another.

Concept note-2: -Actual Delivery: If the goods are physically given into the possession of the buyer, the delivery is an actual delivery. Constructive delivery: The transfer of goods can be done even when the transfer is effected without a change in the possession or custody of the goods.

Concept note-3: -Rule: The ownership/property in goods is said to be transferred from the seller to the buyer once the goods are in deliverable state and the notice for the same is given by seller to the buyer. Till the time itâ€™s in non-deliverable state, then the ownership lies with the seller only.

Concept note-4: -Constructive delivery (called also symbolic delivery) is a substitute (or is an equivalent) of real delivery. It is a term used when the item was not supplied itself but the symbol or sign of it is delivered. Real (actual, physical, manual) delivery is an opposed term to constructive delivery.
