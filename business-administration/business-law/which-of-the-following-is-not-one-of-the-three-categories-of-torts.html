Which of the following is not one of the three categories of torts?

(A) intentional torts

(B) ** unintentional torts

(C) negligence

(D) strict liability

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tort lawsuits are the biggest category of civil litigation and can encompass a wide range of personal injury cases. However, there are 3 main types: intentional torts, negligence, and strict liability.

Concept note-2: -The most common intentional torts for which people contact an attorney are battery, assault, and trespass to property. If you have been the victim of these common torts, please use this form to contact an intentional tort attorney for a free case evaluation.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: The types of torts are negligence torts, Intentional torts, and strict or absolute law. It does not include tort reform, therefore, it is not the type of tort.

Concept note-4: -Torts include both intentional and unintentional acts. Unintentional torts are those injuries caused by an accident or an action that was not intended to cause harm. The most common type of unintentional tort is called negligence. Negligence is the careless conduct that causes foreseeable harm to another person.
