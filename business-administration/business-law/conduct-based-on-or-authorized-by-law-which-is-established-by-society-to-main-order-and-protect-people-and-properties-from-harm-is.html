Conduct based on or authorized by law, which is established by society to main order and protect people and properties from harm is

(A) moral behavior

(B) ** legal behavior

(C) false behavior

(D) mannerly behavior

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Difference Between Ethics and Compliance But ECI also points out that ethics encompasses other nuances, such as this definition from Stephen D. Potts, author The Ethics of Non-profit Management: “A set of standards of conduct that guide decisions and actions based on duties derived from core values.”

Concept note-2: -Which of the following terms is defined as “conduct based on or authorized by law which is established by society to maintain order and protect people and property from harm?” Legal behavior.

Concept note-3: -Ethics is based on well-founded standards of right and wrong that prescribe what humans ought to do, usually in terms of rights, obligations, benefits to society, fairness, or specific virtues.

Concept note-4: -What is Morality? Morality directs people to behave in certain ways and avoid behaving in other ways. It evaluates behavior as right or wrong and may involve measuring the conformity of a person’s actions to a code of conduct or set of principles.
