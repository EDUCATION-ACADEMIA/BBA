When the date is not mentioned on Promissory note, it is called as ____

(A) ** Payable on demand

(B) Payable after date

(C) Unexpired Promissory Note

(D) Special Promissory Note

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A promissory note payable on demand is a way to get repaid when you loan money to someone. It is a document that states the terms of the loan and includes the “payable on demand” notation on it. This means that you can demand full payment of the loan at any time you deem necessary.

Concept note-2: -Date of Repayment-The note must clearly state the date on which the repayment for the loaned amount must be paid.

Concept note-3: -Instruments payable on demand.-A promissory note or bill of exchange, in which no time for payment is specified, and a cheque, are payable on demand.
