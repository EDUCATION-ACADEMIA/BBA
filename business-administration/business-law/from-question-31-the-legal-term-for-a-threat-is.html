EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Form questions are perfect for gathering information about your participants. Form questions can also be used for questions where you’d like to get a list of favorites, likes and dislikes and so forth. Just type your question, then enter the name of each form field (e.g., Name, Rank and Serial Number).

Concept note-2: -’Where are you from’ is a very common question that you often get asked when you meet someone new so it’s important that you know how to answer it correctly. ‘Where are you from’ is normally asking about your country or your city.

Concept note-3: -Use does at the start of questions when the subject is he, she or it. Does he like Indian food? Does she live near you? Does it rain much in winter?

Concept note-4: -Grammar explanation To make questions, we often put the verb before the subject. This is called inversion. I am late. Am I late?
