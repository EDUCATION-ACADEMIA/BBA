To overturn the decision means to ____ the decision.

(A) remand

(B) affrim

(C) ** reverse

(D) amend

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In legal terms, a reversal is when a high court decides the decision of a lower court is incorrect and should be overturned. In a reversal, the Supreme Court overturned the guilty verdict of the lower court, and the prisoner was set free.

Concept note-2: -: to disagree with a decision made earlier by a lower court. The appeals court overturned the decision made by the trial court.

Concept note-3: -reversal. n. the decision of a court of appeal ruling that the judgment of a lower court was incorrect and is therefore reversed. The result is that the lower court which tried the case is instructed to dismiss the original action, retry the case or change its judgment.

Concept note-4: -over·turn. ˌō-vər-ˈtərn. : to turn over or upside down. waves overturned the boat. : invalidate, destroy.

Concept note-5: -verb (used with object) to cause (something) to turn onto its side, face, or back; upset: to overturn a vase. to destroy the power or validity of; overthrow; defeat: The conspiracy finally overturned the regime.
