Brandon and Marcia sign a legal document pledging the house to “Busy Banker” as security for the home loan?

(A) Easement Contract

(B) ** Mortgage

(C) Quitclaim Deed

(D) Warranty Deed

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The person to whom they are delivered is called the “bailee”. Example: Where ‘X’ delivers his car for repair to ‘Y’, ‘X’ is the bailor and ‘Y’ is the bailee. Example: X delivers a piece of cloth to Y, a tailor, to be stitched into a suit. It is contract for bailment.

Concept note-2: -There are three types of bailments-those that benefit both parties, those that benefit only the bailor, and those that only benefit the bailee.

Concept note-3: -The distinction between Bailment and agency is that the term bailment is derived from the French word ‘Bailor’, which means ‘to deliver’ whereas when a person appoints another to act on his behalf with a third party, it is called ‘Agency’.

Concept note-4: –A ‘bailment’ is the delivery of goods by one person to another for some purpose, upon a contract that they shall, when the purpose is accomplished, be returned or otherwise disposed of according to the directions of the person delivering them.
