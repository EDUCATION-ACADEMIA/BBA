Most American law courts can use either damages or an injunction or both as remedies in civil cases.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under tort law, the measure of compensation is usually the money value of any losses or injuries sustained as a “natural and proximate” result of the wrongful act (e.g., negligently causing an automobile accident).

Concept note-2: -Several exceptions to the requirement for informed consent include (1) the patient is incapacitated, (2) life-threatening emergencies with inadequate time to obtain consent, and (3) voluntary waived consent.

Concept note-3: -A contract is a voluntary arrangement between two or more parties that is enforceable at law. It is a legally-binding agreement that obligates two or more parties to complete certain tasks. It creates rights and obligations to parties of the contract.

Concept note-4: -Compensatory damages are intended to compensate the plaintiff of a lawsuit with enough money to cover the loss caused by the defendant.
