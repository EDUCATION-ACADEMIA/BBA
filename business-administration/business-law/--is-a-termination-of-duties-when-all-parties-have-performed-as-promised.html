? ____ is a termination of duties when all parties have performed as promised.

(A) tender

(B) ** discharge

(C) performance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Discharge of contract means terminating the contractual relationship between the two or more parties who entered into the contract previously. When the rights, obligations and duties of the parties come to an end it is known as the discharge of contract.

Concept note-2: -The discharge of the contract takes place when the obligations of the contract between the parties of the contract come to an end. This also ends the legal validity of the contract. The discharge of the contract is also referred to as the termination of the contract.

Concept note-3: -When the parties to a contract fulfil the obligations arising under the contract within the time and manner prescribed, then the contract is discharged by performance.

Concept note-4: -Discharge of performance occurs when the parties to the contract fulfill their obligations set out under the contract within the specified time and in the manner prescribed. In such a case, parties are discharged and contracts come to an end. But if only one of the party performs, he alone is discharged.
