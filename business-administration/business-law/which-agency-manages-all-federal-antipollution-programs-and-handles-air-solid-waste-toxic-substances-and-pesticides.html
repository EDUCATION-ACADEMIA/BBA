Which agency manages all federal antipollution programs and handles air, solid waste, toxic substances, and pesticides?

(A) ** Environmental Protection Agency

(B) Federal Energy Regulatory Commission

(C) Nuclear Regulatory Commission

(D) Department of Energy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The EPA regulates the manufacturing, processing, distribution, and use of chemicals and other pollutants. Also, the EPA is charged with determining safe tolerance levels for chemicals and other pollutants in food, animal feed, and water. The EPA enforces its findings through fines, sanctions, and other procedures.

Concept note-2: -U.S. Environmental Protection Agency. US EPA. Official websites use .gov. A .gov website belongs to an official government organization in the United States.

Concept note-3: -The Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) is responsible for the formulation and implementation of policies, guidelines, and rules related to environmental management, as well as the management and conservation of the countryâ€™s natural resources.

Concept note-4: -The EPA manages the transport and disposal of hazardous waste, and works with industry to find sustainable solutions to minimise the amount of waste going to landfill.
