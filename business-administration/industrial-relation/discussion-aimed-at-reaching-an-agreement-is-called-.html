Discussion aimed at reaching an agreement is called ____

(A) Cheese

(B) Strikes

(C) ** Negotiation

(D) Work-to-rule

(E) Deadlock

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The term negotiation refers to a strategic discussion that resolves an issue in a way that both parties find acceptable. In a negotiation, each party tries to persuade the other to agree with their point of view.

Concept note-2: -It is dialogue. It is a type of discussion between parties aimed at reaching an agreement that is acceptable to everyone.

Concept note-3: -Negotiation is a dialogue between two or more people or parties to reach the desired outcome regarding one or more issues of conflict.

Concept note-4: -The process of reaching an agreement about the meaning of a set of terms is known as Meaning Negotiation.

Concept note-5: -The discussion stage of the negotiation is crucial to getting a better understanding of what the other party is looking for. You want to ensure that you are listening, questioning, and clarifying what the other party has brought forth. It is equally important to communicate what you are looking for.
