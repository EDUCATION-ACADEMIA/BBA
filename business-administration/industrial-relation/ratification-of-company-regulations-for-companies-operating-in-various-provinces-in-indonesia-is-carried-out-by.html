Concept note-1: -The legal system in Indonesia is based on civil law and in civil law systems, codes and statutes are designed to cover all cases. There are various types of business entities in Indonesia, with a limited liability company, as known as a Perseroan Terbatas (PT).

Concept note-2: -Indonesian employment law is generally considered to be pro-employee. It is difficult to terminate an employment contract and the law defines employment broadly, extending labour protection to a large number of workers.

Concept note-3: -Every manpower shall have equal opportunity to obtain a job without discrimination. Every worker/labor shall entitle to obtain equal treatment without discrimination from entrepreneur. (1) In the framework of manpower development, the government shall stipulate policies and formulate manpower planning.

Concept note-4: -What is this minimum age? The Indonesian government has declared that the minimum age of employment is 15 years for all child labour.
